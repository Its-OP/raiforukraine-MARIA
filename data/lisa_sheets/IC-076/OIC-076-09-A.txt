Most common psychiatric comorbidities: [[Anxiety SD-116|anxiety disorders]] and depressive disorders.

Association of alcohol misuse with schizophrenia (20-30%) and bipolar disorder (up to 40%).

[Suicidal risk 2C-066-IU-A01|Risk]] of [[Suicidal ideation or behaviour/self-inflicted injury SD-125|suicide]] very strongly increased, (acute alcoholisations++)


Primary psychiatric disorders: preceding misuse

or secondary: occur as a result of acute or chronic consumption

Diagnosis based on anamnesis and evolution after withdrawal


Frequent association with other product addictions (tobacco +++) and behavioural addictions (pathological gambling +)
