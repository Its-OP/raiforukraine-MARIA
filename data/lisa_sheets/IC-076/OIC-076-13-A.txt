Objective: to prevent or delay the first use of psychoactive substances, in particular by acting on early use.

- Takes into account the person rather than the substance

- Frees itself from any judgmental or stigmatising attitude.

The main tools are information and education for future users, to reduce risk factors and promote healthy behaviour. <br />
