Measles (linked to a paramyxovirus) should be routinely suspected in adults and children.

for any febrile morbilliform exanthema, and the vaccination context should be clarified and a search made for a possible

infection, as well as the cardinal clinical signs of the disease (oculonasal catarrh, Köplick's sign, etc.),

conjunctivitis, respiratory signs)
