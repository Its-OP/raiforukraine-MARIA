Erythroderma is a clinical situation at risk of complications and a genuine medical emergency.

requiring immediate treatment in a specialised facility to investigate the underlying aetiology,

implement rapid symptomatic measures (emollients, protection against heat loss)

and to detect/treat any extracutaneous complications (hydro-electrolytic disorders, hypoprotidemia),

metabolic, cardiovascular/hemodynamic, pulmonary decompensation, infectious complications and

decubitus in particular)
