Ampicillin rash presents as a morbilliform maculopapular exanthema +/- oral enanthema classically appearing on the 9th<sup>day</sup> after ampicillin prescribed for angina in the context of EBV infection (infectious mononucleosis: fever/angina/splenomegaly/asthenia).
