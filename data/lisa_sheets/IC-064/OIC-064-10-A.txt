* early onset (before the age of 25)
* post-partum episode
* sudden onset
* at least 3 episodes of major depression
* family history of mood disorder
* inadequate response to antidepressants: lack of response, worsening, agitation, mood swings
* melancholic intensity
* seasonal depression
* mixed characteristics
