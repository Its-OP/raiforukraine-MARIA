==Main psychiatric differential diagnoses:==

*Recurrent depressive disorder
*Personality Disorder
*Attention deficit disorder with or without hyperactivity
*Schizophrenia
*Anxiety Disorder
*Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder
*Persistent Delusional Disorder

==Main non-psychiatric differential diagnoses:==

===- Psychoactive substances===

*cannabis
*psychostimulants: cocaine, amphetamines, ecstasy

===- Neurological===

*tumour
*vascular accident
*inflammatory, autoimmune or infectious disease
*neurodegenerative disease
*focal epilepsy

===- Endocrine===

*thyroid disorders
*Cushing's disease

===- Metabolic===

*Hypoglycemia SD-209|hypoglycemia
*dyscalcemia
*Wilson's disease

===- Iatrogenic, adverse drug reaction===

*corticosteroid
*antidepressant
*L-Dopa
*isoniazide
*interferon-alpha...
