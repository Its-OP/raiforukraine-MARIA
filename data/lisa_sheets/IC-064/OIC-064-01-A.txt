According to the DSM-5, a time-limited period during which mood is abnormally and persistently elevated, expansive, or irritable, with increased goal-directed activity or energy, persisting most of the time, almost every day, for at least 7 days (or any length of time if hospitalised), with marked impairment of occupational or social functioning or the need for hospitalisation or psychotic features ([[Delusional ideas SD-124|delusional ideas]], [[Hallucinations SD-122|hallucinations]]).
