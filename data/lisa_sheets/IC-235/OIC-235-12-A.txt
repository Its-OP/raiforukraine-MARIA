* Rest, no sport (minimum duration until resolution of symptoms after normalisation of CRP, ECG and ETT: in practice 3 months)'''
* Double anti-inflammatory treatment:''''
** '''AINS :''''
*** especially '''aspirin''' 1000 mg * 3 per day
*** or ibuprofen 800 mg *3 per day for 2 weeks
*** then decrease progressively according to symptoms and CRP
** Systematically combined with '''COLCHICIN:''''
*** 0.5 mg Ã— 2 if weight > 70kg
*** 0.5 mg daily if weight <70kg
*** for 3 months,
*** calms pain and above all reduces the risk of recurrence
*** potential drug interactions (via cytochrome P450)
*** strict biological monitoring (transaminases, creatinine, CPK, CBC, platelets)
*** contraindicated in cases of severe renal impairment

* Association with gastric protection
* No corticosteroids as first-line treatment
* Most often ''favourable'' outcome
