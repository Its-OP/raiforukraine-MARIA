* '''Myocarditis = myo-pericarditis:''''
** elevation of troponin
** diagnosis on cardiac MRI (subepicardial enhancement),
** risk of heart failure and ventricular rhythm disorders

* '''Tamponnade'''
* Recurrence (frequent in benign acute viral pericarditis)
* Pericardial constriction = chronic calcifying pericarditis, progressive evolution towards right heart failure, diagnosed on right catheterisation (dip plateau, equalisation of diastolic pressures).
