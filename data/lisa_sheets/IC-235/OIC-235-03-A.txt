These are signs suggestive of associated tamponade or myocarditis.

* '''Clinical''' :
** '''Signs of acute right heart failure:''' Jugular turgidity, Jugular hepato reflux
** '''Signs of shock''': tachycardia and PAS<90mmHg, mottling, cold extremities
** '''Paradoxical pulse =''' îde la PAS de 10 mmHg à l'inspiration
** '''Fever > 38°'''
* Biological: Troponin elevation (associated myocarditis)
* ECG: microvoltage and electrical alternation (tamponade), ventricular rhythm disorders (myocarditis)
* Ultrasound:
** Effusion > 20 mm
** Compression of the right cavities
