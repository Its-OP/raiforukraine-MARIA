Secondary hypertension is suspected when :

- initially severe hypertension (BP > 180/110 mmHg)

- of hypertension before the age of 40

- hypertension associated with hypokalaemia

- suggestive clinical situations: resistant hypertension, disproportionate target organ damage in relation to the age of the hypertension.
