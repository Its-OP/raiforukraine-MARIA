Following the discovery of hypertension, a [[Announcement of a chronic disease SD-328|announcement consultation]] to the patient is necessary to:

- explore their knowledge, experience and perceptions of hypertension and cardiovascular disease,

- inform them in a fair and relevant manner about hypertension, associated risk factors, complications, treatment methods, expected benefits and overall cardiovascular risk,

- set treatment targets,

- obtain a shared medical decision between doctor and patient in order to promote therapeutic education, compliance and self-medication,

- draw up a personalised care plan with the patient, including lifestyle changes and any medication required.
