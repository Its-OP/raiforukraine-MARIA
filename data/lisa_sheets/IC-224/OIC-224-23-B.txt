The prevalence of essential hypertension has increased in recent years (obesity, etc.), but hypertension in children remains mainly "secondary".

Secondary causes include :

- Renal and renovascular causes (most frequent)

- Endocrine and tumour causes

- Cardiac cause

- Medicinal causes (corticosteroids)
