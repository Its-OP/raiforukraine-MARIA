The minimum initial paraclinical work-up for a hypertensive patient includes:

- natraemia, kalaemia, creatininaemia with estimated glomerular filtration rate,

- fasting blood sugar,

- exploration of a lipid anomaly in the fasting state,

- search for proteinuria (proteinuria/creatinuria ratio),

- resting electrocardiogram.
