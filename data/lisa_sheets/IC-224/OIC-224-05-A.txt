The objectives of the initial assessment of a hypertensive patient are :

- look for damage to target organs and pathologies associated with hypertension,

- assess overall cardiovascular risk "via" the use of appropriate grids such as the SCORE risk score,

- detect secondary hypertension and aggravating factors,

- look for orthostatic hypotension.
