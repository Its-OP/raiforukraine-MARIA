'''Suspicious death:'''

Death for which it is not possible to exclude the involvement of a third party.

'''Unusual death:'''

A death in which at least one of the following elements is unusual: the circumstances of the death, the way in which the body was found, the age of the victim.

'''Violent death:'''

Any death caused by accident, suicide or homicide.
