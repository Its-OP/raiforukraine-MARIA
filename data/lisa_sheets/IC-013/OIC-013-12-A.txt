'''<u>Death:</u>'''

Complete and definitive cessation of an organism's vital functions, followed by the progressive destruction of its tissues and organs. The diagnosis of death is based on the simultaneous observation of [[Knowing how to diagnose death 2C-013-DP-A01|negative signs of life and positive signs of death]].
