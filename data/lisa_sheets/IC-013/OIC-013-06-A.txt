The French statistical data comes from the Violence and Gender Relations survey (Virage)<ref>https://virage.site.ined.fr/</ref>, carried out by telephone in 2015 among 27,000 French people.

'''Prevalence of violence against women:''''

Women are more frequently victims of violence, whether in the public space (25% of women in the last 12 months compared with 14% of men), at work (20% of women in the last 12 months compared with 15.5% of men), or within a couple.


'''Prevalence of violence against women in relationships (domestic violence):''''

Women are more frequently the victims of domestic violence (5% of women in the last 12 months compared with 3% of men) and "22% of French women report domestic violence in the course of their lives". In France, it is estimated that "less than 18% of victims of domestic violence file a complaint".
