The French statistical data comes from the Violence and Gender Relations survey (Virage)<ref>https://virage.site.ined.fr/</ref>, carried out by telephone in 2015 among 27,000 French people.


'''Particularities of violence against women:''''

Women are more frequently victims of sexual and psychological violence, particularly in the public space (Figure 6).


'''[[Specific aspects of domestic violence 2C-011-DE-B01|Particular aspects of domestic violence against women:]]''''

o '''Multiple types of violence:''''

- Psychological (humiliation, belittling, etc.),

- Verbal (insults, threats, etc.),

- Physics,

- Sexual,

- Economic (control of money earned, etc.),

- Administrative (collection of identity papers, etc.),

- Health (refusal to see a doctor, etc.).

o '''Chronicity and recurrence of violence;''''

o '''Mechanism of the cycle of violence:''' Tension, aggression, justification, reconciliation with a possible acceleration of the cycle ;

o ''Few complaints filed'' (< 18% overall and < 11% for sexual violence);

o '''Major lifelong consequences:''' physical, psychological, social and economic health.
