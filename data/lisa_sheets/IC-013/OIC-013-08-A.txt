'''<u>Situations of vulnerability, particularly in cases of domestic violence:</u>''''

o '''Related to the medical situation'' :

-Disability (physical or mental),

-Pregnancy,

-Substance use disorder,

-Ages (teenagers and the elderly),

o '''Linked to the social and economic situation'' :

-Social, economic or family vulnerability,

-Difficulties accessing care,

-No command of the French language,

-Illegal status on French territory.
