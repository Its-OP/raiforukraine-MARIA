'''<u>Different types of compulsory medical certificates</u>'''<ref>Circulaire n° DSS/MCGR/DGS/2011/331 du 27 septembre 2011 relative à la rationalisation des certificats médicaux</ref> ''':'''

-Death,

-Report of violence (initial medical certificate),

-Accident at work and occupational disease,

-Application to the Maison Départementale des Personnes Handicapés,

-Compulsory vaccinations,

-Child health (8<sup>th</sup> day, 9<sup>th</sup> month and 24<sup>th</sup> month),

-Some school certificates,

-Certain certificates stating that you have no contraindications to practising sport.

Apart from these situations provided for by law or regulation, the doctor may refuse to issue a medical certificate.


'''What to do in the event of an abusive or illegal request for a certificate:'''

-Explain the reasons for refusal to the patient,

-Record the refusal of the certificate in the medical file,

-Contact the departmental medical council if you have any problems.
