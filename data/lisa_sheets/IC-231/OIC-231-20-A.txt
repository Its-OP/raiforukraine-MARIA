*Very rapid anarchic ventricular rhythm, polymorphic, responsible for cardiac arrest,
*The meshwork of the ventricular fibrillation becomes smaller and smaller and the ventricular fibrillation progresses to asystole,
*Indication for emergency cardiac electrical defibrillation.
