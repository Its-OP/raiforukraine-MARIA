[[File:ECG normal|thumbnail|link=Special:FilePath/ECG_normal]]

*Regular sinus rhythm between 60 and 100 bpm,
*Axis between -30° and +90°,
*No conduction disorder, PR between 120 and 200 ms, QRS ≤ 100 ms,
*no repolarisation disorder,
*normal QTc, (normal value 340ms ≤ QTc ≤440ms, long QTc if ≥480ms),
*No atrial or ventricular hypertrophy,
*no Q waves or planing of R waves,
*no microvoltage.
