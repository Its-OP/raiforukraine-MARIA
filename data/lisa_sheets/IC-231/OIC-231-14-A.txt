- '''Irregular rhythm'''

- '''Baseline increment''' without visible P wave (more or less large meshes)

- '''QRS fins''' unless :

o Bundle branch block (organic or frequency-dependent functional),

o Ventricular pre-excitation (anterograde accessory pathway).

- '''Causes of atrial fibrillation with regular ventricular rhythm'' :

o BAV complete with exhaust,

o Pacemaker-driven ventricular rhythm,

o Bi-tachycardia (combination of atrial fibrillation and ventricular tachycardia).
