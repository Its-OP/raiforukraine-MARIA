'''D.     Know the main causes of glomerular diseases''''

They are routinely sought in the presence of any sign of glomerular damage. Questioning, clinical examination (Table 2) and laboratory tests can help guide the diagnosis. The diagnosis is confirmed by histological examination of the renal parenchyma.
