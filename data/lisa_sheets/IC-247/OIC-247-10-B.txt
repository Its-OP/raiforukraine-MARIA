Risk factors for foot ulcers: OAHI, neuropathy with impaired statics/deformities of the foot, history of ulceration, social insecurity, etc.

Grading: 0: absence of sensory neuropathy and arteriopathy 1: presence of isolated sensory neuropathy 2: association of neuropathy with arteriopathy or foot/toe deformities 3: history of ulceration or amputation
