'''Methods of screening for T2D:'''

mainly fasting venous glycaemia, diabetes if >1.26g/l on 2 occasions.

For some (not consensual), an HbA1c >6.5% is also diagnostic of diabetes.


'''Target populations for T2D screening:'''

1/ patients with symptoms

2/ asymptomatic patients and

over 45 years of age, every 3 years if no FDR

patients with FDR every year

FDR for T2DM: metabolic syndrome, vascular or renal disease, family history of diabetes in the 1st<sup>degree, history of gestational diabetes or child with macrosomia, history of temporarily induced diabetes, geographical origin, sedentary lifestyle, history of delivery of a low-birth-weight child or pregnancy with IUGR.
