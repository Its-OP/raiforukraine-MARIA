*Acute pain on examination of the abdomen ('''[[Abdominal pain SD-004|abdominal pain]]'''')
*signs of peritoneal irritation
**Localised or generalised tension and/or contracture
**Pain on rectal examination or abdominal decompression
*Infectious syndrome
**Fever ≥ 38°5 C ("''[[Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|hyperthermia/fever]'''), respiratory rate > 20/min, tachycardia > 90/min
**Organ failure
*Other signs
**Digestive signs (ileus, nausea ('''[[Nausea SD-012|nausea]]'''''), vomiting ('''[[Vomiting SD-013|vomiting]]''''), tenesmus, hiccups or dyspnoea, diarrhoea)
**Urinary signs (dysuria, pollakiuria), in cases of pelvic abscess.
