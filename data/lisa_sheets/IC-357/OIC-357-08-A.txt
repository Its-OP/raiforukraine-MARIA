'''Sepsis = onset of life-threatening organ dysfunction caused by an overreaction of the host to an infection'''

<u>→ Suspicion of infection</u>

<u>→ At least two of the following clinical manifestations:</u>

*Systolic blood pressure ≤ 100 mmHg related to sepsis ('''[[Discovery of arterial hypotension SD-043|discovery of arterial hypotension]]''');
*lactate ≥ 18 mg/dL (2 mmol/L or normal laboratory values)
*Diuresis < 0.5 mL/kg/h for more than 2 hours despite appropriate filling.
*PaO2/FiO2 ratio < 300 mmHg in the absence of pneumonia
*Creatininaemia > 2 mg/dL (176.8 μmol/L)
*Bilirubinemia > 2 mg/dL (34.2 μmol/L)
*Thrombocytopenia < 100,000 /mm3
*Confusion or Glasgow score < 13

Septic shock

*Sepsis
*Vasopressor drugs (noradrenaline++) to maintain MAP ≥ 65 mmHg
*lactate ≥ 18 mg/dl (2 mmol/L) despite adequate filling
