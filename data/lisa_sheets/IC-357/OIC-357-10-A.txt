*In adults, the only radiological examination to be carried out when peritonitis is suspected is an abdomino-pelvic CT scan, with contrast injection ("''[[Rational request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic examination SD-178|rational request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic examination]]'').

*In children, abdominal ultrasound is the 1st-line examination.
