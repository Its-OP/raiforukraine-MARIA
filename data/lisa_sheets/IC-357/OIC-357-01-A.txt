* Definition of peritonitis = acute inflammation of the peritoneum, due to digestive perforation in the majority of cases.
* A frequent condition, it is the 2nd most common cause of sepsis in intensive care units, and has a very high mortality rate in the absence of appropriate and prompt treatment.
* In severe cases, the prognosis is life-threatening from the moment of diagnosis.
