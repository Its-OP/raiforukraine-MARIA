The 3 main scannographic signs of peritonitis are :

*gaseous effusion (indicating perforation of a hollow organ)
*intraperitoneal fluid effusion
*enhancement of the peritoneal layers

The last two may be combined in the event of the formation of partitioned fluid collections suggestive of intra-abdominal abscesses.
