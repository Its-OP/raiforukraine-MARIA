=== Suicidal thoughts ===
Baromètre santé 2020: 4.2% of respondents aged 18 to 85 said they had thought about committing suicide in the last 12 months.

=== Suicide attempts
Between 150,000 and 200,000 suicide attempts resulting in contact with the healthcare system in France, i.e. one attempt every 4 minutes.

The 2017 Health Barometer shows that 7.2% of 18-75 year olds said they had attempted suicide in their lifetime and 0.39% in the last 12 months.

Recurrence after ST: 40%. Half within the first year. Lifetime suicide mortality rate for a person who has attempted suicide = 10%.

=== Suicides ===
* Leading cause of preventable death
* 16.4 suicides per 100,000 person-years (25.3 for men and 8.0 for women) in France
* 2019: 9,000 deaths by suicide
* Second leading cause of death among 15-24 year olds and the leading cause of death among 25-34 year olds.
* At least one psychiatric disorder at the time of suicide in 90% of suicides.
