CAT guided by the following elements:

* Characteristic depressive episode to be sought systematically. Common in old age.
* Systematic search for comorbid psychiatric disorders;
* Think of physical pain and/or abuse in the event of any recent change in behaviour;
* Systematic psychosocial assessment.


In the event of a proven suicidal crisis: research the means envisaged and available. '''Removal of access to the means.'''
