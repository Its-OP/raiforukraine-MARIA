'''Fibromyalgia''' : diffuse pain in joints and tendon-muscle insertion zones, of progressive onset, disabling, associated with other symptoms (asthenia, sleep disorders, headaches, digestive disorders, anxiety-depressive disorders), with a strictly normal somatic examination and no differential diagnosis explaining the pain.
