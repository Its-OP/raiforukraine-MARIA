|Description=Hygroma, erysipelas and deep vein thrombosis
|Rubric=Positive diagnosis

*Involvement of periarticular structures: tenosynovitis, bursitis (or hygroma).
*Skin involvement: erysipelas (large acute red leg, colour anomalies of the extremities)
*Soft tissue involvement: oedema from deep vein thrombosis (localised or diffuse oedema)
