'''Mechanical pain: joint pain'''

- aggravated by or during exercise ;

- calmed by rest;

- maximum at the end of the day, with night-time awakenings when the position is changed;

- associated with a morning wake-up <30 minutes.

'''Inflammatory pain : Joint pain'''

- improved by effort except in the case of hyperalgesic thrust;

- not soothed by rest;

- worsens in the second half of the night, waking up at night;

- associated with a morning wake-up time >30 minutes.
