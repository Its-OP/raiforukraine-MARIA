* Isolated AF = AF without associated heart disease or co-morbidity.

* AF and heart failure" = the relationship between AF and heart failure is a two-way street:
AF can be the cause of heart failure, responsible for ''rhythmic heart disease'' ** Heart failure is a risk factor for AF.
** Heart failure is a risk factor for AF.

* AF and stroke": AF can be diagnosed when one of its complications, such as ischaemic stroke, occurs, particularly if it is asymptomatic.
