* ''A patient with atrial fibrillation may experience haemodynamic instability'', responsible for :
** Arterial hypotension / Cardiogenic shock / Cardiac decompensation
** Syncope

* Management'' :
** '''Rare''' indication for emergency cardioversion, regardless of the duration of effective anticoagulation prior to cardioversion.
** Implement effective anticoagulation as soon as possible.
