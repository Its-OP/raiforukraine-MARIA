AF is the most common supra-ventricular rhythm disorder: 1 in 3 people will develop AF during their lifetime.

'''Epidemiological characteristics''' :

* Male predominance
* Increasing prevalence with age. ''''
* Prevalence varies from country to country: role of "ethnicity" and "genetics" in the epidemiology of AF.
* The prevalence of AF is dependent on AF risk factors (see below).
* AF is strongly associated with common atrial flutter.
