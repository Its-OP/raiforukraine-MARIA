* A ''supra-ventricular'' rhythm disorder resulting from ''anarchic electrical activity in the atria'' leading to a loss of atrial contraction.
* Diagnosis of AF requires the presence of AF for ''more than 30 seconds'' on a surface ECG trace (Holter-ECG for example), or on the whole of a 12-lead ECG.
