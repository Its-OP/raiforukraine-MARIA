==Transient risk factors (TRFs)===
{| class="wikitable"
|+

!'''FdR transitoire <u>majeur</u>''''
!'''Main corresponding clinical situations'''
|-
|'''Surgery with general anaesthesia > 30 min in the last 3 months'''
|Orthopaedics (THR, PTG, hip fracture), neurosurgery, carcinological surgery...
|-
|Traumatology
|Lower limb fracture within the last 3 months
Prolonged immobilisation (plaster cast)
|-
|'''Immobilisation médicale > 3j dans les 3 derniers mois'''
|Stroke, acute heart failure, sepsis, acute respiratory decompensation...
|-
|Gynaecology - obstetrics
|Oetroprogestogenic contraception, pregnancy, post partum, hormone replacement therapy for the menopause...
|-
|
|
|-
!'''Transient ToR <u>minor</u>''''
!'''Main corresponding clinical situations'''
|-
|Traumatology
|Injury to a non-platellar lower limb with reduced mobility ≥ 3 days.
|-
|Travel
|Prolonged (plane > 6h)
|}

{| class="wikitable"

!Persistent <u>major</u> risk factors

|Active or treated cancer, myeloproliferative syndromes
|-
|'''Severe thrombophilias''''
|Antiphospholipid syndrome
Antithrombin deficiency

(Other thrombophilias are a minor ToR)
|-
|History of VTE
|1<sup>rst</sup> degree relatives
|-
| '''Persistent <u>minor</u> FdR'''
| '''Main corresponding clinical situations'''
|-
|Chronic inflammatory diseases''
|Digestive or articular (Crohn's, UC, Behçet), nephrotic syndrome...
|-
|General characteristics
|Age (increasing risk), obesity: BMI ≥ 30 kg/m².
