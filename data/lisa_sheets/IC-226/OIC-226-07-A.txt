=== '''Venous Doppler ultrasound:''' ===

====== '''If DVT is diagnosed:''' ======
[[File:Thrombus Echo.jpg|vignette]]
- [[Reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic examination SD-178|first-line examination]] to confirm the diagnosis of DVT.

- no contraindication to its use.

- diagnostic criterion: inability to compress the vein.

- Limitations: difficulties in accessing the venous network, for example: obesity, digestive interpositions, external fixator, plaster, wound, pain.



'''If PE is diagnosed:''''

- allows a diagnosis of PE to be made when there is a proximal, popliteal or suprapopliteal DVT and a clinical suspicion of PE.

- performed as a first-line treatment in cases of suspected PE in pregnant women or subjects with severe renal insufficiency
