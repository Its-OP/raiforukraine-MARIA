- '''Adenoma''' is the main lesion that increases the risk of cancer.

o The adenoma may be sporadic or appear in the context of a polyposis defined by a large number of polyps on endoscopy (> 100).

- '''Les lésions festonnées'''

o Sessile scalloped adenomas, most often in the colon, with a high risk of degeneration into adenocarcinoma

o Traditional scalloped adenomas (rarer) which also carry a risk of degeneration into adenocarcinoma
