The risk factors for colorectal cancer are :

- '''Exogenous:'''

o Sedentary lifestyle, overweight

o A diet rich in red meat and low in fibre

o Tobacco, alcohol (modest role)

- Endogenous

o Personal history of colorectal adenoma or adenocarcinoma ++++

o First-degree family history before the age of 65 of colorectal cancer or adenoma larger than 1 cm.

o Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) with colonic and/or rectal involvement for more than 20 years
