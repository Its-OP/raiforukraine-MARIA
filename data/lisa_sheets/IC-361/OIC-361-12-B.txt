'''<u>Achilles tendinopathy:</u>'''

* Pain around the Achilles tendon, during or after exercise, lasting a few days, sometimes chronic.
* Palpation of the tendon: painful, sometimes with one or more indurated nodules.
* Plantar flexion is painful.

'''<u>Retrocalcaneal bursitis:</u>''''

* Bursitis located anteriorly and deep to the insertion of the Achilles tendon on the calcaneus.
* Traumatic (ill-fitting shoes) or inflammatory (rheumatoid arthritis, gout).
* Pain, swelling and heat in the upper part of the heel in front of the Achilles tendon.
