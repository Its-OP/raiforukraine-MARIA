=== '''Malaria in the world''' ===
- Malaria is rife in the intertropical zone

- '''Africa''': > 95% of cases worldwide occur in sub-Saharan Africa ('''P. falciparum'' in > 90% of cases)

- '''Metropolitan France''': around 4,000 cases of '''imported''' malaria, 10 to 20 of which are fatal <gallery perrow="1">
