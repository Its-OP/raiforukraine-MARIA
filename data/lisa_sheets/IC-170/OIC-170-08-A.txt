====[[Management of a malaria attack SD-262|Treatment of a simple attack of ''P. falciparum'']]====

======<sup>st</sup> intention: Artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT) ======

*Artemether - lumefantrine
*Dihydroartemisinin - piperaquine

======2<sup>th</sup> intention: Atovaquone - proguanil or mefloquine in children======

======3<sup>th</sup> intention: Quinine======

====[[Management of a malaria attack SD-262|Treatment of a simple non-"falciparum" attack]]====

======1<sup>ère</sup> intention======

*Artemisinin-based combination - 3 days
*OR Chloroquine - 3 days

======Prevention of relapses (following an outbreak of P. vivax or P. ovale)======

*Primaquine - 14 days
*The absence of G6PD deficiency must be checked beforehand.
