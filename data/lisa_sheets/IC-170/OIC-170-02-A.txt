<big>'''''" Any fever on return''''' ''(days to months) '''from an endemic area is malaria until proven otherwise''' and even when another infection is identified '''"'''''</big>

- The risk exists even if appropriate antimalarial prophylaxis is taken.

- Any fever should be investigated in the context of a stay in a malaria-endemic area

- Thrombocytopenia should prompt a search for a stay in a malaria-endemic area and for malaria.
