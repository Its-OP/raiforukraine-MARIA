'''The treatment of malaria,''' whatever its form '''is a therapeutic emergency.''<gallery>
File:PEC palu.png|General principles of malaria management.
</gallery>The initiation of curative treatment must be preceded by the cessation of chemoprophylaxis and a search for contraindications.

====Hospitalisation criteria====
Hospitalisation criteria should not be confused with severity criteria.

The presence of at least one criterion requires initial hospitalisation:

======-Terrain======

*Children
*Risk of complications: co-morbidities, splenectomy, immunosuppression, pregnancy, etc.

======-Clinique/paraclinique======

*Signs of seriousness
*Platelets < 50 to G/L
*haemoglobin < 100 g/L
*Creatinine > 150 µmol/L
*Parasitaemia > 2%.

======-Doute diagnostique======

======-Thérapeutique======

*Digestive problems / digestive intolerance (effectiveness of PO treatment compromised)
*Risk of poor compliance
**Isolation, socio-cultural factors
**Difficult access to treatment and follow-up: difficulties in obtaining supplies of antimalarial drugs, distance of home from health services, etc.
**Failure of initial treatment
