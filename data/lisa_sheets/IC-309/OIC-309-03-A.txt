'''Exogenous risk factors:'''

* Exposure to work-related carcinogens:
** Asbestos,
** Silica,
** Polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons,
** Diesel.
* Exposure to non-occupational carcinogens:
** Radon,
** Air pollution (fine particles, including diesel).

'''Personal risk factors:''''

* Family predisposition,
* Chronic respiratory pathology: COPD; fibrosing diffuse interstitial lung disease.
* A history of lung cancer
