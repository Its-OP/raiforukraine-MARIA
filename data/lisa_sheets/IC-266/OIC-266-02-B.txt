The disease is inherited in the dominant autosomal dominant mode.

Two genes are involved in the vast majority of cases, ''PKD1'' and ''PKD2''.

The disease is similar in these 2 cases, with one exception: the mean age of end-stage renal failure (54 years ''PKD1'' ''vs'' 69 years ''PKD2'')''.''
