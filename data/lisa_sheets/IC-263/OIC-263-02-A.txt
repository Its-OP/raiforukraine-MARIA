The MAT syndrome meets a biological definition combining :

* haemolytic anaemia (collapsed haptoglobin, elevated LDH), mechanical type (presence of schizocytes);
* consumption thrombocytopenia;
* associated with one or more organ dysfunctions.


Histologically, it corresponds to the thrombotic microangiopathy lesion.
