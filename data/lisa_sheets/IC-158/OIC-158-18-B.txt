In France, the mortality rate for patients presenting with sepsis is around 30%. It is lower in children (10-20%).

It is around 40% in the case of septic shock''([[Announcement of a diagnosis of serious illness to the patient and/or his family SD-327|announcement of a diagnosis of serious illness to the patient and/or his family]])'''.

Survivors may develop complications such as chronic renal failure, limb amputations, etc.
