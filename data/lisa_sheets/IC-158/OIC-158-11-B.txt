The most common causes of sepsis/septic shock in adults are :

* Pneumonia (50% of cases)
* Intra-abdominal infections (20% of cases)
* Urinary tract infections (10% of cases)
* Catheter infections (5% of cases)
* Soft tissue or neuro-meningeal infections (< 5% of cases)


In children, the origin of sepsis varies according to age, and is strongly influenced by vaccination.
