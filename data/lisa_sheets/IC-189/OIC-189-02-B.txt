'''Frequent primary immune deficiencies with preferential revelation in adults'''

*Variable common immune deficiency
*IgG subclass deficiency
*IgA deficiency


'''Rare primary immune deficiencies with preferential revelation in adults'''

*Good's syndrome (Thymoma)
*CD4 lymphopenia
*LOCID
*Immune deficiencies favouring infections by mycobacteria (deficiencies in cytokine pathways such as interferon gamma and interleukin 12 (FNg-R, IL12)).

'''Other rare primary immunodeficiencies affecting children may also occur exceptionally in adults''.
