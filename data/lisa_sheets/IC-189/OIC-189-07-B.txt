*Weighted immunoglobulin dosage
*NFplatelets
*If associated hypoalbuminemia on EPP --> suggestive of Ig loss mechanism
**Proteinuria/24 h or proteinuria/creatinuria
**Clearance of alpha 1 antritrypsin
*If no hypoalbuminemia
**Immunofixation, Bence Jones proteinuria, serum calcium
**T and B lymphocyte phenotyping
**depending on the context and if haemopathy is suspected: myelogram, CT scan, skeletal X-ray
