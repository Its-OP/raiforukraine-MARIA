Diagnosis

*Positive: gamma globulin level <6 g/l on EPP.
*False hypogammaglobulinaemia (rare e.g. cryoglobulinaemia).

Causes

*Excess wasting (frequently associated with hypoalbuminemia on EPP)
**Nephrotic syndrome
**Exudative enteropathy
**Accelerated metabolism (undernutrition)
*Production defects
**Drug interactions (prolonged corticosteroid therapy, cyclophosphamide, rituximab, anti-epileptics)
**Lymphoid and plasma cell haemopathies (myeloma ++)
**Humoral immune deficiency: agammaglobulinaemia, CVID
