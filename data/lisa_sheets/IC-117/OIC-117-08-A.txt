Psoriasis is a systemic inflammatory disease. Numerous co-morbidities may be associated with it, especially in severe and extensive forms of the disease. Systematic screening for these is essential, with the cooperation of other specialities.

Depression and anxiety: linked to the visible nature of the dermatosis, its stigmatisation, pain, pruritus, cracks, loss of quality of life and social handicap. Look for addictions: alcohol, tobacco

Metabolic syndrome: abdominal obesity, diabetes, hypertension, dyslipaemia.

Cardiovascular diseases: increased risk from an early age.

Inflammatory diseases of the digestive tract
