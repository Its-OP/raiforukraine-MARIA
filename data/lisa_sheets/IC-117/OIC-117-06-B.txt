Monoarthritis, oligoarthritis or polyarthritis

Associated tenosynovitis: dactylitis with sausage fingers

Axial involvement clinically similar to that of ankylosing spondylitis and/or associated peripheral involvement similar to rheumatoid arthritis, with inflammatory arthralgias (nocturnal) and morning rash.

Digital involvement (distal interphalangeal joints) is often associated with nail involvement.
