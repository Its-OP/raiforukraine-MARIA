Definition of "classic" prolonged fever '''([[Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|hyperthermia/fever]])'''''

* Occurrence in a subject with no particular medical condition (non-immunocompromised, not hospitalised)
* Temperature (taken at rest) ≥ 38.3°C on several occasions,
* For ≥ 3 weeks,
* No cause identified after appropriate investigations carried out during three days in hospital or after three consultations. There is no pre-established, definitive list of these investigations, which vary according to the clinical context of each patient.


Note: there is no pre-established, definitive list of appropriate investigations (which vary depending on the patient) before making the diagnosis of prolonged fever.
