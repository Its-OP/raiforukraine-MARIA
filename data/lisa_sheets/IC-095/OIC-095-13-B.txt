|Order=13}}Differential diagnoses of carpal tunnel: Guyon's canal syndrome (ulnar nerve), involvement of the median nerve at the elbow, C6 radiculalgia, plexus involvement (thoraco-brachial outlet).

Differential diagnosis of ulnar nerve compression at the elbow: C7 radiculalgia

Differential diagnosis of fibular nerve compression at the neck of the fibula: L5 radiculalgia

Differential diagnosis of thoraco-brachial crossing syndrome: cervicobrachial neuralgia
