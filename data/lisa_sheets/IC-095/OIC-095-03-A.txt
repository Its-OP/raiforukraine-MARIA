*'''Plexopathy''' '''='''' symptoms and signs associated with damage to several spinal roots
*'''Pancoast Tobias syndrome:''''
**Background: cancer of the pulmonary apex +++, invading by contiguity the inferior primary trunk of the brachial plexus (C8/T1 spinal roots), the stellate ganglion of the orthosympathetic chain and the posterior arch of the 1<sup>ere</sup> rib.
**Root involvement in C8-T1 territory: pain and hypo/anaesthesia of the medial aspect of the upper limb up to the 4th and 5th fingers + deficit of the flexors of the fingers and interosseous muscles, thenar and hypothenar amyotrophy.
**Claude Bernard-Horner syndrome (damage to the stellate ganglion of the orthosympathetic chain): miosis + reduction in the palpebral slit due to damage to the lift muscle of the upper eyelid + enophthalmos.
