*Diopathic in the majority of cases
*Repetitive strain injury: favoured by certain occupations (''cf'' occupational disease table N°57 of the general Social Security scheme)
*Hormonal factors: pregnancy (last trimester), menopause, etc.
*Compression due to a reduction in the volume of the container:
**vicious calcar after radius fracture
**Exuberant arthrosis
*Compression by volume increase of the content / "infiltrative" causes:
**Inflammatory tenosynovitis in rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, scleroderma, gout
**Microcrystal deposits in chondrocalcinosis
**Endocrinopathies: hypothyroidism, diabetes, acromegaly
**Amyloidosis, chronic renal failure on dialysis
