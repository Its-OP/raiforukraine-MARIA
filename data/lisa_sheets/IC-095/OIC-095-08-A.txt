- First-line treatment of radiculalgia: short course of NSAIDs if no contraindication, painkillers avoiding morphine (Prescribe non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs); Prescribe painkillers).

- Second-line treatment: epidural infiltration of cortisone derivatives

- Third-line treatment if good radioclinical concordance: spinal surgery

- Physiotherapy for spinal pain (Prescription of physiotherapy)

- Lumbar restraint to be discussed (prescription of a simple brace)

Socio-professional support (adaptation of the workstation) if necessary with occupational health structures (Therapeutic modification of lifestyle (sleep, physical activity, diet, etc.); Announcement of the diagnosis of a serious illness to the patient and/or his family).
