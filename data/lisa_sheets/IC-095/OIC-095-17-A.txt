|Order=17}}(1) General principles

Pain management (Assessment and management of acute pain; Prescribing analgesics; Prescribing non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs))

(2) Carpal tunnel treatment :

- aetiological if a curable secondary aetiology is found

- medical in the first instance: wearing a rest splint (prescription of a simple device) and corticosteroid infiltration (prescription of general or local corticosteroids)

- surgical treatment as a second-line treatment or if the patient has a deficient form from the outset (neurolysis of the median nerve = section of the annular ligament of the carpus)

- prophylactic (functional splint, adaptation of workstation) indicated in the case of work activities that encourage it (repeated movements of the wrist, use of vibrating equipment) (Therapeutic modification of lifestyle (sleep, physical activity, diet, etc.))

(3) Treatment of fibular nerve compression: elimination of prolonged weight-bearing. In the event of a compressive lesion, surgery should be discussed.

(4) Thoracic outlet syndrome :

- First-line physiotherapy

- Surgery in cases of arterial damage or anatomical abnormality (supernumerary rib)
