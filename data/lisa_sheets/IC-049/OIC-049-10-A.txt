'''Questioning data:''' pubertal ages of parents and siblings, age at menarche of mother, parental heights with calculation of genetic target height, family or personal history (IUGR, neurological history, adoption, neurofibromatosis type 1 (NF1)), visual signs or signs of HTIC (headache, nausea, morning vomiting), analysis of growth rate (curve).

Clinical examination: Complete examination, pubertal stage (Tanner), skin spots, signs of hyperandrogenism (acne, hirsutism, hyperseborrhoea, clitoral hypertrophy).
