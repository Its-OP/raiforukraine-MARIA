Ptosis can be :

*neurogenic'' by paralysis of the III, or by paralysis of the cervical sympathetic (Claude-Bernard-Horner syndrome: association of ptosis-myosis-enophthalmos).
*myogenic'' due to damage to the levator muscle of the upper eyelid: myasthenia, congenital ptosis.
*senile'' due to relaxation of the levator aponeurosis.
*traumatic'' due to rupture of the levator aponeurosis
