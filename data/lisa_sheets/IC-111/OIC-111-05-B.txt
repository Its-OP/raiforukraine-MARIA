=== Neonatal acne: linked to maternal androgens, transient and self-limiting. ===
<br />

=== Acne linked to endocrinopathies: suspect if severe acne resistant to treatment and clinical signs suggestive of hyperandrogenism: hirsutism, alopecia, cycle disorders. Prescribe a hormonal assessment and abdomino-pelvic ultrasound ===
<br />
