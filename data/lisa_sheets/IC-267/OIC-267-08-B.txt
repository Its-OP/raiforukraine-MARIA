*Treatment is necessary to prevent muscle wasting, bone demineralisation and, in children, stunted growth.
*Maintain plasma bicarbonate levels > 22 mmol/L by taking sodium bicarbonate orally (1 to 6 capsules a day, for example in cases of chronic renal failure).
