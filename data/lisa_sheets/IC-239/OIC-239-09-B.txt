In the case of RA, the major step is to be able to recognise secondary RA. Questioning and clinical examination provide important clues, and should be supplemented by testing for antinuclear antibodies and capillaroscopy in cases of suspected secondary RA, or recent onset or worsening RA ('''''SD-178 : [[Reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic test SD-178|reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic test]]'')'''''
