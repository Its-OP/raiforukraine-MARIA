Know that fractures in children predominate in the upper limbs and that fractures of the lower limbs increase in prevalence from pre-adolescence onwards.

Know that the kinetics of trauma increase with age and with the use of wheeled or rolling devices.

Knowing that sport becomes the dominant aetiology from pre-adolescence onwards.
