In addition to biological screening, ultrasound monitoring helps to detect trisomy 21. If there is an abnormality on ultrasound, a foetal karyotype will be prescribed. The main signs are as follows:

In the 1st trimester: neck greater than 3.5 mm;

In the 2nd trimester: heart disease, hypoplasia or aplasia of the nasal bones, short femurs, indirect signs of duodenal stenosis... ;

In the 3rd trimester: intrauterine growth retardation with no placental cause, heart disease.
