'''Mechanisms of the placebo effect:'''

- Largely unknown.

*Hypothesis: for example, production of endorphins with a placebo analgesic.

- Individual variability

*Existence of responders, non-responders and occasional responders (depending on the context).

- Non-reproducible (random).

- Non-specific.

- No dose effect.

- Exists even in young children ("placebo by proxy", effect mediated by parents).

'''Factors influencing the placebo effect:'''

- Appearance: route of administration, colour, taste, etc.

- Doctor: empathy, conviction, sociability, age, reputation.

- Patient: psychological profile, culture, beliefs, expectations.

- The doctor-patient relationship.

- Illness and its cultural or media representation.
