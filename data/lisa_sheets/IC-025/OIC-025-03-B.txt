Increasing incidence in developed countries, 2% in France.

Risk factors: all those affecting tubal motility:

- Upper genital infections (UGI) .

- Tobacco (effect - dose relationship) '''([[Prevention of tobacco-related risks SD-314|prevention of tobacco-related risks)]]''''

- Previous tubal or abdominopelvic surgery (excluding appendectomy and uncomplicated endo-uterine aspiration)

- History of EP

- Alterations of the tubal wall: endometriosis, tuberculosis, bilharzia, uterine or tubal malformation '''([[Pelvic pain SD-099|pelvic pain]], [[Abdominal pain SD-004|abdominal pain]])'''''

- High maternal age (> 35 years)

- Microprogestational contraception, intrauterine device

- Medically assisted procreation (in vitro fertilisation in particular) '''([[Difficulty procreating SD-033|difficulty procreating]])'''''
