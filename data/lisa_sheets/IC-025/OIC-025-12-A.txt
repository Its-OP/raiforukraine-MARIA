Prevention of mandatory anti-D alloimmunisation in rhesus-negative patients

Martial supplementation or transfusion in cases of anaemia

Secondary prevention :

- Stop smoking '''([[Smoking risk prevention SD-314|smoking risk prevention]])''''

- Screening and treatment of STIs in the patient and her partner (if sequelae of IGH at laparoscopy)

- Psychological support

- Risk of recurrence of around 20% regardless of treatment: early ultrasound to locate the disease during the next pregnancy
