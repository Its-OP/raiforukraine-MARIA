'''A normal menstrual cycle lasts 25 to 35 days according to the WHO.''' '''[[Menstrual cycle disorders SD-094|troubles du cycle menstruel]]''' are very often the result of a failure to ovulate. Anovulation (absence of ovulation) or dysovulation (abnormal ovulation) can lead to various menstrual cycle abnormalities.
