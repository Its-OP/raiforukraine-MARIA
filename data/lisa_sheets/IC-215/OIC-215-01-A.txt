Purpura ('''[[Purpura/ecchymosis/hematoma SD-089|purpura/ecchymosis/hematoma]]''')

* red or purplish skin lesions
* separated by an interval of healthy skin
* do not disappear with vitro-pressure
* associated with extravasation of blood into the dermis
* variable in appearance: punctiform, "pinhead" (petechial purpura), in the form of linear streaks (vibices) or larger lesions (ecchymotic purpura).
