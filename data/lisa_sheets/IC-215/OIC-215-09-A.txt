Apart from the 2 emergency situations described in this chapter, the management of purpura depends mainly on that of its cause.

Platelet transfusion '''([[Prescribe and perform a blood transfusion SD-272|prescribe and perform a blood transfusion]])''' may be indicated in the setting of central thrombocytopenia in the event of haemorrhage and/or profound thrombocytopenia.
