Threatening seizure = seizure lasting more than 5 minutes - delay in administration of first line of treatment

Permanent seizure disorder = seizure lasting more than 30 minutes (life-threatening in the case of a tonic-clonic generalised seizure ([[Convulsions SDD-120|convulsions]]) or seizures that recur without regaining consciousness ([[Coma and disturbed consciousness SDD-028|coma and disturbed consciousness]]).
