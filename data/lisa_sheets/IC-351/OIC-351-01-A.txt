[[Agitation SD-114|Agitation]] and acute delirium are two distinct syndromes which may or may not coexist. A non-psychiatric cause must be systematically sought.

A state of emotional tension and unproductive physical and mental hyperactivity.

The activity is usually unproductive and stereotyped. It takes the form of behaviours such as walking, inability to stay still, hand rubbing, pulling on clothes, inability to sit still.
