Acute delirium generally includes positive psychotic symptoms ([[Delusional ideas SD-124|delusional ideas]] and [[Hallucinations SD-122|hallucinations]]). Other symptoms may be associated:

* Symptoms of psychotic disorganisation
* Negative psychotic symptoms
* Depressive or manic symptoms
