Nosocomial diarrhoea is defined as diarrhoea that occurs within 3 days of hospitalisation.

Infectious causes are at the forefront, in particular viruses +++ and Cl. Difficile.
Covid 19 infection, which can manifest as acute diarrhoea, should also be considered.

Risk factors: prolonged length of stay, advanced age, large number of antibiotics received.
