Discomfort is usually benign but may be associated with a serious pathology. Look for :

* haemodynamic signs: signs of shock, bradycardia or tachycardia, signs of cardiac insufficiency
* Respiratory signs: signs of hypoxia (cyanosis or desaturation), apnoea or bradypnoea, signs of struggle, signs of hypercapnia.
* Neurological signs: signs of HTIC, consciousness disorders, tonus disorders, abnormal movements, focal signs.
