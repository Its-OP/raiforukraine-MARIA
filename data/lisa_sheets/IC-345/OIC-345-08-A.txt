All parents must be aware of the preventive measures (they are reminded in the health record).

* No co-sleeping
* No co-sleeping
* Cradle in parents' bedroom for at least 6 months
* No pillows, duvets, cocoons, bumper pads or blankets
* Do not over-cover the child

* Avoid smoking in the family
* Encourage breast-feeding
