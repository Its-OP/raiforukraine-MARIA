- '''Malaria:''' "any fever on return from an endemic area should be investigated for malaria". This is a diagnostic emergency (see [[item 170]] - Malaria).

- '''Dengue:''' The most common arbovirosis, 2nd most common cause of fever on return from the tropics

- Other arboviroses

- '''Viral hepatitis'''

- '''Typhoid fever'''

- Rickettsioses

- '''Tissue amoebosis : (See [[item 172]] - Digestive parasitoses)''''

- '''Schistosomiasis in the invasion phase'''

Cosmopolitan diseases may also manifest themselves as fever on return from a tropical zone, whether infectious (pyelonephritis, pneumonia, influenza, shigellosis, leptospirosis, HIV, etc.) or not (thromboembolic, inflammatory, neoplastic, etc.).
