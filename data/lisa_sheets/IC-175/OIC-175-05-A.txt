In addition to the compulsory and recommended vaccinations listed in the vaccination record (item 146), other vaccinations may be indicated depending on the situation:

- the global epidemic situation

- the place of residence: health and hygiene conditions, period and duration of stay, administrative obligations, etc.

- the patient: age, pregnancy, medical history, previous vaccination status, immunity status, budget (social security does not cover traveller's vaccines)

Compulsory vaccinations:

- yellow fever vaccination compulsory or recommended for a stay in inter-tropical Africa or the Amazon basin,

- Meningococcal A,B,C,Y,W135 obligatory for pilgrimages to Saudi Arabia and recommended for a stay in the meningitis belt in Africa during the dry zone or any epidemic zone in the event of prolonged and close contact with the local population or work in refugee camps.

Vaccinations recommended:

- Hepatitis A vaccine (areas with low levels of hygiene).

- typhoid vaccine (typhoid fever) for prolonged stays in areas with low levels of hygiene (particularly the Indian sub-continent)

- rabies vaccine (high-risk areas for rabies, developing countries), pre-exposure and post-exposure in the event of a high-risk bite. Particularly recommended for young children and extended stays.
