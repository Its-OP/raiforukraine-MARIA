Climacteric symptoms are essentially linked to oestrogen deficiency.

The main symptoms are

- '''[[Hot flushes SD-024|troubles vaso-moteurs]] and sleep disorders''' (more or less associated with night sweats)

In 80% of women, the average duration of symptoms is 5 to 7 years, but BVM can sometimes be observed up to 15 years after the onset of the menopause.

'''-[[Sad mood/moral pain SD-123|mood disorders]]''' frequent, (irritability, thymic lability and/or a depressive tendency)

'''-arthralgias''' '''of the inflammatory type associated with a morning shake-off''''


Main symptoms reported in relation to the menopause. Symptoms directly attributable to estrogen deficiency are shown in bold.
