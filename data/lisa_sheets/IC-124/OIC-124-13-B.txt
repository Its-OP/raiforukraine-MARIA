The recommended dietary hygiene measures for menopausal women are

* Avoidance of cardiovascular risk factors (tobacco, alcohol)
* Increasing energy expenditure: regular physical activity
* Moderate carbohydrate and fat energy intake.
* Adequate calcium and vitamin D intake: supplementation if necessary, particularly in cases of osteoporosis.
