Between 5 and 8.8 million poor people in France, depending on the definition adopted.

At 30 June 2018, around 4.94 million people affiliated to the general scheme benefited from complementary universal health cover (CMU-C).

Nearly 1.6 million people benefit from the ACS (Aide pour une complémentaire santé).
