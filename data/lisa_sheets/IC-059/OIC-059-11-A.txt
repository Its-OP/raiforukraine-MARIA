Protection maladie universelle (PUMA): guarantees all persons working or residing in France on a stable and regular basis the right to cover their healthcare costs on a personal basis and on a continuous basis throughout their lives.

Supplementary CMU (CMU-C): free, means-tested supplementary health insurance that covers what is not covered by compulsory health insurance schemes.

State Medical Aid (AME): for nationals in an irregular or precarious situation.
