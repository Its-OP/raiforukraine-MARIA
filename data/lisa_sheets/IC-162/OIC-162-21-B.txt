Probabilistic treatment: 2018 recommendations (CNGOF - SPILF) recommend:

- Uncomplicated IGH: Ceftriaxone 1g IV/IM single dose + Doxycycline 100mg/12h po 10 days + Metronidazole 500mg/12h po 10 days


- Gonococcus: Ceftriaxone (1 g/d, IV, 14 days).

- Chlamydia: Doxycycline (100 mg/12 hours, "per os" or IV, 14 days).
