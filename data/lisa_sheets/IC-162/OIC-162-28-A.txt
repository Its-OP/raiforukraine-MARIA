Early syphilis: Benzathine penicillin G single dose IM 2.4 million units

- If penicillin allergy: doxycycline 14 days

- Herxheimer reaction: possible for primary or secondary syphilis, lysis of treponemes


HIV+ patient (primary or secondary syphilis) : Benzathine penicillin G single dose IM 2.4 million units
