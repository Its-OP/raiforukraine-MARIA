STI test :

- Serologies : HIV, HBV, treponemal test +/- non-treponemal test

- PCR: Gonococcus and chlamydia (on 1st urine stream, urethral discharge, vaginal, pharyngeal and anal self-sampling depending on sexual practices)

- Pharyngeal and anal PCR according to sexual practices

- Culture: systematic for gonococcus, urethral discharge, vaginal self-sampling, depending on symptoms

- Upper respiratory tract infection in women: CRP, CBC, samples taken with a speculum for direct examination + standard culture + PCR, surgical or radioguided samples if complicated infection for culture + PCR + to look for mycoplasma.

- Examination of the cervix if there is a condyloma in the patient or her partner
