'''Gonococcus''':

- Uncomplicated infection: ceftriaxone single dose IM or IV 1000 mg

- Systematic anti-chlamydia treatment: doxycycline 7 days

- If Î²-lactam allergy: single dose of azithromycin or gentamycin or ciprofloxacin

- In pregnant women: single dose of ceftriaxone or cefixime


'''-''' Orchi-epididymitis: ceftriaxone single dose (IM or IV 1000 mg) or cefixime

- Sepsis, endocarditis, meningitis, endometritis, salpingitis: ceftriaxone +/- hospitalisation


Chlamydia:

- Uncomplicated infection: Doxycycline per os 200mg for 7 days or azithromycin 1g single dose (risk of resistance to other microorganisms)

- 2nd</sup> intention: erythromycin or ofloxacin

- In pregnant women: single-dose azithromycin

- Newborn: eyrthromycin (if pneumopathy or opthalmia)

'''-'''' Orchi-epididymitis: doxycycline 10 days

- Endometritis, salpingitis: doxycycline 14 days


Trichomoniasis:

- Metronidazole PO 2g single dose or 500 mg 2x/d 7 days or Secnidazole 2g single dose
