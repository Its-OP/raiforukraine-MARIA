Infectious :

- Syphilitic chance: single with clean, indurated background, variable location

- Herpes: painful superficial ulcers with polycyclic outlines

- Chancre mou: terrain (Africa, South America, Asia), single/multiple lesion(s), dirty background, pain (+++), inflammatory adenopathies

- Donovanosis: in Africa, South America and Asia, mildly painful granulomatous lesions

- Nicolas-Favre disease (or lymphogranulomatosis venereum): terrain (Africa, South America, Asia, homosexuals), anorectitis, anogenital ulcerations, misleading diarrhoea


Non-infectious: traumatic, caustic, mechanical, bullous dermatitis, toxidermia, etc.
