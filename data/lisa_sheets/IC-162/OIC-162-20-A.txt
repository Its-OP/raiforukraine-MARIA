Gonococcal :

- Ceftriaxone single dose IM or IV 1000 mg

- If allergic to Î²-lactams: single dose of azithromycin or gentamicin or ciprofloxacin


Chlamydia :

- Doxycycline per os 200mg for 7 days (or azithromycin 1g single dose but risk of resistance to other micro-organisms)

- 2<sup>th</sup> intention: erythromycin or ofloxacin


Early syphilis: Benzathine penicillin G DU IM 2.4 million units


Trichomoniasis: Metronidazole per os 2g single dose or 500 mg 2x/d 7 days or Secnidazole 2g single dose
