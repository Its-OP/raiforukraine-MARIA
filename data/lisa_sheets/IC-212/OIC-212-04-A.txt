'''A haemogram must be performed urgently in the event of:'''

- '''a state of shock;'''

- '''an intense pallor;'''

- '''ulcerative or antibiotic-resistant angina;'''

- '''a high fever after taking medication, especially after antimitotic chemotherapy;'''

- '''an antibiotic-resistant fever;'''

- '''extensive petechial purpura, haemorrhagic bullae in the mucous membranes, retinal haemorrhages at the back of the eye, haemorrhagic syndrome.'''

- '''A tumour syndrome of the haematopoietic organs'''
