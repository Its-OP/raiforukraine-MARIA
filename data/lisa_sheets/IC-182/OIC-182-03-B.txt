There is greater protection for job retention, but redundancy is still possible (economic redundancy or inability to redeploy the employee).

In the event of dismissal, redundancy payments are doubled.

Disabled worker status is granted if the degree of disability resulting from the accident at work or occupational disease is at least 10%.
