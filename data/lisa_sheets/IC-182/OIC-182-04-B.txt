*Part-time work for therapeutic reasons, to be requested by the attending physician. Work usually at least 50%, no maximum duration (but exceptionally exceeds 3 years). Salary at 100% during TPT.

*Recognition of the status of disabled worker (RQTH)'': file completed by the attending physician, the occupational physician and the patient, then sent to the Maison Départementale des Personnes Handicapées (Departmental House for Disabled Persons)

*1st, 2nd or 3rd category social security disability:
**Applied for by the treating doctor and granted by the social security medical officer.
**Allows an invalidity allowance which can compensate for the reduction in salary linked to the reduction in activity (and thus, for example, allow the person to work part-time), and compensate for the loss of salary due to invalidity in the 1st category of social security.
**Please note that a patient cannot be granted invalidity for an illness that has already been recognised as an accident at work or an occupational disease.
