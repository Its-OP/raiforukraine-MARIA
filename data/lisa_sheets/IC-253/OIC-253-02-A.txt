'''WHO definition''': an excess of body fat leading to adverse health consequences'' [[Cardiovascular risk factors and prevention]]


''' In adults:''''

                             Obesity defined by a BMI ≥ 30 kg/m2 (BMI=Weight (in kg)/Height (in m<sup>2</sup>)

                             Gradation of obesity in adults according to BMI

{| class="wikitable"
|'''Classification'''
|'''BMI (kg/m<sup>2</sup>)'''
|-
|Normal weight
|18,5 à 24,9
|-
|Overweight
|25 à 29,9
|-
|Stage I obesity (moderate)
|30 à 34,9
|-
|Stage II obesity (severe)
|35 à 39,9
|-
|Obesity stage III (massive)
|≥ 40
|}



''' In children:''''

Diagnosis based on the BMI recorded on the weight curves (see health record) of reference girls and boys, according to the international standards of the IOTF (International Obesity Task Force).

- Overweight if BMI above the IOTF 25 curve (corresponds to a BMI of 25 kg/m2 at age 18)

- Obesity if BMI above the IOTF 30 curve (corresponds to a BMI of 30 kg/m2 at age 18)
