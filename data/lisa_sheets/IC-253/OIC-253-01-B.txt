'''Epidemiology:'''' Prevalence in France in 2020

- Obesity: 17% of adults and 6% of children aged 8 to 17

- Obesity + overweight: Around 50% of adults and 16-20% of children

- Increasing in adults, stable in paediatrics

- Increases with age

- Higher in disadvantaged social classes, in the north and east of France, and in overseas territories.
