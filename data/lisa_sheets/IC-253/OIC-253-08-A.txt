'''Pathophysiological elements:''''

Common obesity results from the interaction of genetic, epigenetic, environmental, psychological, social and biological factors.

Determinants of common obesity :

1.     Education to promote dysregulation of eating behaviour

2.     Life difficulties (including psycho-trauma) / psychological difficulties / stress

3.     Physical inactivity / sedentary lifestyle

4.     Lack of sleep

5.     Poorly diversified diet / sugary drinks
