'''<u>[[Discovery of abnormalities on pulmonary auscultation SDD-020|List]] non-exhaustive clinical situations serving as red flags</u>''' (= seek a diagnosis other than sarcoidosis; or conclude sarcoidosis only after thorough and repeated evaluation of differential diagnoses)

*- age of onset less than 20 years or more than 50 years;
*- presence of ''[[Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|fever]]'' or ''[[Weight loss SD-017|general signs]]'' (except LÃ¶fgren's syndrome);
*- presence of ''[[Discovery of abnormalities on pulmonary auscultation SD-020|crackling sounds]]'' (pulmonary auscultation is classically normal in sarcoidosis) or digital hippocratism;
*- micronodules of non-lymphatic distribution on the lung scan;
*- compressive or asymmetric mediastinal ''[[Single or multiple adenopathies SD-016|adenopathies]]''';
*- normal chest X-ray;
*- Tuberculin intradermal reaction positive;
*- serum protein electrophoresis SD-193|hypogammaglobulinaemia]'' at diagnosis;
*- positive HIV serology.
