- Sometimes normal

- Most frequent anomaly: diffusion disorder (lower carbon monoxide diffusion capacity (DLCO))

- The following are possible: restrictive, obstructive (of various mechanisms) or mixed ventilatory disorders

- Possibility of carrying out exercise tests such as the 6-minute walk test or the respiratory exercise test (VO2max).
