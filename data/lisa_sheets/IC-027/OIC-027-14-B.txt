3 messages:

1/ in case of primary infection: maternal treatment (aciclovir 1g in 5 doses or valaciclovir 2g in 2 doses for 5 to 10 days) and caesarean section if infection within 6 weeks before start of labour except RPM > 4h.

2/ recurrence: no sampling, vaginal route possible if RPM >4h

3/ prevention in case of recurrence with valaciclovir 1g in 2 doses from 36 SA until birth
