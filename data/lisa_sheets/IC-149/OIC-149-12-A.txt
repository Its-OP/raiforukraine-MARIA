They involve hypertrophy of the inflamed tonsils, covered '''with thick, grey, adherent and bleeding, extensive false membranes''. They often extend "beyond the pillars of the tonsil and may cover the uvula". They may be associated with ''purpura of the soft palate in infectious mononucleosis''.
