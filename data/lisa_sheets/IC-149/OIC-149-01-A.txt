'''Angina''' is an inflammatory disease of the oropharynx affecting the tonsils.'''

Classified according to 4 clinical forms:

- erythematous/erythematopulmonary angina

- pseudomembranous" angina

- vesicular angina

- ulcerative and ulcero-necrotic angina
