=== Individual prevention: ===
- Chemoprophylaxis with Oseltamivir: benefit-risk balance debated

- Vaccination +++

=== Collective prevention: ===
  Hygiene measures

o Cover your mouth when coughing (using your elbow), then wash your hands

o Cover your nose when sneezing, then wash your hands

o Blow your nose with single-use tissues

o Always spit into a single-use tissue

o Dispose of these items in a covered bin, then wash your hands with a hydro-alcoholic solution.
