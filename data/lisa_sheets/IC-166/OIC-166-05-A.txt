=== Respiratory ===

==== - High ====
o Otitis

o Sinusitis

o Laryngitis

o Possible bacterial superinfections

==== - Low ====
o Acute bronchitis

o COPD exacerbations

o Primary viral pneumonia and, rarely, primary malignant influenza: hypoxaemic alveolar-interstitial lung disease (ARDS)

o Secondary bacterial pneumonia: on average, between D5 and D7: reappearance of respiratory symptoms (productive cough)
<br />

=== Extrarespiratory ===
- Digestive disorders (diarrhoea)

- Lymphocytic meningitis with or without encephalitis

- Pericarditis, myocarditis

- Rhabdomyolysis in children

- Reye's syndrome: influenza B treated with aspirin in children, with encephalitis and acute fulminant hepatitis, and death in 1 out of 2 cases.
