Vaccine recommended for :

- Over 65s

- Pregnant women

- People, including children aged 6 months and over, with conditions that increase the risk of complications from influenza

- Healthcare professionals

- Professionals in regular and prolonged contact with people at severe risk

- Flight attendants and tourism professionals accompanying groups
