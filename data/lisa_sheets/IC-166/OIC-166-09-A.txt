Influenza is diagnosed clinically and no paraclinical examination is necessary.

In hospitalised patients, to guide treatment and hygiene measures: nasopharyngeal swab and Myxovirus influenzae PCR.

Indication of additional tests for epidemiological purposes :

- TROD (Test rapide d'orientation diagnostique) for epidemic and virological monitoring of surveillance networks

- TROD in children to reduce

o additional examinations

o hospital admissions

o antibiotic therapy
