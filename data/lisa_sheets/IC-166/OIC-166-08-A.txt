=== The decision is shared according to: ===
- Clinical expertise

- General condition

- The patient's preferences and social environment.


Membership of a risk group :

- Less than 6 months

- Over 65s

- Heart failure

- Respiratory failure

- Renal failure

- Diabetic

- Immunodepression

- Sickle cell disease.
<br />

=== Presence of signs of complication: ===
- Pneumonia

- Cardiac signs

- Neurological signs
