The following action can be taken after a report of concern has been sent to CRIP

           - multidisciplinary assessment within 3 months

           - then :

                       - no further information

                       - PMI follow-up proposal, departmental social service follow-up

                       - proposal to set up contractual educational support (home educational assistance, for example)

                       - forwarding to the public prosecutor (judicial alert)


The Public Prosecutor has the power to initiate criminal and civil proceedings:

           - criminal: investigations leading to criminal proceedings or dismissal of the case

           - civil :

                       - urgent need for protection : OPP (see item) and referral to the juvenile court judge to decide whether to continue the placement measure and implement an educational assistance measure.

                       - no urgent need for protection

                                   - referral to the juvenile court judge for educational assistance investigations and, if necessary, implementation of an educational measure

                                   - or transmission to the CRIP

                                   - or no further educational action
