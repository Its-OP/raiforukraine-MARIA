The lesions are often multiple and of different ages. The appearance of the fractures is related to the traumatic mechanism: by direct impact or by traction/torsion with periosteal detachment and metaphyseal tearing.

/Any discrepancy between the reported mechanism and the radiological lesion(s) should raise a red flag.
