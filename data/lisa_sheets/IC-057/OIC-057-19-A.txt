The basic traumatic injuries in physical abuse can be :

- bruises/bruises or wounds of unusual location and/or particularly numerous (> 15) and/or with unclear or incompatible circumstances of onset
[[File:Pantin mb SFPML.jpg|centré|vignette]]


- clean-edged burn (no "splatter")

- skin lesions (bruises, burns, wounds) in "good shape

- Mucosal lesions with no obvious explanation (stomatitis, mouth ulcers)

- fractures in which the circumstances of occurrence are unclear or incompatible (mechanism incompatible with the injury, mechanism incompatible with the child's development)
