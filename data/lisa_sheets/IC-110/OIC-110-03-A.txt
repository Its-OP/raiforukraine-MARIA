'''General rules:'''

As far as possible, adopt regular wake-up and bedtimes.

'''The day: '''

- Get plenty of light during the day, in natural light if possible

- Eat meals on a regular schedule, without neglecting breakfast

- Avoid heavy meals in the evening

- Avoid long naps (> 1 hour) or naps too late (after 4pm).  

- Avoid caffeine, especially after 1pm, alcohol in the evening, and nicotine.  

- Exercise during the day, preferably before 5 p.m.

'''Before bedtime'''

*Limit noise, light and excessive temperature in the bedroom.  
* For children: introduce a positive bedtime ritual lasting no more than 15 minutes (reading a short story, singing a song).

'''Night'''

- Sleeping in bed

- Sleep as much as you need, not more (don't try to get more sleep by staying in bed too long)

- Keep screens (television, telephone, tablet, etc.) out of the bedroom

- In the event of nocturnal awakening :

o Limit light sources,

o Avoid checking the time,

o Don't stay in bed too long without sleeping, plan some quiet activities.
