==Primary prevention
- Eliminating alcohol consumption

- Stop smoking

- HPV vaccination (for boys and girls)

- Controlling occupational exposure <br />

==Secondary prevention==
- Screening for nasosinusal cancer in woodworkers

- Regular dental check-ups (screening for pre-cancerous lesions in the oral cavity)
<br />

==Tertiary prevention==
- Post-therapy follow-up
