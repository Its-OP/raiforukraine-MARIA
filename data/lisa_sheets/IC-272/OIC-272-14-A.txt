Gastritis is an acute or chronic inflammation of the gastric mucosa, defined anatomopathologically on biopsies taken during oeso-gastro-duodenal endoscopy. Gastropathies, which are endoscopic gastric disorders without inflammation of the gastric mucosa, are included in this "entity".
