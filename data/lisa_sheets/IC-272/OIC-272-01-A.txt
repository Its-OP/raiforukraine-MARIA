'''Gastric or duodenal ulcer (UGD) : Loss of substance of the gastric or duodenal wall reaching the muscularis of the wall of the digestive tract.  '''

To be distinguished from erosions, which are limited to the mucosa, and superficial ulcerations, which reach the submucosa but do not extend beyond it.
