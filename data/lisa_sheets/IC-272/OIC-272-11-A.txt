Abdominal contracture is suggestive of peritoneal irritation by perforation (chemical or septic peritonitis). The most urgent examination is an abdominopelvic CT scan (to look for pneumoperitoneum and/or abdominal effusion).

This is a contraindication to endoscopy +++.
