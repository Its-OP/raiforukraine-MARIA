'''Chest CT scans may be useful in the setting of acute IDP in cases of:'''

* Diagnostic doubt
* Poorly defined abnormalities on chest X-ray, which will be analysed in greater detail on CT scan.
* Doubt about associated pulmonary embolism, need for a thoracic angioscan.

'''In the presence of subacute or chronic PID, a thoracic CT scan is systematic'''.
