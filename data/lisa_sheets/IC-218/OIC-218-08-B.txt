'''Visceral lesions related to ENP''' = possible whatever the causative disease and whatever the eosinophil count (= therefore to be screened in any chronic eosinophilia) :

- endomyocardial fibrosis ;

- pulmonary, digestive and skin disorders ;

- central or peripheral neurological damage;

- arterial and venous thrombosis.
