Classical causes of eosinophilia :

- parasitosis ;

- atopy ;

- drug hypersensitivity syndrome ;

- cancer (solid cancer or haematological malignancy).

- viral origin to be systematically considered, in particular human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) infection.
