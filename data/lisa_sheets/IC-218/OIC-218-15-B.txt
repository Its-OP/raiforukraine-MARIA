- Parasitological examination of urine if staying in sub-Saharan Africa;

- Parasitic serology based on clinical and travel findings ;

- If you are travelling to a tropical area: microfilaria search, total IgE assay, filariasis, bilharzia and strongyloidiasis serologies, and ''parasitological examination of stools ([[Prescription and interpretation of a microbiological examination of stools SD-191|prescription and interpretation of a microbiological examination of stools]])'' ;

- Toxocariasis and distomatosis serology, even in the absence of clinical signs and travel to tropical areas;

- According to clinical signs: ANCA, chest X-ray, abdominal ultrasound or thoraco-abdomino-pelvic CT scan;

- For visceral damage: electrocardiogram and echocardiography.
