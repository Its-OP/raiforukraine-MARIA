Blood eosinophilia = '''leukocyte abnormality ([[Interpretation of haemogram SD-223|interpretation of haemogram]])''' corresponding to a number of circulating eosinophilic polymorphonuclear cells (PNE) > 500/mm<sup>3</sup>, and observed on several successive haemograms (persistent nature).

Moderate" eosinophilia: between 500/mm<sup>3</sup> and 1,500/mm<sup>3</sup>.

'''[[Hypereosinophilia SD-219|Hypereosinophilia]]:''' above 1,500/mm<sup>3</sup>.


Trap = percentage of PNEs useless for the diagnosis or follow-up of an eosinophilia (it's the absolute number that counts)
