* Pregnancy and the post-partum period increase the risk of relapse of previous mood disorders or schizophrenia.

*During pregnancy:
**anxiety disorders affect 5 to 15% of women, particularly in the first and last trimesters of pregnancy,
**Characterised depressive episodes account for 10% of cases,
**denial of pregnancy affects around 3 out of every 1,000 pregnancies.
*In the post-partum period:
**post-partum blues are frequent and physiological, affecting up to 80% of women giving birth,
**Characterised depressive episodes affect 15% of women and are often under-diagnosed,
**manic episodes and brief psychotic episodes are less frequent (0.1-0.2% of births).
