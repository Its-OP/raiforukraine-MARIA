- Non-psychiatric medical causes: cerebral thrombophlebitis, retained placenta, infections (e.g. endometritis).

- Psychiatric medical causes : Post-partum blues, [[Suicide and suicide attempts, suicidal ideation 2C-353-PE-A01|intoxication]] by a psychoactive substance (for example, [[Alcohol and pregnancy: mechanism - Dose-effect relationship 2C-027-EP-B07|alcohol]], drugs).
