Any mental disorder related to pregnancy, birth and the child's first year, in the form of decompensation of a psychiatric disorder prior to pregnancy or a first psychiatric episode.

== Main disorders concerned

*Recurrence of previous disorders: [[Bipolar disorders I and II 2C-064-DE-B01|bipolar]], recurrent [[Sad mood/moral pain SD-123|depressive]] disorder, schizophrenia, addictive disorders, [[Anxiety SD-116|anxious]] disorders.
*During pregnancy: anxiety disorders, depressive episode, denial of pregnancy.
*Post-partum psychiatric disorders SD-137|Post-partum disorders]]: post-partum blues (non-pathological), major depressive episode, manic episode, brief psychotic episode, anxiety disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder.
