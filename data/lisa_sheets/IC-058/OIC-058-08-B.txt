==Overall care:==

*Assess and treat the symptom(s)
*look for personal and couple triggers
*Assess the impact on the couple and themselves

==First consultation:==

*Dedicated time and place
*A listening attitude
*Patient and partner
*Empathetic, caring, non-judgemental attitude
*Interviewing the patient and their partner, with open questions:
**History of illness
**Patient profile
**Positive/negative life events
**Patient's/partner's desire for care

*Targeted physical examination of the patient

==Support methods:==

*Identify the precise reason for the consultation (patient and couple)
*Set objectives with the patient and the couple (active involvement).
*Implement a therapeutic strategy:
**Information work and concrete rational explanations
**Cognitive-behavioural and emotional psychotherapy
**Assertiveness techniques
**Pharmacological support if necessary
**Body" approach: often fundamental, based on improved behavioural, sensory and emotional awareness;
**Relationship management: in the form of couple therapy, based on improving communication between partners
**Treatment of general causes as part of a multi-disciplinary approach.
