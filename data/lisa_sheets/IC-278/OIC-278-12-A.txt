Biological work-up :

Total and conjugated bilirubin

ASAT, ALAT, GGT, PAL

CBC - platelets


Imaging work-up

Hepato-biliary ultrasound to look for dilatation of the bile ducts


Other tests will be ordered depending on the clinical context and the results of the first-line tests.
