'''Angioedema''' : '''deep urticaria''' most commonly affecting mucous membranes.

In all cases of superficial and/or deep urticaria, ''look for signs of anaphylaxis'':

- respiratory signs: hypersialorrhoea, dysphonia, dyspnoea, bronchospasm ;

- cardiovascular signs: tachycardia or bradycardia, arterial hypotension ;

- digestive signs: nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea

- Anaphylactic reactions require emergency treatment (items 187 & 332).
