Monocular diplopia persists with occlusion of the healthy eye and disappears with occlusion of the pathological eye. This may be due to :

'''- corneal''' by diffraction of light rays: corneal scarring following keratitis or trauma, keratoconus, significant astigmatism.

'''- irienne :''' traumatic iridodialysis

'''- crystalline:''' nuclear cataract
