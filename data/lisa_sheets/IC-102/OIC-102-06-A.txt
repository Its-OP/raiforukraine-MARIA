'''Paralysis of the III'''

Total paralysis of the Ill causes on the pathological side :

- total ptosis (which may mask diplopia)

- a marked divergence (functional lateral right)

- paralysis in raising and lowering the eye

- loss of accommodation

- areflective mydriasis


'''Paralysis of the IV'''

- Vertical and oblique diplopia, accentuated downwards and inwards (field of the superior oblique muscle), disabling in activities such as reading or descending stairs.

- Compensatory head position: tilted to the healthy side, chin lowered.


'''Paralysis of the VI'''

- Horizontal diplopia

- Convergence of the affected eye

- Abduction deficit

- Compensatory position of the head: turned towards the side of the oculomotor paralysis
