==Questioning is the key to diagnosis ==

*The clinical signs sought are [[Urinary incontinence and voiding disorders in adults and the elderly|lower urinary tract symptoms 125]]
**<u>Storage symptoms</u>: daytime and nocturnal pollakiuria, urgency.
**<Emptying symptoms: dysuria, micturition in several stages, sensation of incomplete bladder emptying, delayed drops.
**These symptoms are grouped together in the IPSS/35 score (0-8: not very symptomatic - 8-19: moderately symptomatic - 20-35: severe symptoms).

*The frequent association of sexual symptoms with BPH or its treatment requires an assessment of sexuality in patients consulting for LUTS.
**Urination disorders and urinary incontinence in adults and the elderly|Erectile dysfunction 125]], [[Menopause, premature ovarian failure, andropause, age-related androgen deficiency|Libido disorder 124]].
**IEEF score 5/15

=='''Clinical examination:'''==

*Abdominal palpation to look for [[Acute retention of urine|globe 347]]
*Examination of the [[Genito-scrotal pathology in boys and men|external genital organs 50]] to look for urethral meatus stenosis, phimosis, etc.].
*A digital rectal examination to look for cancer, pain (prostatitis) and to assess the volume of the prostate.
