'''Axonal polyneuropathies''':

- Their aetiology is metabolic (diabetes, glucose intolerance, renal failure...); '''([[Hyperglycaemia SD-208|hyperglycaemia]]) ([[Increased creatinine SD-199|increased creatinine]])'''''

- Amyloidosis should be suspected in the presence of dysautonomic symptoms;

- The toxic causes are either exogenous (alcohol) '''([[Prevention of alcohol-related risks SD-313|prevention of alcohol-related risks]])''' or medicinal '''([[Suspicion of an adverse drug reaction or an adverse drug reaction SD-348|suspicion of an adverse drug reaction or an adverse drug reaction]])'''' chemotherapy and antiretroviral drugs); cytostatics (in particular vincristine and platinum : neuronopathies); thalidomide, izoniazid, amiodarone, antiretrovirals, disulfiram, chloroquine, metronidazole, etc.).
