The diagnosis is urgent and relies on nerve biopsy. '''([[Preoperative explanation and collection of consent for an invasive diagnostic or therapeutic procedure SD-239|preoperative explanation and collection of consent for an invasive diagnostic or therapeutic procedure]]) ([[Reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic examination SD-178|reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic examination]]) ([[Interpretation of an anatomopathology report SD-180|interpretation of an anatomopathology report]])''''

Treatment of multiple mononeuropathies is urgently required to limit the loss of nerve fibres in the nerve affected by the ischaemia.

Treatment is based on corticosteroids and/or immunosuppressants.
