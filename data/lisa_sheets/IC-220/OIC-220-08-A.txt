Adenopathy is frequently found in children, ''particularly cervical polyadenopathy, particularly in winter, in the context of episodes of rhinopharyngitis''.

The most common causes are infectious.

Fear of a malignant cause or one linked to a systemic disease should, however, dictate a rigorous approach and consultation with specialists, particularly in the case of isolated adenopathy, chronic symptoms or associated general symptoms. Do not hesitate to see your child again in consultation
