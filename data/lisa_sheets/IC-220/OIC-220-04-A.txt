- '''Age'''

- ENT symptoms (nasopharyngitis) in the case of cervical adenopathy

- History and ''lifestyle'' :

o vaccinations, travel,

o '''cancer''',

o smoking

o taking ''medication'' (hydantoins)

o business,

o '''contact/scratching/bite with domestic animals'' (cat, dog...) or wild animals (game),

- Altered general condition (asthenia, anorexia, weight loss)

- '''Fever''' even chills

- '''Tumour syndrome (hepatosplenomegaly, superior cava syndrome suggestive of a large mediastinum)''''

- Night and/or day sweats

- '''Loco-regional signs in the drainage area'' (particularly if isolated adenopathy)

- '''Bone pain'''

- Skin rash

- '''Pruritus'''

- Haemorrhagic signs and anaemic syndrome
