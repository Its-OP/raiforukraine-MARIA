'''1) Infectious causes:'''

- Viral or bacterial ENT infection

- Systemic infection (EBV, CMV, Toxoplasmosis)

- Oral and dental infections

- HSV

- Pyogenic adenitis (staphylococcus and streptococcus)

- Tuberculosis or atypical mycobacteria

- Cat scratch disease

- Pasteurellosis

- Tularemia

-

'''2) Tumour causes:''''

- Leukaemia

- Hodgkin's or non-Hodgkin's lymphoma

- Lymph node metastasis

'''3) Inflammatory causes:''''

- Granulomatosis

- Castelman's disease

- Histiocytosis
