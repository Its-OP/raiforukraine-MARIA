* This is a clinical definition.
* The main symptom is anginal chest pain at rest or on exertion, which has worsened or appeared recently (< 3 weeks).
* The ECG qualifies the coronary syndrome as "ST+" if it shows a permanent ST-segment elevation or "ST-" in all other cases (including normal ECG).
