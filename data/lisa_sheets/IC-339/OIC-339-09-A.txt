* Any chest pain at rest should prompt a call to the SAMU-Centre 15.

<br />

* In ACS, an emergency is defined as the occurrence of one of the following:
** Cardiac arrest
** Shock
** Ventricular rhythm disorder/high-grade conduction disorder
** Acute heart failure
** Persistent chest pain
** Persistent undershift or undershift
