*Otitis externa:'''''
*#treatment with local antibiotic drops and possible meching
*#Beware of ototoxic drops on an open eardrum.
*#Particular risk in diabetics: so-called "malignant" otitis externa: necrotising otitis most often linked to Pseudomonas aeruginosa, which may be complicated by osteitis of the base of the skull; need for prolonged antibiotic treatment<br />.

*Seromucous otitis

treatment: adenoidectomy, placement of transtympanic aerators
