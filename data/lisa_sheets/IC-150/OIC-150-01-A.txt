* Otalgia'': reflex or projected pain resulting from a cervico-facial, temporo-mandibular, buccal or pharyngo-laryngeal disorder, in particular neoplasia, felt in the ear by the patient.
* Otodynia'' : pain linked to a disorder of the outer or middle ear
* '''Different types of otitis:'''
** Otitis externa: inflammation of the external auditory canal
** Other causes of external earache: shingles, earwax, foreign bodies, tumours, etc.
** Acute otitis media: inflammation of the tympanic cavity
** Phlyctenular otitis: presence of phlyctenes on the tympanic membrane.
** Chronic otitis: inflammatory process of the middle ear lasting more than 3 months.
** Reflex earache: due to irritation of the trigeminal, facial, glossopharyngeal, pneumogastric or cervical sympathetic nerve.
