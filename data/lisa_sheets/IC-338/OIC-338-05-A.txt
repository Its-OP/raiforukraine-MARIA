* severity criteria for histamine angioedema: these are the criteria used to define anaphylaxis
* severity criteria for bradykin angioedema :
** involvement above the shoulders (neck, face, ENT area)
** abdominal involvement with pain scored on the Visual Analogue Scale above 5/10
