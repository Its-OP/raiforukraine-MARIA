* Angioedema is localised oedema of the subcutaneous or submucosal tissues: subcutaneous or submucosal swelling that is localised, firm, painful, not erythematous and not pruritic. In the head, it predominates on the eyelids, lips, uvula and tongue.  
* Histaminic angioedema is
** is often associated with urticaria
** is sensitive to anti-histamines and glucocorticoids
** usually resolves within 24 to 48 hours
