*prevention of the biphasic reaction (which occurs 4 to 12 hours after the first symptoms): oral prednisone or IV methylprednisolone
*prevention of recurrence in the event of further exposure to the allergen:
**allergen eviction
**adrenaline auto-injector in the event of anaphylaxis
**desensitisation for certain allergies
