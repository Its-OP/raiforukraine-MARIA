* indicated in case of risk of imminent cardiac arrest, refractory form, severe haemodynamic instability ''in a patient already perfused''.
* dilute 1 mg adrenaline in 10 ml
* IV bolus of 50 µg (0.01 µg/kg) over 1 to 2 minutes
* Continuous intravenous infusion with an electric syringe (0.1 µg/kg/min) may be used as a relay.
