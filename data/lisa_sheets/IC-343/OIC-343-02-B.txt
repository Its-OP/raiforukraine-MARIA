The confusional state (''[[Mental confusion/disorientation SD-119|mental confusion/disorientation]]'') is very common, particularly in people aged over 70 (in this population, its prevalence is between 30 and 40% in hospitalised patients, 50% in post-operative patients and 70% in intensive care patients).
