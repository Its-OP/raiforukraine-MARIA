In the presence of a confusional syndrome '''([[Mental confusion/disorientation SD-119|mental confusion/disorientation]])''', the biological tests '''([[Reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic test SD-178|reasoned request/prescription and choice of a diagnostic test]])''' to be performed as a first-line test are:

- Blood ionogram, urea, total protein, creatinine with calculation of creatinine clearance, blood calcium, capillary blood sugar

- Blood count, CRP

- Urine dipstick (leucocytes, nitrites)
