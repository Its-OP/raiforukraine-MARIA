In more than 50% of cases, acute polyradiculoneuritis is preceded by a respiratory or digestive infection '''([[Interpretation of a SD-236 serology result|interpretation of a serology result]])''' :

Guillain-Barré syndrome: infection with cytomegalovirus, Epstein-Barr virus (demyelinating forms).

For acute axonal motor neuropathy: Campylobacter jejuni diarrhoea.
