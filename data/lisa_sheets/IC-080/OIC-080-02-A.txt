'''Substances and methods used for doping purposes in sport'''

'''The different substances'''

* Products and methods considered to be doping substances are those on a list published annually by the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA).
* Some substances are banned permanently, others only during competitions.
* The full list of banned substances is available on the AFLD website.
* '''The exception to the rule''': the <u>Therapeutic Use Exemption</u> (TUE) procedure.
** Allows athletes to undergo treatment with medication containing a prohibited substance.
* Categorisation of different substances
** Substances banned in and out of competition
** Substances banned in competition
** Substances banned in certain sports

'''The main methods used for doping purposes'''

* Handling blood or blood components
* Chemical and physical manipulation
* Genetic doping
