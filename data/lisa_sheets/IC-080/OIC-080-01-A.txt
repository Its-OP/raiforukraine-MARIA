'''Doping behaviour:''''

Intentional use of products and methods to modify a person's skills in order to overcome an obstacle, real or perceived, and/or to improve physical, intellectual or artistic performance...

'''Doping'''

Behaviour concerning only athletes who, in the context of competitions or events organised by federations, use substances or methods included on a list updated each year by the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA), available at <nowiki>http://list.wada-ama.org/fr/</nowiki>
