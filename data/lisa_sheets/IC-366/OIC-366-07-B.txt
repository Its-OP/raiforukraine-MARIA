'''URGENT CARE OF THE VICTIM +++'''

'''1/ Forensic management:''''

- By forensic medicine

- Samples taken to identify the assailant

- On request and after filing a complaint

'''2/ Management of infectious risk:''''

- Not a forensic doctor, emergency doctor and/or infectiologist

- Date of last period and emergency contraception

- Serological tests (syphilis, HIV HBV HCV) and PCR Chlamydia gonococcus

- PPE/TPE if indicated. HBV sero-vaccination if indicated

- Organisation of serological monitoring
