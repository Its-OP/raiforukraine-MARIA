'''EMERGENCY : Cleaning the wound'''

- not to cause bleeding

- clean immediately with soap and water

- rinsing followed by antisepsis (dakin, chlorine or alcohol solution)

- if mucous spray: rinse for 5 minutes with plenty of saline solution
