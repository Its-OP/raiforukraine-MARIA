Etiological diagnosis is based on the following data

- History :

- notion of trauma: traumatic PNO

- notion of physical effort or Valsalva manoeuvre (effort with closed glottis) : Spontaneous PNO

- In the field

- Long-limbed young man: primary spontaneous PNO

- Pulmonary pathology: emphysema, cavern/excavation (tuberculosis, cancer, etc.) : Secondary spontaneous PNO. Favouring role of tobacco ++.
