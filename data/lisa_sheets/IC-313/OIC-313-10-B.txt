* Testicular abscess
* Orchi-epididymitis
* Leydig cell tumours: 90% benign. Sex steroid secretion (testosterone, estrogen). Infertility, gynaecomastia. Small hypoechoic tumour on ultrasound.
* Dermoid cyst, benign (child, characteristic "onion skin" appearance on ultrasound).
* Pre-pubertal teratoma: benign.
* Non-testicular scrotal tumours:
** Epididymal tumours
** Cord cyst
** Liposarcoma of the cord
