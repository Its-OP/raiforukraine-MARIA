==A/ Testicular examination:==

*Bilateral and comparative
*Description of the mass and characteristics often found:
**Progressive enlargement
**Circumscribed
**stony hardness
**Irregular
**Not painful but tender, with loss of normal testicular sensitivity
**Preservation of the epididymo-testicular groove (Chevassu's sign)
