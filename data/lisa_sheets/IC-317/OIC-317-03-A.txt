- Chronic Myeloid Leukaemia: AEG, gout, priapism, thrombosis, splenomegaly, incidental finding

- Polyglobulie de Vaquez: Erythroderma, [[Acrosyndromes (Raynaud's phenomenon, erythermalgia, acrocyanosis, frostbite, digital ischaemia)|erythermalgia]] (or erythromelalgia), hypertension, headache, tinnitus, vertigo, arterial occlusive event, venous thrombosis, pruritus (triggered by water), splenomegaly, incidental finding.

- Essential thrombocythemia: headache, bleeding, arterial occlusion, venous thrombosis, splenomegaly (rare), chance discovery

- Myelofibrosis: AEG in the foreground, splenomegaly, anaemia, other cytopenias
