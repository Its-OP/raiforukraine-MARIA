Zopiclone and zolpidem have the same potential adverse effects as benzodiazepines, particularly in the elderly.

Like benzodiazepines, they can both lead to "pharmacological tolerance", "use disorders" and "rebound anxiety" when stopped.

For other treatments, please refer to the relevant sections.
