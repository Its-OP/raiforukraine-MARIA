'''Psychotropic drugs''': all chemical substances of natural or artificial origin which have a '''psychological''' effect, i.e. are capable of modifying mental activity.

'''Main classes''' :

* antipsychotics
* antidepressants
* anxiolytics
* hypnotics
* thymoregulators
* psychostimulants.
