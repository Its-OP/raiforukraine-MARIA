'''Bilan biologique''' :

- routine plasma or urine Î²-hCG in all patients of childbearing age

- Interpretation of blood count SD-223|NFS]]

- Martial assessment: [[haemoglobin decreased SD-217]], [[abnormal erythrocyte indices (haemoglobin level, haematocrit, etc.) SD-214]], [[ferritin: decreased or increased SD-207|ferritinaemia]] + CRP (because an inflammatory syndrome may mask hypoferritinaemia);

- Haemostasis work-up (PT, APTT), Willebrand factor assay to test for Willebrand's disease (widely indicated in adolescents).
