Treatment is based on a levonorgestrel IUD (the treatment with the best benefit-risk balance in this indication), systemic hormonal treatment (progestogen, estrogen-progestogen or GnRHa) or surgery (endometrectomy or hysterectomy in the event of failure or refusal; in the absence of a desire for pregnancy).
