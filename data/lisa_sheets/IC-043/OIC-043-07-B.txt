==Medical treatment ==
Initiation of amenorrhoea with ''progestins'' (21 days/month or continuous), ''or GnRH agonists'' (combined with oestroprogestogenic hormone replacement therapy such as add-back therapy, depending on the duration of treatment).

=='''Surgical treatment'''==
If medical treatment fails.

Hysteroscopic resection: submucosal myomas (FIGO 0,1 or 2) of less than 3 or 4 cm.
[[File:FIGO Fibroids.png|vignette| FIGO Fibroid Classification]]
Myomectomy (laparoscopy or laparotomy): conservative treatment if pregnancy is desired for interstitial (FIGO 3 or 4) and subserous (FIGO 5, 6 or 7) myomas.

Hysterectomy, in the absence of a desire for pregnancy, inter-annexal with bilateral salpingectomy or with bilateral adnexectomy depending on the patient's age.
