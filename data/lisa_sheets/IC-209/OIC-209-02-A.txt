* Risk factors
** smoking (> 80%)
** occupational air contaminants (15%)
** domestic pollution (biomass combustion in emerging countries)
** air pollution
** genetic factors (in particular alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency)
**
* Comorbidities (related to smoking, physical inactivity and psychosocial repercussions)
** cardiovascular disease (hypertension, coronary artery disease, heart failure)
** osteoporosis
** anxiety/depression
** undernutrition/obesity
** skeletal muscle dysfunction/deconditioning,
** bronchial cancer
