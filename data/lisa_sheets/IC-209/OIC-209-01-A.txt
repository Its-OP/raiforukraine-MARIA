A common, preventable and treatable chronic respiratory disease defined by

* '''the existence of chronic respiratory symptoms''' (at least one of the following: [[Dyspnoea SD-162|dyspnoea]], [[Cough SD-167|cough]], [[Expectoration SD-163|expectoration]], repeated or nagging lower respiratory infections) '''AND'''''
* '''permanent airway obstruction''' (non-reversible obstructive ventilatory disorder (OVD)).


Chronic bronchitis: productive cough every day or almost every day for at least 3 months of the year and for at least 2 consecutive years.


Emphysema: abnormal and permanent enlargement of the distal airspaces with destruction of the alveolar walls, without associated fibrosis.
