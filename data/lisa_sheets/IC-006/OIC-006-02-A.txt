There are different methods of working in multi-professional teams in outpatient and inpatient settings:

- A multi-professional protocol

- Multidisciplinary consultation meetings (RCP)

- The checklist in the operating theatre or for so-called "interventional" activities (endoscopy, interventional radiology, central venous catheters, chemotherapy, etc.).

- Mortality and morbidity review (RMM)
