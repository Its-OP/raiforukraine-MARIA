'''Other causes of pelvic swelling (apart from uterine fibroids and ovarian cysts):'''

- Advanced pregnancy

- Benign tumour of the uterus (adenomyosis)

- Malignant tumour of the uterus (endometrial cancer, sarcoma)

- Benign adnexal tumour (hydrosalpinx, haematosalpinx, pyosalpinx, endometriosis)

- Adnexal malignancy (cancer of the ovary or fallopian tube)

- Benign or malignant tumour of the colon

- Retroperitoneal tumour (sarcoma, urological cancer, arterial aneurysm)

- Peritoneal carcinosis, ascites
