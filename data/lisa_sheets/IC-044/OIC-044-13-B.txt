Genital prolapse (Prolapsus) is a herniation of the pelvic organs through the vagina.

It may concern :

- Anterior compartment: the bladder (cystocele)

- Middle compartment: the uterus (hysteroptosis)

- Posterior compartment: the recto-uterine (Douglas) cul-de-sac (elytrocele) and the rectum (rectocele).
