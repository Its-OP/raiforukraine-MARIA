'''Occupational health medical file:''''

*Traces the employee's state of health and occupational exposure. Subject to medical confidentiality.

'''Company sheet''' :

*Document on which are recorded in particular
**the ''occupational risks'',
**as well as the occupational physician's opinion on the hazards existing in the company or the constraints linked to the organisation of work
**and the ''recommendations for reducing exposure'' to these occupational risks.
*Transmitted to the employer.


'''Fitness / unfitness form:'''

*Form sent to the employer, possibly accompanied by recommendations on adapting the workstation, but without any information on the employee's pathology.
