Fractures are distinguished according to their anatomical location:

*Damage to the mandibular body, or horizontal branch (toothed portion and angle),
*involvement of the mandibular ramus, or vertical branch ("retro-dentate" portion, condylar and coronoid processes).

In particular, the number and location of fracture lines should be specified
