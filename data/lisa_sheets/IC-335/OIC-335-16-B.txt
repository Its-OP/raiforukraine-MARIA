Compulsory ophthalmological examination due to the risk of reduced visual acuity, diplopia and direct ocular damage. Clinical and medicolegal interest.

Imaging

* standard x-rays, obsolete compared with CT scans (Blondeau, Waters, Hirtz)
* non-injected CT scan of the facial mass, with reconstruction in the 3 planes of space, essential in the event of functional ophthalmological signs and, in practice, accepted as first-line treatment.
