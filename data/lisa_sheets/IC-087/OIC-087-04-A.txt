'''Severe epistaxis:'''

* Blood cell count,
* Pre-transfusion tests (blood group, Rhesus, RAI).

'''When haemostasis disorders are suspected:'''

* PT, APTT, platelets,
* INR (for VKAs),
* Anti-Xa activity (for xabans).
