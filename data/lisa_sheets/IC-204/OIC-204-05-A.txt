Main causes of acute cough in adults :

* Upper respiratory infections+++ (rhinopharyngitis, rhinosinusitis)
* Lower respiratory infections (acute bronchitis, more rarely pneumonia)
* Asthma
* COPD (exacerbation)
* GERD
* Left heart failure (rare)
* Pulmonary embolism (rare)
* Pneumothorax and pleurisy (rare)
