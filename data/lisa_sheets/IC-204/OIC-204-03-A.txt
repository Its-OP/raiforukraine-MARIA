|Title=Look for signs of seriousness in acute or chronic cough.
|Description=None
|Rubric=Emergency identification
|Contributors=
|Order=3}}


* Altered general condition,
* Dyspnoea on exertion
* Haemoptysis
* Infectious syndrome,
* Appearance or modification of cough in a smoker,
* Dysphonia, dysphagia, false routes,
* Suspicious cervical adenopathy,
* Abnormalities in the cardiopulmonary clinical examination.
