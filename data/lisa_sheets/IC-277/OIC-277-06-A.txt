Hepatic colic is a pain :

- brutal, intense,

- continuous, grinding or cramping type of pain

- located in the epigastrium or right hypochondrium,

- radiating towards the right shoulder blade or shoulder.

- Inhibits respiration

- usually lasts from a few minutes to a few hours (<6h).

- There may be inspiratory inhibition, nausea and vomiting.

<On clinical examination, there may be a Murphy's sign: palpation of the right hypochondrium causes pain which inhibits deep inspiration.
