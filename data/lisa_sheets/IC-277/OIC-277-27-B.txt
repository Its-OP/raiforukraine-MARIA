In the absence of angiocholitis or pancreatitis, the 2 possible treatments for unblocking the main bile duct are :

- 1-stage surgery (cholecystectomy with intraoperative clearance of the main bile duct)

- in two endoscopic stages (endoscopic biliary sphincterotomy) followed by cholecystectomy.
