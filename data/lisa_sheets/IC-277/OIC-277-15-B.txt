The 6 main risk factors for cholestatic biliary lithiasis are :

- age

- the female sex

- overweight (obesity)

- pregnancy

- prolonged fasting and parenteral nutrition

- history of gastrectomy or bypass surgery

Certain drugs (oestrogens, fibrates, somatostatin analogues) can also cause cholesterol stones.
