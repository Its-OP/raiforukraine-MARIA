- Blood tests

o Blood ionogram, urea/creatinine

o Liver function tests: AST, ALAT, bilirubin

o Blood count/platelets

o Haemostasis: TP TCA Fibrinogen, D-Dimers

o Haemolysis work-up: haptoglobin, schizocytes, LDH

- Proteinuria: by dipstick on sample and over 24 hours

- Fetal monitoring :

o Cardiotocography for analysis of foetal heart rate

o Obstetrical ultrasound: fetal vitality, fetal biometrics, quantity of amniotic fluid, fetal Doppler indices, uterine Doppler indices

- Depending on the complications :

o Fundus if visual disturbances

o Liver ultrasound if abdominal pain to look for a subcapsular haematoma of the liver

o Cerebral MRI in the event of an abnormal neurological examination
