- Questioning to look for signs of severity: headaches, tinnitus, phosphenes, myodesopsia, consciousness disorders, etc.

- Measurement of blood pressure, heart rate and respiratory rate in the event of dyspnoea

- Osteotendinous reflexes in search of pyramidal irritation

- Foetal vitality: active foetal movements, foetal heart sounds

- Measurement of uterine height

- Uterine contractions
