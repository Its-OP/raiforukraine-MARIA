- Prevalence: 1 to 2% of pregnant women in our countries and up to 10% in developing countries.

- Risk factors :

o age <20 and >35

o obesity

o type 1 or type 2 diabetes

o Chronic hypertension

o Chronic kidney disease

o Antiphospholipid syndrome

o multiple pregnancy

o personal and family history of pre-eclampsia,

o hydatidiform mole

o hydrops fetalis
