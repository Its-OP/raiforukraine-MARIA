These are:

- video game disorder: video games are the basis of addiction. Online multiplayer games are one of the main causes of addiction in vulnerable people.

- sexual addiction (or compulsive sexual behaviour): this relates to online or offline sexual activity, or both. Paraphilias, bipolar disorder and disorder induced by certain medications are excluded diagnoses.

- pathological buying: buying and spending are the root of the problem

- pathological exercise with bigorexia

- Internet addiction (e.g. social networking)
