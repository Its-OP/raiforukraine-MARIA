A number of factors need to be taken into consideration:

#Pharmacological approach: no current marketing authorisation for behavioural addictions, but pharmacological treatment is always indicated in cases of psychiatric or addictive co-morbidities (marketing authorisation for tobacco, alcohol and opioids).

2. Non-pharmacological approach :

- individual psychotherapy (support, motivational interviewing, CBT, systemic, psychodynamic), to treat addiction and comorbidities, reduce impulsivity and compulsivity, manage cravings and improve emotional regulation;

- peer support groups

- expert patients

- Support for family and friends
