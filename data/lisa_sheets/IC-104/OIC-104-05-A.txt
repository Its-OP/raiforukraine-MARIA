|Title=Knowing how to define an attack of the disease
|Description=None
|Rubric=Definition
|Contributors=
|Order=5}}
- Appearance of new symptoms, reappearance of old symptoms or worsening of pre-existing symptoms.

- Subacutely in a few hours to a few days.

- And spontaneously recovering more or less completely.

- Minimum duration = at least 24 hours.

- Fatigue alone, or symptoms occurring in the context of a fever, are not considered a relapse.

- By definition, two attacks must be at least one month apart.
