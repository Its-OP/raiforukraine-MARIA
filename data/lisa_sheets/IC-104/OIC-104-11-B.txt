- If disabling attack = high-dose methylprednisolone (1 g per day), intravenously or by mouth, for 3 days. ([[Prescribe systemic or local corticosteroids SD-251|prescribe systemic or local corticosteroids]])

- Allows faster recovery (anti-inflammatory effect). Has no effect on preventing new attacks.

- If the flare-up is not bothersome = abstention from treatment should be discussed.
