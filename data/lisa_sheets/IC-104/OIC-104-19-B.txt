- Recovery of visual function is usually favourable and complete in 80% of cases at 6 months in MS.

- An Uhthoff phenomenon may be observed, manifesting itself after recovery from optic neuritis as a transient drop in visual acuity lasting a few minutes ([[Vision abnormality SD-138|Vision abnormality]]), occurring with exertion or an increase in body temperature. It is important to explain this to the patient so that they do not see it as a flare-up.
