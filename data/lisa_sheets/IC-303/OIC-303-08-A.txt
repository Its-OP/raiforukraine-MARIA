'''1. Symptoms'''
Gastric tumours are usually asymptomatic.

Symptoms depend on the location of the tumour:

o Cardia (oeso-gastric junction): dysphagia, regurgitation

o Gastric body: pain, particularly epigastric pain, but usually not very symptomatic

o Antre-pylorus: high occlusive syndrome (food vomiting, no water-aerobic levels)

o Digestive haemorrhage irrespective of the site of the tumour


General symptoms of an advanced tumour process :

o Anorexia, asthenia

o Weight loss

o Deep vein thrombosis of the lower or upper limbs

o Left supra-clavicular adenopathy (Troisier lymph node, classic but rare), neoplastic ascites, metastatic hepatomegaly ....
