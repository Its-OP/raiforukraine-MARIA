Depending on the theme, different clinical forms.

* Erotomanic delirium: delusional belief in being loved by another person. More common in women.
* Jealousy: delusional belief that one's partner is unfaithful. More common in men.
* Megalomaniac delirium: Belief that one is gifted with a little-known talent or power, or that one has made an important discovery.
* Persecutory delusions: belief in being the victim of a plot / espionage / conspiracy.
* Somatic delirium: delusions about bodily sensations or functions.

Mixed or undifferentiated forms possible.
