- Hospitalisation in a psychiatric unit, possibly in [[Explain hospitalisation for psychiatric care at the request of a third party SD-240|care without consent]] at the request of the State representative (SDRE) if there is a risk to personal safety.

- Drug treatment: [[Antipsychotic|antipsychotic]] but limited efficacy, antidepressant if emotional involvement.

- Supportive psychotherapy or systematic CBT.
