'''Characterisation in 5 points:'''

* Theme = the subject of the [[Delusional Idea SD-124|Delusional Idea]]. Several themes may exist.
* Mechanism = process on which the construction of delusions is based. 4 possible mechanisms (interpretative, hallucinatory, intuitive and imaginative).
* Systematisation = organisation and coherence of delusional ideas.
* Adhesion = degree of delusional conviction.
* The semiological analysis must also assess the degree of behavioural and emotional impact (risk of acting out auto or hetero aggressively, mood, [[Anxiety SD-116|anxiety,]]). [Suicidal ideation or behaviour/self-inflicted injury SD-125|suicidal ideation]]...).
