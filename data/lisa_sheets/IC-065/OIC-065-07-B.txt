Non-psychiatric :

* neurodegenerative disorders
* intoxication by psychoactive substances: cannabis, L-Dopa, other dopaminergic agonists

Psychiatric: schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorders, mood disorders with psychotic features, personality disorders, OCD.
