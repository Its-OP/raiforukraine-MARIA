Lifetime prevalence: 0.2

Annual incidence: 1 to 3%<sub>0</sub>.

Onset between the ages of 40 and 50

The most common theme of persecution.

Risk factors found: advanced age, lack of stimulation (isolation, sensory deficits), personality disorder, family history of delusional disorders, immigrant background.
