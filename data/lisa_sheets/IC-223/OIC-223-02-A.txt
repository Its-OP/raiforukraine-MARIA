* 3 main types of dyslipidemia:
** pure hypercholesterolaemia
** pure hypertriglyceridemia
** mixed hyperlipidaemia

* Hyperlipidaemia is distinguished according to its cause into primary hyperlipidaemia, secondary hyperlipidaemia (due to disease or medication) and dyslipidaemia associated with type 2 diabetes and metabolic syndrome.
