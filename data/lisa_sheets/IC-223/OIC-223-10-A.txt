*Hypercholesterolaemia:
**daily fat intake <35% of total calorie intake,
**reduce saturated fatty acids (<10%),
**prefer monounsaturated and polyunsaturated omega-3 fatty acids,
**encourage consumption of fruit and vegetables.

*Moderate hyperglyceridemia:
**weight reduction (diet + physical activity),
**reduction (or even elimination) of alcohol,
**reducing carbohydrate intake, particularly simple sugars.

*Severe hyperglyceridemia:
**low-fat diet,
**use of oil based on medium-chain triglycerides.
