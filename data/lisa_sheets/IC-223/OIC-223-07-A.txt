Main causes of secondary hyperlipidaemia :

* Endocrinopathies: hypothyroidism, hypercorticism
* Renal diseases: nephrotic syndrome, glomerulopathy
* Cholestasis
* Medications: corticoids, oestroprogestogens, retinoids, antiretrovirals, neuroleptics, mTOR inhibitors, etc.
