== Persistent complex grief

=== Context of death ===

* sudden, unexpected, violent death or suicide
* the deceased is younger than the bereaved
* deceased spouse or child

=== Characteristics of the bereaved ===

* young age
* female
* close relationship
* repeated bereavement
* history of psychiatric disorder
* poor internal stress management resources
* ideas of guilt
* poor social network
* lack of understanding of the situation
* absence of mourning rituals

== Concomitant psychiatric disorders ==
The risk factors for psychiatric disorders are the same whether or not they occur in the context of bereavement (see corresponding items).
