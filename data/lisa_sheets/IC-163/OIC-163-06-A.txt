The aim of the treatment is to :

* Reduce contagiousness
* Shorten symptoms (only possible if administered early in the catarrhal phase. Treatment has no effect on symptoms if prescribed during the coughing phase.

'''In first intention = Macrolides'''

* Clarithromycin 7 days
* Azithromycin 3 days

Alternative if macrolides contraindicated = cotrimoxazole 14 days

Note: beta-lactams are ineffective.

Special information for pregnant women: azithromycin and clarithromycin can be used during pregnancy at any stage, and during breast-feeding.
