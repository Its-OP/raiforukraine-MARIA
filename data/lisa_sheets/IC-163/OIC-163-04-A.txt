Etiologies of subacute non-febrile cough:

* Infectious cough following viral or bacterial infection with Mycoplasma pneumoniae or Chlamydia pneumoniae, influenza, tuberculosis, chronic sinusitis with posterior rhinorrhoea.
* Pneumo-allergological cough: allergy, asthma, diffuse interstitial lung disease
* Mechanical cough: gastro-oesophageal reflux, tumour, tracheal compression, foreign body (elderly)
* Iatrogenic cough: angiotensin-converting enzyme inhibitors, beta-blockers.
* Psychogenic cough
