* Notification of cluster cases (≥ 2) occurring in communities (schools, boarding schools, crèches, etc.) to the ARS

* ''Notification of nosocomial cases'' in maternity wards, hospitals and nursing homes: declaration to the ARS and to the CLIN (nosocomial infection control committee) of the establishment.
