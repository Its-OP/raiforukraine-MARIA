'''Definitions of childbirth'''.
{| class="wikitable"
|'''Childbirth'''
|All the phenomena that lead to the expulsion of the foetus and its appendages (placenta, amniotic fluid and membranes).
|-
|'''Eutoctal or normal delivery''''
|A delivery which, through the influence of natural phenomena alone, results in the expulsion of the child by vaginal delivery.
|-
|'''Dystocic childbirth'''
|This is a delivery which leads to difficulties or impossibility of delivery by vaginal route.
|-
|Premature delivery
|A delivery that occurs before 37 weeks' gestation.
|}
