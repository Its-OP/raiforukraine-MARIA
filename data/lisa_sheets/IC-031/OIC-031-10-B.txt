There are 4 main varieties of commitment position (OIGA, OIDP, OIGP, OIDA) and 2 accessory varieties (OIGT, OIDT).

Definition of presentations.

The "presentation" is the part of the foetus that makes contact with the DS, engages it and moves into the pelvic cavity until the foetus is expelled.

The ''varieties'': OP (occipito-pubic), OIGA (occipito-iliac left anterior), OIGT (occipito-iliac left transverse), OIGP (occipito-iliac left posterior), OS (occipito-sacral), OIDP (occipito-iliac right posterior), OIDT (occipito-iliac right transverse) and OIDA (occipito-iliac right anterior).
