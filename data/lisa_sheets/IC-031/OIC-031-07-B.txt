'''The 3 stages of work.'''
{| class="wikitable"
|'''1st stage of labour'''
|From the onset of actual labour to the end of cervical dilation (cervical dilation).
|-
|''2<sup>th</sup> stage of labour'''
|From the end of cervical dilatation to the birth of the baby (descent of presentation and expulsion).
|-
|'''3<sup>e</sup> stage of labour''''
|from birth of child to expulsion of placenta (delivery)
|}
The woman and newborn are then monitored for 2 hours after birth.
