It is the '''second leading cause of cancer'', all sexes combined, with around '''49,500''' estimated new cases and the '''leading cause of cancer deaths'' with around '''31,000 deaths'' in mainland France in 2017.

'''In men''', lung cancer is the '''2nd<sup>nd</sup> most common cancer''' with an estimated 32,500 new cases in 2017.

It is the ''1<sup>re</sup> cause of death from cancer'' with around 21,000 deaths in 2017.

'''In women''', lung cancer is the '''3rd<sup>th</sup> most common cancer''' with an estimated 17,000 new cases in 2017. It is the '''2<sup>th</sup> cause of death''' '''from cancer''' with around 10,000 deaths in 2017.
