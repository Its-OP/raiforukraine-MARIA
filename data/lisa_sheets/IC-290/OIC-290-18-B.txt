|Title=Knowing the risk factors for bronchial cancers and mesothelioma in the workplace

|Rubric=Etiologies

Tobacco (including passive), radiation, occupational exposure to certain chemical substances (asbestos), outdoor air pollution, personal or family history of lung cancer, personal history of lung disease.

For mesothelioma, the only recognised occupational risk factor is asbestos.
