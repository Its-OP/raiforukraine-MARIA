40% of cancers can be prevented through changes in behaviour and lifestyle.

The main risk factors are

- tobacco (19.8%)

- alcohol (8%)

- unbalanced diet (5.4%)

- overweight (5.4%)

- certain infections: papilloma virus, helicobacter pylori, hepatitis B and C (4%)

- occupational exposure (3.6%)

- UV radiation (3%)

- ionising radiation (1.8%)1
