- The prevalence was 59,000 women in France in 2017. Breast cancer is the 2<sup>th</sup> cancer in terms of frequency (the 1<sup>rst</sup> in women) and the 3<sup>th</sup> in terms of mortality (the 1<sup>rst</sup> in women).

- The incidence rate is 94.7 per 100,000 women.

- It was responsible for 12,000 deaths in France in 2017. The mortality rate is 14.6 per 100,000 women.
