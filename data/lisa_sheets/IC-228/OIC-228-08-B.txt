'''Necrotic angiodermatitis'''

The diagnosis is made in the presence of an ulcer:

*of the anterolateral aspect of the leg;
*with areas of superficial necrosis ;
*with irregular margins
*with a cyanotic and livedoid peri-ulcer halo;
*very painful, sleepless.

We are looking for :

*high blood pressure, diabetes;
*pulses are present (there is no arteriopathy in pure angiodermatitis).
