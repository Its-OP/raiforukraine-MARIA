Distal or ankle pressures may be falsely normal or elevated in cases of mediacalcosis (diabetes, renal failure, advanced age, etc.). In these situations, it is essential to measure toe pressure.

The measurement of haemodynamic criteria is essential for the detection of OAMI at the stage of critical ischaemia:

* ankle pressure < 50 mmHg ;
* toe pressure < 30 mmHg;
* TcPO2 < 30 mmHg.
