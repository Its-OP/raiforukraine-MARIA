Definitions :

*Healthcare users = people who use or are likely to have to use healthcare services.
*Trusted support person = designated by the user to accompany them and express their wishes if they are unable to do so themselves.
*Expert patient = a patient with a chronic illness who has developed sound knowledge of his or her illness, as well as expertise in the day-to-day management of his or her condition.

Involving the patient and his or her support person in the care process.

The involvement of the people concerned refers to any form of action, individual or collective, for the benefit of their health, well-being or quality of life, or those of their peers.

Support for the involvement of the people concerned (patients, carers, users) by decision-makers and managers recommended by the HAS.

Approved health system user associations (list available on ''solidarites-sante.gouv''), patient associations to support users.

Development of therapeutic patient education programmes, involvement of expert patients.
