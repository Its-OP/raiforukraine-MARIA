Definitions :

- Health = "a state of complete physical, mental and social well-being".

- Functioning = includes all the dimensions of organ/anatomy, activity and participation. Dysfunction is all the consequences of an alteration in health status (organic, anatomical, activity, participation) in daily life.

- Disability = limitation of activity + restriction of social participation ([[Disability situation SDD-345|Disability situation]])
