- Activity limitation : Assessment by interview and clinical examination, using generic and/or specific scales (Rankin Scale, Barthel Index, Functional Independence Measure, IADL, AGGIR grid) ([[Progressive loss of autonomy SDD-031|31-progressive loss of autonomy]], [[Appearance of difficulty walking SDD-066|66-appearance of difficulty walking]], [[Management of a patient in prolonged decubitus SDD-276|279-management of a patient in prolonged decubitus]], [[Identify the consequences of a pathology/situation on maintaining a job SDD-316|316-identify the consequences of a pathology/situation on maintaining a job]] and using instrumental analysis. - Restriction of participation = social impact of the disease ([[Situation of disability SDD-345|345-Situation of disability]]). Assessment mainly through questioning.

- Assessment of quality of life using questionnaires and generic or specific scales.
