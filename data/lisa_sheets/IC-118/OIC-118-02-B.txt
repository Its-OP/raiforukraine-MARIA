Law of 11 February 2005 "for equal rights and opportunities, participation and citizenship for people with disabilities":

- Introduction of the right to compensation for the disabled.  

- Drawing up a compensation plan with the Disability Compensation Scheme (PCH)

- The Caisse nationale de la solidarité pour l'autonomie (CNSA), at national level, and the Maison Départementale des Personnes Handicapées (MDPH), at local level, assess and establish the PCH.
