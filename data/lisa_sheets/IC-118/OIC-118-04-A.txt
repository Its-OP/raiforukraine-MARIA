Maison Départementale des Personnes Handicapées (MDPH): reception, information, support and advice for people under 60.


Commission des droits et de l'autonomie des personnes handicapées (CDAPH) :

- Belongs to the MDPH

- Makes decisions on the allocation of rights based on the personalised disability compensation plan (PPC)


Assistance awarded by the MDPH :

- Disabled Adult Allowance (AAH)

- Prestation de compensation du handicap (PCH) (disability compensation allowance): funding for human and/or technical assistance.

- Inclusion mobility card (CMI)


Human assistance :

- Nurse / nursing auxiliary (nursing care),

- Third party (home help or family carer),

- Domestic help with meal delivery,

- Hospitalisation at home.


Carer= a person who provides non-professional help, in whole or in part, to a dependent person in his or her family, for daily activities. This regular assistance may be provided on a permanent or non-permanent basis. This help can take several forms
