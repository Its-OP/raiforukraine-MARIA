'''Antidepressants (tricyclics, serotonin reuptake inhibitors with or without action on noradrenaline) and psychotropic drugs'''

No superior efficacy to placebo.

Undesirable effects can amplify symptoms.

Antidepressants may be useful in treating co-morbid anxiety and depression.

'''So-called natural products (phytotherapy, etc.)''''

No superior efficacy to placebo.
