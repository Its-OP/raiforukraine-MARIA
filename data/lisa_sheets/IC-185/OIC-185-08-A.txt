INFECTIONS (ACUTE AND CHRONIC)

* Sepsis, pneumonia, acute pyelonephritis, acute prostatitis, meningitis, encephalitis, spondylodiscitis, arthritis, etc.
* Osler's disease, tuberculosis, cold abscess, sigmoidal diverticulitis, Whipple's disease, Castleman's disease...

NEOPLASIA AND HEMATOLOGICAL DISEASES

* Kidney cancer, colon cancer, prostate cancer...
* Non-Hodgkin's lymphoma, Hodgkin's disease...

INFLAMMATORY AND AUTOIMMUNE DISEASES

* Rheumatoid arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, Still's disease, myositis and dermatomyositis, systemic lupus erythematosus, vasculitides (giant cell arteritis, periarteritis nodosa, micropolyangiitis, GPA, EGPA, Beh√ßet), polychondritis, Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis, retroperitoneal fibrosis, chronic aortic dissection, etc.
* Genetic auto-inflammatory diseases: familial Mediterranean fever, cryopyrinopathies, TNF receptor-associated periodic syndromes ("TRAPS"), etc.
