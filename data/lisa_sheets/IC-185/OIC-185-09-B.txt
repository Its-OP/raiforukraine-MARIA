In the event of inconsistent changes in inflammatory markers.

-Isolated elevated VS: due to anaemia or monoclonal or polyclonal hypergammaglobulinaemia

-Inflammatory syndrome with dissociated elevation of inflammatory proteins

* Low haptoglobin: intravascular haemolysis
* Low complement: diseases with circulating immune complexes (Lupus, endocarditis)
* Low ferritinemia: associated martial deficiency
* Low fibrinogen: disseminated intravascular coagulation


Severe hepatic impairment disrupting acute-phase protein synthesis
