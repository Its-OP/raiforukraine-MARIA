Acute appendicitis is the most frequent abdominal surgical emergency (7% of the population, including 20% of complicated appendicitis).

Appendicitis has a low mortality rate:

- 0.1% in uncomplicated forms

- 1.5% in case of peritonitis

Morbidity is 10%:

- surgical site infection (SSI)

- parietal infection

- deep infection

- haemorrhage

- acute small bowel obstruction

- appendicitis on appendix stump.
