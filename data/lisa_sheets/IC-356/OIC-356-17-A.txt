The deadline must be short:

- less than 4 hours in forms with severe clinical signs or in fragile patients

- less than 24 hours in the absence of serious clinical signs (HAS)

Treatment of uncomplicated forms :

- urgent surgical treatment by appendectomy (Mac Burney or laparoscopy)

- one dose of intraoperative antibiotic prophylaxis is often sufficient

Option under evaluation: medical treatment alone with amoxicillin-clavulanic acid antibiotics (not currently recommended).
