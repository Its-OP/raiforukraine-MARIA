The deadline must be short:

- less than 4 hours in forms with severe clinical signs or in fragile patients

- less than 24 hours in the absence of serious clinical signs (HAS)

Treatment of uncomplicated forms :

- urgent surgical treatment by appendectomy (Mac Burney or laparoscopy)

- one dose of intraoperative antibiotic prophylaxis is often sufficient

- option under evaluation: medical treatment alone with antibiotics

Treatment of an abscessed form :

- percutaneous radiological drainage by ultrasound or CT scan

- 7-day antibiotic treatment

- appendectomy at 3 months

Treatment of a breastplate :

- 7-day antibiotic treatment

- appendectomy at 3 months

Treatment of peritonitis :

- very urgent surgical treatment with appendectomy, peritoneal cleansing and drainage of the peritoneal cavity (laparoscopy or laparotomy)

- antibiotic therapy 5 days
