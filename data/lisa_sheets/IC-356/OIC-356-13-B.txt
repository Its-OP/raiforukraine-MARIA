Clinical variability in the elderly :

- Symptoms of acute appendicitis are less obvious, delaying diagnosis

- The evolution is more torpid

- Ileus is often present

- Differential diagnosis of cancer

- No more complicated or delayed forms

- In polypathological patients, the prognosis is worse.
