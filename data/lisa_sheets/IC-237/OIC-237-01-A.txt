Palpitations'' are a subjective disorder corresponding to :

*a sensation of ''abnormal perception of heartbeats'', normal or abnormal, described as ''the heart struggling'' in the chest;
*a sensation that the heart is beating too hard (hyperkinetic palpitation), or too fast or irregularly (rhythmic palpitation, ''tachycardia'')

They can be felt laterocervically along the carotid arteries.
