'''<big>- Secondary vasculitis:</big>'''''

* systemic lupus or rheumatoid arthritis
* infection
* medication
* haemopathies and solid cancers
* toxic substances (tobacco, cannabis, cocaine)
* cholesterol emboli.

'''<big>- Other differential diagnoses (variable depending on the type of vasculitis):</big>''''

* bronchial cancer or metastases in the presence of one or more pulmonary nodules (GPA)
* compressive or diabetic-related neurological damage in the presence of multiple mononeuropathy
* other causes of glomerulonephritis with glomerular proteinuria and/or haematuria SDD-102|''haematuria''']].
