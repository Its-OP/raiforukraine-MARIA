Vascular ''[[Purpura/ecchymosis/haematoma SDD-089|Purpura]]''' = erythematous skin lesion which does not fade on in vitro pressure, due to inflammation of the vascular wall.

Clinical features: infiltrated, confluent, sometimes necrotic, horizontal, predominantly in the lower limbs, without mucosal involvement.
