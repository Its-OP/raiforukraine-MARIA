The three main causes of salivary gland pathologies are :

- Sialitis: inflammatory diseases of the gland (sialadenitis) and/or the excretory canal (sialodochitis),

- tumours: localized benign or malignant proliferation of the gland,

- sialosis: non-infectious diffuse pathology of the parenchyma.
