The main complications of sinusitis are :

Ø Orbital (acute ethmoiditis): orbital or periorbital cellulitis, phlegmon, subperiosteal abscess, optic neuritis.

'''''Warning signs: palpebral oedema, exophthalmos, ocular motor disorders, reduced visual acuity'''''

Ø Neuromeningeal (any sinus involvement): meningitis (pneumococcus), thrombophlebitis of the [[Anatomy:Cavernous sinus|cavernous sinus]] or superior longitudinal sinus, cerebral abscess, subdural empyema, etc.

'''''Warning signs: meningeal syndrome, consciousness disorders'''''
