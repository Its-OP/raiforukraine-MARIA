Involvement of the neuromuscular or muscular junction is suggested when :

- fluctuating pure motor impairment, particularly in the case of neuromuscular junction impairment (no sensory or vesicosphincter disorders);

- a predominantly proximal deficit;

- no abnormalities in tendon or plantar skin reflexes.
