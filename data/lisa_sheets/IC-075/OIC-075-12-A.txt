* There is no threshold below which smoking represents no risk.
* The duration of intoxication is the most important risk factor
** doubling the dose => cancer risk x 2
** doubling the duration => cancer risk x 23)
* Tobacco responsible for 25% of all cancers
** 80% of deaths from bronchopulmonary cancers are linked to smoking
* Other tobacco-related cancers
** upper aerodigestive tract
** pancreas
** kidney
** bladder
* Passive smoking increases the relative risk of bronchopulmonary cancer by 26%.
