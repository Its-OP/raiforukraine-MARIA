Recent trends in smoking :

* In 2017 and again in 2018, the proportion of smokers fell by 12% after several years of stability.
* 11 million smokers in 2018

There are many contributing factors, including

* The price has risen sharply,
* the neutral packet
* or the "No Smoking Month" operation.

Daily and occasional smoking: (among 18-75 year olds in 2018)

* 32% smokers, including 25.4% daily smokers and 6.6% occasional smokers
