*The average age at which the first cigarette is smoked is stable at 14, with daily smoking at 15.
*30.4% of people (aged 18 to 75) say they smoke tobacco.
**men 34.6%
**women 26.5%
*Tobacco intoxication is responsible for one in ten deaths worldwide.
*In France: 1<sup>rst</sup> cause of avoidable death = 75,000 deaths/year
*The <u>duration of intoxication</u> is the <u>most important risk factor</u>.
**doubling the dose => risk of cancer x 2
**doubling the duration => cancer risk x 23)
*''49% of regular smokers show signs of dependence (rank B)''
