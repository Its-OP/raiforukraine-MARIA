Certain situations require an urgent nephrological opinion in order to discuss the interest of a '''[[renal biopsy]]''', in particular:

*Rapidly progressive glomerulonephritis syndrome" ("haematuria", "proteinuria", "renal failure") often associated with extra-renal signs (vasculitis, lupus, etc.),
*Myeloma" responsible for renal failure,
*haemolytic uraemic syndrome.
*Acute interstitial nephritis (drugs, sarcoidosis, etc.).
