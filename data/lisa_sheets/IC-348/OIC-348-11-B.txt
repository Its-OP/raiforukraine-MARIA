'''Vascular nephropathies (cf item 263)''' associated with damage to small-calibre arteries and capillaries in :

* Haemolytic uraemic syndrome (HUS)
* Scleroderma renal crisis
* Cholesterol crystal embolism disease
* Malignant hypertension

Or linked to damage to the main renal arteries or veins:

* Arterial embolisms, atheromatous arterial thrombosis, renal artery dissection or venous thrombosis (bilateral or in a single kidney).
