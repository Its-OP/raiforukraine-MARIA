ARF affects 25% of hospitalised patients, i.e. more than 4 million patients in France, with less than 1% being treated in intensive care or intensive care units. The most frequent causes of ARF are functional ARF, certain obstructive ARF, acute tubular necrosis (ATN) and drug-induced ARF.
