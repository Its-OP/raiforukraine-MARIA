'''A history of IE''' defines a '''high risk of IE recurrence''' ([[Know the situations at risk of infective endocarditis (group A cardiopathy, presence of intra-cardiac material, gram-positive cocci bacteremia) 2C-152-PE-A01|group A cardiopathy]]). '''Prevention education''' of this risk is essential.

There are a number of objectives:

<u>A- Preventing the formation of a gateway:</u>

*Dental:
**'''Maintaining good oral health''''
**Dental consultation 2 times a year'''
*Skin :
**'''Skin hygiene''' (disinfection of wounds and appropriate care to obtain rapid healing)
**'''Avoid all mucocutaneous invasions'': piercing, tattooing, acupuncture, etc.
**'''Management of intravenous drug addiction'''
**Limiting invasive procedures,'' particularly endovascular procedures (catheters, central venous lines, etc.)
**Limit the use of peripheral venous lines''. If necessary, apply the [[Know the rules for preventing catheter-related infections 2C-004-PC-B11|measures for preventing catheter-related infections]].

<u>B- Regular cardiological monitoring</u>.

<u>C- What to do in case of fever</u>.

*'''Consult a doctor if you have a fever'''
*'''Investigate any fever with no obvious cause:'''
**Take blood cultures.
**Do not administer antibiotics indiscriminately.
