Acute adrenal insufficiency can be a sign of the disease, in particular:

*In the neonatal period: complete adrenal enzyme block (21-hydroxylase +++).
*In adulthood
**Addison's disease
**bilateral adrenal haemorrhage
**pituitary apoplexy.
