'''Clinical :'''

* extracellular dehydration with skin wrinkling, hypotension ''([[Discovery of arterial hypotension SD-043]]),'' up to and including collapse ;
* confusion, convulsive seizures secondary to hyponatraemia and hypoglycaemia ''([[Hypoglycaemia SD-209]]])'', even coma;
* Digestive disorders: anorexia, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, diarrhoea;
* diffuse pain,
* fever, which may be caused by an infection that precipitated the decompensation.


'''Biology:'''

* haemoconcentration*, functional renal failure* (+++);
* hyponatremia ([[Dysnatremia SD-202]])'', hyperkalaemia* ([[Dyskalaemia SD-201]])'' (aldosterone deficiency) (+++);
* hypoglycaemia ''([[Hypoglycaemia SD-209]]]'')''
* metabolic acidosis ;
* anaemia, hyperlymphocytosis, hypereosinophilia;
* preserved natriuresis;
