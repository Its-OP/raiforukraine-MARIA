Any intercurrent pathology (vomiting, diarrhoea, infection, fracture, myocardial infarction, surgery, anaesthesia, invasive diagnostic procedure, major physical effort, intense psychological stress, etc.) is a cause of decompensation of treated adrenal insufficiency.

And the patient's failure to adapt hydrocortisone treatment sufficiently to these situations: Importance of therapeutic education in adrenal insufficiency.
