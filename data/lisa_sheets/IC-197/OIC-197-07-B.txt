Several classification criteria have been proposed:

- Amor criteria

- ESSG criteria

- ASAS criteria (the most recent and most widely used) classifying spondyloarthritis as axial (in cases of spinal pain lasting more than 3 months and starting before the age of 45; Figure 1) or peripheral (Figure 2).
