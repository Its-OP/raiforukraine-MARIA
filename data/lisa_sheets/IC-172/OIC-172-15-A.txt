'''Giardiosis:''' Nitro-imidazoles

Example: Metronidazole orally for 5 days

'''Oxyurosis'''

2 courses of anthelmintic 2-3 weeks apart

* Flubendazole for 1<sup>rst</sup> intention
* Albendazole

To avoid recurrences, it is essential to combine preventive measures

* treatment of the whole family (2 courses)
* wash hands before every meal and after every trip to the toilet
* keep fingernails short
* do not put your hands to your mouth
* wear pyjamas
* clothes and blankets washed at >60°C
* change all bedding on the day of treatment and wash at 60°C
* vacuum around the bed

Ascaridiosis

* Flubendazole
* Albendazole as an alternative

'''Taeniasis'''

Praziquantel: a single course of treatment
