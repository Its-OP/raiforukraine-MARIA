- Follow-up, where appropriate, of improvement in clinical and imaging signs

- Parasitological examination of control stools, 1 month after the end of treatment (to be repeated)

- Taenia saginata: no rings emitted within 3 months of treatment

- Blood count check: normalisation of eosinophilia, if necessary
