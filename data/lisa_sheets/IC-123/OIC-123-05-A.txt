- Average lifespan: biologically programmed lifespan under ideal conditions

- Maximum service life: maximum observed service life

- Life expectancy: average number of years of life for a given age group (life expectancy at birth or life expectancy at a given age, which corresponds to the average number of years remaining to live beyond that age).

- Disability-free life expectancy: average number of years of disability-free life
