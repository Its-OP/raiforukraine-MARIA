There is no consensual definition.

You are an elderly person:

* From the age of 60 for social benefits in France;
* From the age of 65 for the World Health Organisation;
* 75 and over for the risk of loss of autonomy and frailty;
* 75 and over to enter a geriatric service, with an average age of > 80.
