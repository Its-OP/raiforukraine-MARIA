Ageing:

* results in a reduction in the body's functional reserve capacity, making it vulnerable to aggressive situations.
* varies greatly from one organ to another (inter-organ variations).
* varies greatly from one person to another of equal age (inter-individual variations).

1- vascular arterial stiffness leading to systolic hypertension

2- cardiac myocardial relaxation disorder and left ventricular hypertrophy impairing ventricular filling

3- brain attention disorders

4- sarcopenia muscle

5- Nephron reduction kidney

6- neuro-sensory presbycusis, presbyopia
