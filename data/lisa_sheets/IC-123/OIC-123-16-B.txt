|Rubric=Pathophysiological elements
|Contributors=
|Order=16}}
Cardiovascular: arterial stiffness, myocardial relaxation disorder, stable cardiac output (at rest)

Sensory: cataracts, presbyopia, presbycusis

Neurological: reduced attention span, altered sleep patterns, reduced sensation of thirst

Pulmonary: reduced ventilatory capacity

Digestive: slower intestinal transit, reduced salivary secretion

Nephrological: nephron reduction and reduced capacity to adapt in the event of water or salt loss, changes in the ability to dilute or concentrate urine.

Metabolic: reduction in lean body mass and increase in fat mass

Immune: prolonged pro-inflammatory state at the time of an infectious episode, poorer vaccine response
