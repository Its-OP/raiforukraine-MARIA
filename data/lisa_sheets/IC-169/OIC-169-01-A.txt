- 150,000 people living with HIV in France in 2018, around 6,000 new infections/year

- Main modes of contamination: heterosexual intercourse (56%), homosexual male intercourse (40%), IV drug use (2%).

.      Notion of at-risk groups: patients from highly endemic countries, men who have sex with men (MSM), intravenous drug users, etc.

- Decline in the number of new HIV diagnoses per year in all risk groups between 2013 and 2018, except for MSM (especially foreign-born MSM)

- 29% of people are diagnosed at an advanced stage of the disease (AIDS or LT CD4 < 200/mm3), a rate that has unfortunately remained stable over the years.
