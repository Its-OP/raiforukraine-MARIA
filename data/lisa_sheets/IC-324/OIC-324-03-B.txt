== Evaluation of the impact of therapeutic education

* behavioural: acquisition of self-care and safety skills
* cognitive: improving erroneous beliefs, prejudices and knowledge
* on a medical level: improvement in disease-related parameters (HbA1c in diabetes, for example, or attacks of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease).
