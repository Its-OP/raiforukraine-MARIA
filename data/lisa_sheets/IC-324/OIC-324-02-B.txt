== '''ETP programmes'''==
'''1. '''Therapeutic patient education programmes (can be offered for all chronic illnesses).

'''2.  Support services: assistance and support for patients and their families (e.g. Alzheimer's disease).

'''3.  Learning programmes: technical gestures for using a medicine or technique (e.g. insulin pump, inhalation chamber for asthma).

== '''The stages involved in setting up an ETP programme''' ==
'''1. '''Drawing up an educational diagnosis: patients' skills and expectations

'''2.  '''Define a personalised therapeutic patient education programme

'''3.  '''Plan and implement group and/or individual therapeutic patient education sessions

'''4.  '''Carry out an individual assessment: skills obtained, disease management
