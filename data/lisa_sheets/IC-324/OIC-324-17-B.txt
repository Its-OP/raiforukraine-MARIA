== Self-medication drugs must meet certain conditions set by the ANSM ==

* The risk-benefit ratio must be highly favourable.
* The targeted pathology must be common and benign.
* The packaging, dosage, labelling and package leaflet must be appropriate for self-medication (short-term).
* The patient must be warned of the need to consult a doctor if symptoms persist.

<br />

== Certain candidate drugs are not eligible for safety reasons ==

* Where there are major contraindications or a significant risk of drug interactions,
* Medicines intended for paediatric use that are not safe enough for self-medication.
