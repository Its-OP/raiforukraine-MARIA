'''Trans-cranial Doppler:''' at initial management, as part of the FAST ultrasound: search for signs of intracranial hypertension (Figure 3, [[2C-334-PC-B02]]).

'''Brain scan:''''

*initially without injection, then with injection of the polygon of Willis and supra-aortic trunks (to look for carotid or vertebral dissection)
*systematically combined with a CT scan of the cervical spine
*to be repeated if initial brain scan very early, if clinical or para-clinical worsening (trans-cranial doppler) or if usual treatment with anticoagulants.
