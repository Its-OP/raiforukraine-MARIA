* Short-term complications :
** multi-visceral failure syndrome
** infectious complications: open fracture, perforation of hollow organs, inhalation syndrome
** bleeding complications
** thromboembolic complications
** death: brain death, multivisceral failure syndrome

* Medium-term complications :
** complications of decubitus: bedsores, vicious attitudes
** infectious complications: nosocomial infections
** undernutrition, sarcopenia

* Long-term complications :
** somatic sequelae: functional physical disability
** psychological sequelae: anxiety-depression syndromes, post-traumatic stress disorder
