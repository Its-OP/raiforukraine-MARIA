'''Locoregional complications:'''

* nerve lesions (particularly fractures of the middle third of the humerus, which can damage the radial nerve)
* vascular lesions: in addition to the clinical examination: Doppler, injected CT scan
* compartment syndrome -> pressure in all the aponeurotic compartments

'''Systemic complications:''''

* fat embolism -> Gurd's triad, fundus
* acute post-traumatic rhabdomyolysis -> CPK, myoglobin, blood and urine ionogram, urine sediment
