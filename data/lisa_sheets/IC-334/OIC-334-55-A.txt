'''Anamnestic criteria:'''

*aggravating factors: taking treatments that modify blood crase, alcohol and/or psychotropic drugs
*initial prolonged loss of consciousness > 5 min

'''Clinical criteria:''''

*initial or secondary neurological deficit
*focal signs
*pupillary anomaly
*initial Glasgow score < 14
*presence of extra-cranial lesions

Application of the ''Vittel algorithm'' to severe trauma (Figure 1, [[2C-334-DP-A02]])
