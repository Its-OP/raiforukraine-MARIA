*'''History:''' chest pain ('''[[Chest pain SD-161|chest pain]]'''''), dyspnoea
*Signs of acute respiratory distress ('''[[Acute respiratory distress SD-160|acute respiratory distress]]'''''), signs of shock ('''[[Discovery of arterial hypotension SD-043|discovery of arterial hypotension]]''''')
*'''Inspection:''''Belt sign'', parietal wounds and contusions, costal flap, haemoptysis
*Palpation: fractures, subcutaneous emphysema
*Auscultation: auscultatory asymmetry, crackling rales ("''[[Discovery of anomalies on pulmonary auscultation SDD-020|discovery of anomalies on pulmonary auscultation]]''')
