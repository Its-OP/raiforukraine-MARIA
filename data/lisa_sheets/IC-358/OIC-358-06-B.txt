Transient biological abnormalities are suggestive of a biliary origin (lithiasis migrating into the main bile duct):

* an early peak in transaminases (ASAT and ALAT), sometimes high (often >10N),
* often associated with an increase in cholestasis markers: PAL, GGT and bilirubin.
