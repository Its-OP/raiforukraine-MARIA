'''Definition:''' acute inflammation of the pancreas combining interstitial oedema and cytosteatonecrosis (necrosis of the pancreas and surrounding tissues).

'''Mechanism:''' pancreatic autodigestion by proteolytic pancreatic enzymes.

'''Two forms:'''

* benign oedematous acute pancreatitis (AP) (80% of cases)
* severe necrotising AP (20% of cases): 1 or more organ failures and/or local complications (necrosis, abscess, pseudocyst).
