== There are no fewer than 11 types of urinary incontinence (Figure 1). The 4 most frequent and most important types to look for as a first line of defence are: ==

*stress urinary incontinence
*Urge urinary incontinence
*mixed urinary incontinence (stress + urgency)
*and urinary incontinence due to chronic retention of urine.


'''Figure 1: classification of urinary incontinence types'''
[[File:Fig1bis.png|neant|vignette|After 6th International Consultation on Incontinence/International Continence Society 2017]]

*Stress incontinence is defined as the occurrence of urine loss during effort, urge incontinence as urine loss immediately preceded by a sudden and irresistible urge to urinate and mixed incontinence as the combination of stress and urge incontinence.
