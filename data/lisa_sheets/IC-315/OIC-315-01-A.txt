A clonal haematopoietic stem cell disease of the bone marrow resulting in proliferation and invasion of the bone marrow by immature, cancerous haematopoietic cells blocked at an early stage of differentiation: blasts. By definition, these blasts represent more than 20% of the cells on cytological examination of the myelogram.
