Acute leukaemia (ALL) accounts for 30-35% of childhood cancers (the most common cancer). There are 2 types of blast cell proliferation:

- Lymphoblastic AL = 80% of childhood leukaemias

- in 75%: immature B-lineage lymphoblasts

Ø 400 ALLs in France <15 years / year

- peak frequency 2 - 6 years

- girl = boy

- Myeloblastic LA = 20%.

Ø 100 LAM in France /year
