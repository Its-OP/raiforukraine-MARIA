Main types of disability in children= motor/sensory/cognitive/mental/psychic disability ([[Disability status SD-345|Disability status]]).


Congenital malformations: 2-3% of children, around 20% with severe disabilities

Cerebral palsy: 1<sup>rst</sup> cause of motor disability (1/1500 births) linked to early cerebral lesions

Trisomy 21: 1<sup>rst</sup> cause of hereditary mental retardation

Foetal alcohol syndrome: 1<sup>rst</sup> cause of non-hereditary mental retardation

Dyslexia- dysorthographia: the 1<sup>rst</sup> cause of specific learning difficulties

Multiple disabilities: around 9,000 children in France
