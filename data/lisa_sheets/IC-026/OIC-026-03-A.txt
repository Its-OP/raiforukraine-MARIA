Orientation of the clinical examination in the context of acute abdominal pain in pregnant women

==Search for life-threatening etiologies (extreme emergency)==
- Obstetrical context

- Medical and obstetrical history

- Characteristics of pain

- Haemodynamic tolerance: BP and pulse

- Palpation of the uterus: uterine contracture

- [[Genital bleeding during pregnancy SD-111|Metrorrhagia]]

- Detection of foetal heart sounds<br />.

==Extreme emergency eliminated==
- Taking the temperature

- Abdominal palpation, lumbar fossa

- Vaginal touch / speculum: cervical changes / [[Loss of fluid in a preterm pregnant woman SD-109|loss of fluid]]

- [[Urine dipstick analysis SD-182|Urine dipstick]]
