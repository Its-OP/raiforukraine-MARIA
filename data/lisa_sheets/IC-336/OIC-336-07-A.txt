- evoked by capillary blood glucose, confirmed by blood glucose (blood glucose < 3 mmol/L)

- emergency resucration with direct IV glucose serum (G30% 1 ampoule) if consciousness is impaired

- orally, if you are conscious enough: sugar cubes, orange juice, etc.
