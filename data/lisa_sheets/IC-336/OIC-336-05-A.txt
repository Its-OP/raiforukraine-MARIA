- onset: abrupt, rapidly progressive, gradual

- presence of fever, purpura

- presence of meningeal syndrome

- presence of oculomotor disorders

- trauma (bruising, haematoma in the eyeglass or retroauricular area)

- convulsions, myoclonus

- context :

o chronic intoxication: alcohol, illicit substances

o farewell letter, empty blister packs

o Empty syringes, injection marks

o prescriptions for oral antidiabetics or insulin

o dilapidated accommodation, heating methods (CO ...)
