Etiological diagnosis and monitoring of CKD is based primarily on functional respiratory investigations.

Chest X-rays are routinely ordered at the time of diagnosis, and can sometimes be used to confirm the diagnosis. It may be supplemented by a thoracic CT scan, particularly if PID is suspected.
