*Gynecomastia is a frequent hyperplasia of mammary glandular tissue, which may be uni- or bilateral.
*It is sometimes discovered by chance, more often on the occasion of a sensitive discomfort in the peri-areolar region, or even a visible and painless enlargement of the breast in men.
*It is a frequent symptom, associated with numerous aetiologies, the common feature of which is an increase in the oestradiol/testosterone ratio.
