== Early Interaction


Early, stable and secure parent-child interactions play a central role in emotional and cognitive development.

Deleterious interactions and environments, as well as exposure to traumatic events, have various consequences including [[Stress and adaptation to stress; Adjustment disorders 2C-066-DE-A05|Adjustment disorders and post-traumatic stress states]]; [[2C-066-DE-A04]]
