==Definition==
Specific learning disabilities are neurodevelopmental disorders characterized by cognitive abnormalities that disrupt acquisition (reading, spelling, mathematics) without this disruption being explained by intellectual development disorders, sensory abnormalities or environmental circumstances (serious environmental deficiencies, lack of schooling, etc.).

==Main specific disorders==

*Specific oral language disorders (SOLD)
*Specific written language disorders: affecting reading (formerly "dyslexia") and/or spelling (formerly "dysorthographia").
*Mathematical cognition disorders

==Main differential diagnoses===

*Sensory anomalies
*Severe environmental deficiencies
*Other [[Neurodevelopmental disorders (see items 55, 118, 121, 122, 138)|neurodevelopmental disorders]]
