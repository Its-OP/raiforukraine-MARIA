== Call signs

* language delay
* psychomotor delay
* learning difficulties which may affect several areas: comprehension, reasoning, problem solving, planning, synthesis and abstraction skills
* behavioural problems

== Main causes

* Antenatal :
**Environmental: infectious or toxic exposure in utero (alcohol and other toxins, certain drugs, etc.).
**Genetic: abnormalities in chromosome number and structure, X-linked, linked to point mutations.
*Perinatal: complications of pregnancy and childbirth
*Postnatal :
**Trauma
**Infections (meningitis)
**Neurodegenerative encephalopathies
