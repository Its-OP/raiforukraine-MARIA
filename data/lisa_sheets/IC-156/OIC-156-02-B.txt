'''Prevalence :'''

- overall (community-acquired HAIs and HAIs on equipment): 70 / 100,000 inhabitants / year in France

- children (community-acquired HAI): 22/100,000 / year (80/100,000 in children under 1 year of age)

'''Terrain and contributing factors:''''

- age

- male sex

- diabetes

- obesity

- active smoking

- underlying joint pathology: osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, etc. ...

In children, HAI usually occurs in the absence of risk factors.

Overall mortality: 5%.

'''Morbidity:''' 40% functional sequelae
