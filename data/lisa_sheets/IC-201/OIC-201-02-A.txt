The different organs transplanted are :

*Rein
*Liver
*Heart
*Pancreas
*Lungs
*Intestines


Cell and tissue transplants can involve :

*Haematopoietic stem cells
*Cornea
*Amniotic membranes
*bones, tendons, ligaments, menisci
*Skin
*Heart valves, arteries and veins
