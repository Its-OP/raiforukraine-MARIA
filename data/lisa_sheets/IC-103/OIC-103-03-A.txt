*'''Elements of the diagnosis of [[Vertigo and vertiginous sensation SD-064|vertigo]]''''
**Interrogation is essential.
**Analytical semiology is based on the <u>characteristics of the vertigo</u>, its <u>duration</u>, the <u>circumstances of onset</u>, its <u>evolving characteristics</u>, the <u>associated symptoms,</u> and the <u>antecedents</u>.
**cf: [[Definition of vertigo 2C-103-DE-A01]]: '''<big>notion of illusion of movement +++++</big>'''<br />

*'''Differential diagnoses:''''
**Lipothymia or vagal malaise [[Coma and consciousness disorders SD-028|(coma and consciousness disorders)]], orthostatic hypotension ([[Discovery of arterial hypotension SD-043|discovery of arterial hypotension]])
**Syncope, glare, flying flies, of cardiovascular, migraine ([[Headache SD-118|headache]]), visual ([[Diplopia SD-143|diplopia]], astigmatism) or epileptic origin ([[Convulsions SD-120|convulsions]]).
**Polymorphic manifestations of [[Anxiety SD-116|anxiety]] or hypochondria, phobia (acrophobia, agoraphobia, panic attacks, etc.).
