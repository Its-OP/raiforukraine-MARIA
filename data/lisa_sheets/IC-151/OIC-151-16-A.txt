Presumed bacterial = cloudy or clear CSF and glycorrhea ≤ 0.4 x blood glucose

Argument for listeriosis = terrain, progressive onset, rhombencephalic involvement

Association with Dexamethasone: if IC on LP, if CSF cloudy, or ED positive for CG+ or CG-.
