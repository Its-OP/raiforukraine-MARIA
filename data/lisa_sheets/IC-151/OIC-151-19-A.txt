Complementary respiratory precautions such as droplets

Invasive meningococcal infections are notifiable diseases: confirmed or probable cases must be reported immediately by clinicians and biologists to the Regional Health Agencies (ARS), which will look for contacts in the vicinity of the cases.

Individual preventive measures for contact subjects (antibiotic prophylaxis, and in some cases vaccination)
