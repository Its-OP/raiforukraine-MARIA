Meningitis is suspected on the basis of a febrile meningeal syndrome.

The symptoms encountered are :

* headache SD-118|Headache]] violent, in helmet
* Photophobia
* Nausea-vomiting SDD-013|Vomiting
* Stiff neck
* Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|Fever
