In the case of encephalitis, central neurological disorders are present:

* Coma and disorder of consciousness SD-028|disorder of consciousness]]
* [[Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit SD-121|focalisation]]
* [[Mental confusion/disorientation SD-119|disorders of higher functions]]
* [[Convulsions SD-120|seizures]]

They can be present regardless of the type of pathogen.

The main infectious cause is viral, linked to HSV1. Hyperthermia tends to be moderate. Personality disorders, frontal and/or temporal syndrome, memory problems and hallucinations are common in this infection.
