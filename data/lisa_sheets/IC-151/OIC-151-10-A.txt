Any clinical suspicion of meningitis should benefit from an emergency lumbar puncture.

There are some contraindications to its immediate use:

- Haemodynamic instability

- Infection of the puncture site

- Clinically suspected or known haemostasis disorders

- Focus sign

- Persistent convulsive seizure

- Glasgow<12

- Signs of cerebral involvement
