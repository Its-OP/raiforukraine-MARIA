The diagnosis of brain abscess is difficult to make on clinical grounds alone:

Fever is inconstant

The absence of meningeal syndrome is the rule, but headaches are frequent, sometimes associated with signs of intracranial hypertension.

The progressive appearance of focal signs points to this diagnosis
