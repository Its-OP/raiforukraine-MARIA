This is an absolute emergency.

Immediate antibiotic treatment on site as soon as it is recognised.

Immediate IV or IM injection of a third-generation cephalosporin: Ceftriaxone or, failing that, Cefotaxime (1g in adults, 50mg/kg in infants and children).

Systematic call of 15 if pre-hospital care for medical transport

Management of shock

Additional droplet-type respiratory precautions

LP not necessary and contraindicated in an emergency
