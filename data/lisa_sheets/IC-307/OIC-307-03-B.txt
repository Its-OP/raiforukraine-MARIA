Search for inflammatory syndrome ('''acute or chronic inflammatory syndrome'', '''elevation of C-reactive protein (CRP))''''

Search for signs in favour of bone marrow invasion: anaemia, leucopenia, thrombocytopenia (''interpretation of the blood count'')

Search for hypercalcaemia (''Dyscalcaemia'')

Protein electrophoresis: ruling out multiple myeloma (''Interpretation of a myelogram, Analysis of serum protein electrophoresis)''
