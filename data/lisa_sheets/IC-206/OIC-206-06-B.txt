The diagnosis is based on the presence of at least one of the following items:

Abundant parapneumonic effusion, a partitioned effusion (non-decomposed) on X-ray, ultrasound or CT scan, a macroscopically purulent pleural fluid (or cytologically a predominance of altered neutrophils), a germ detected on direct examination of the pleural fluid, a positive bacterial culture of the pleural fluid.
