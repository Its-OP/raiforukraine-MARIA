== Non-medicinal therapeutic measures should be discussed at an early stage. ==

* Re-education adapted to the different stages of the disease, particularly in the form of speech therapy (re-education of dysarthria and swallowing problems) and physiotherapy (teaching daily gymnastics, preventing musculoskeletal complications by maintaining flexibility and muscle strength, working on postural balance, learning how to transfer, etc.) ([Prescription for re-education SD-247|prescription for re-education]]).
* Encouragement of physical activity and, if the patient wishes, sporting activity.
* Application for ALD.
* Application for professional recognition for people exposed to pesticides.
* Therapeutic education for patients and carers ("Global support for carers" SD-330|"Global support for carers").
* Patient associations (information for patients and carers).
