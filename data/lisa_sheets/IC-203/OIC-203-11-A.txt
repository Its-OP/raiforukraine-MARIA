'''See item 359 - [[Acute respiratory distress SD-160|respiratory distress]]''''


'''Clinical signs:'''

- Signs of struggle: tachypnoea (>25/min in adults), recruitment of inspiratory and expiratory muscle groups (pulling, active expiration, flapping of the wings of the nose).

- Signs of failure: paradoxical abdominal breathing, extreme bradypnoea, cyanosis, neurological repercussions (impaired alertness).

- Haemodynamic failure: acute cor pulmonale, arterial hypotension, mottling, cold skin, oliguria.
