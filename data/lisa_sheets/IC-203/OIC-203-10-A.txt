'''Cardiac etiologies: the majority of cardiopathies can be the cause of chronic dyspnoea. The main aetiologies described are:''''

- ischaemic heart disease

- hypertrophic cardiomyopathies

- Valvulopathy

- rhythm and conduction disorders

- pericardial causes

'''Pulmonary etiologies:''''

- Bronchial diseases: COPD, asthma.

- Diseases of the lung parenchyma: diffuse infiltrative pneumonitis, emphysema (associated with COPD)

- Diseases of the ventilatory pump: neuromuscular diseases, skeletal deformities (scoliosis), obesity-hypoventilation syndrome.

- Pulmonary vascular diseases: pulmonary arterial hypertension
