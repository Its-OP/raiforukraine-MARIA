'''Interrogatory:'''

*Age: acute or chronic
*Rhythm and frequency?
*Characteristics
**Effort (dyspnoea score, MMRC, NYHA)
**Positional (orthopnoea, antepnoea)
**Paroxysmal
*Mode of onset
*Triggering factors (nycthemeral, seasonal periodicity)
*Associated symptoms
*History and particular exposures

'''Clinical examination:'''

*Look for signs of severity: see item 359 [[Acute respiratory distress SD-160|acute respiratory distress]].
*Complete thoracic and extra-thoracic examination (cf respiratory semiology) - [[Discovery of anomalies on pulmonary auscultation SD-020|discovery of anomalies on pulmonary auscultation]].
