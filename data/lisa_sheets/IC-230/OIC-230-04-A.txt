'''The 4 cardiovascular emergencies that can cause chest pain are:'''

- aortic dissection

- acute coronary syndrome (ACS)

- pulmonary embolism (PE)

- pericarditis (tamponade)

=> Mnemonic PIED (Pericarditis, Infarction, Embolism, Dissection)
