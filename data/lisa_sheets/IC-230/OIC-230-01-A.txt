'''Know the definition of acute chest pain'''

<nowiki/>= pain (or discomfort) in the region of the rib cage (between the neck and the umbilicus) occurring suddenly.

Variable characteristics: intensity, location, irradiation, duration, triggering or relieving factor, frequency.
