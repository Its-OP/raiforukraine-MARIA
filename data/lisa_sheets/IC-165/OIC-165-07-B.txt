Confirmation important for isolated cases, diagnostic doubts, care of family and friends (possibly administration of a 3rd<sup>dose</sup>):

*Direct molecular diagnosis using RT/PCR in saliva, nasopharyngeal swabs and urine samples, and CSF in cases of meningitis.
*''Serological diagnosis'' to determine immunisation status
