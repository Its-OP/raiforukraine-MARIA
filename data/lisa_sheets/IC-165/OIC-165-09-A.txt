'''Prevention by MMR vaccination''' (trivalent vaccine): compulsory since January 2018 for under-2s (12 and 16-18 months), then catch-up if not immune or incomplete vaccination for subjects born after 1980 (2 doses)

Hygiene measures (hand washing) and isolation for 9 days after the onset of parotitis.
