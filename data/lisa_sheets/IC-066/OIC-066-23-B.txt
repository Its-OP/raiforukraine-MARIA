Worries centred on performance in sport and at school. Perfectionism is often associated.

Separation anxiety has clinical signs similar to GAD in children. It is characterised by persistent and excessive worries about losing one's parents or something bad happening to them, a refusal to leave the house or for one's parents to leave the house, repeated nightmares involving separation themes, and physical complaints in separation situations.
