SSRIs are not a first-line treatment, but should be prescribed in cases of severe or chronic symptoms, or when psychotherapy is not possible. Benzodiazepines should be avoided after trauma. Other anxiolytics (hydroxyzine, sedative antipsychotics) can be used to treat insomnia or anxiety.
