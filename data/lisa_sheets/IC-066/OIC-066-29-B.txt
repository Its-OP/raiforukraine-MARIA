Therapeutic education'' central to any chronic disorder with a risk of relapse (GAD, PD): explanations of symptoms, reassurance and information about the condition. Hygienic and dietary rules'' to be adopted over time: stopping stimulants (coffee, tobacco, alcohol), a balanced diet, sleep, regular exercise, relaxation techniques, information on the use of anxiolytics (Item 74).
