Treatment of the crisis with a rapid-acting anxiolytic such as [[Benzodiazepines|benzodiazepine]] or hydroxyzine on an occasional basis, temporarily in combination with SSRIs (background treatment). Prevent the risk of dependence on [[Benzodiazepines: mechanisms of action, indications, side-effects, drug interactions, monitoring procedures and main causes of failure 2C-330-PC-A09|benzodiazepines]] and include behavioural management (reassurance, abdominal breathing and therapeutic education).
