The lifetime prevalence of PTSD is 5-10% of the general population, higher in at-risk populations up to 50% (rape victims, survivors of combat or terrorist acts). Sex ratio of 2 women to 1 man. Exposure to trauma is common in children and adolescents (25%), with 10% developing PTSD before the age of 18.
