EDC is multifactorial, with the interaction of genetic and environmental risk factors. Depression is associated with disturbances in monoaminergic neurotransmission (serotonin, noradrenaline, dopamine), the corticotropic stress axis, inflammatory biomarkers, neurotrophins and fronto-subcortical loops.
