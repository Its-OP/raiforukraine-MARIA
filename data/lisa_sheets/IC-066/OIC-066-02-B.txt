Common disorder, 10% of adults, 2 times more in women from adolescence onwards.

EDC increases the risk of suicide: 30-50% of suicide attempts are the result of an EDC, and more than 80% of suicide victims have an EDC at the time of the [[Suicide, suicidal, suicidant, suicidaire 2C-353-DE-A01|suicide]].

The first EDC can occur at any age, but more often in young adults (median age 25), regardless of culture, level of education or socio-economic status.
