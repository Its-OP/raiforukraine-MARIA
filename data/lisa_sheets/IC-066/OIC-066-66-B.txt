Hospitalisation is exceptional in cases of overwhelming anxiety, depressive comorbidity, suicidal crisis or very negative environment. In vulnerable patients, assess living conditions, current or past trauma and the risk of further trauma. Hospitalisation provides an opportunity to assess and protect.
