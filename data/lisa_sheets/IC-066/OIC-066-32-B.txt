First-line, long-term use of SSRIs for severe and disabling forms (GAD, PD). No medication is indicated for simple phobias.

In severe SAD, the use of SSRIs or SNRIs can reduce anticipations [[Anxiety SD-116|anxious]] and emotional activations (Item 74). A combination of pharmacological treatment and CBT is indicated in adults and children/adolescents if the intensity is moderate to severe or if treatment with CBT has failed.

In the case of school refusal anxiety, the drug targets psychiatric comorbidity in cases of severe disorder or failure to respond to non-pharmacological treatment.
