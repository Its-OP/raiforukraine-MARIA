Cardiovascular diseases (acute coronary syndrome, hypertension, rhythm disorders), respiratory diseases (asthma, pneumopathy), neurological diseases (epilepsy, MS, migraines, transient ischaemic attacks), endocrine diseases (hypoglycaemia, diabetes, dysthyroidism, pheochromocytoma, hyperthyroidism, Cushing's syndrome, hypoparathyroidism).

Intoxication (amphetamines, cocaine, hallucinogens, cannabis) or withdrawal (alcohol, benzodiazepines, opiates).

Other anxiety disorders: post-traumatic stress disorder, adjustment disorder, PD and OCD.
