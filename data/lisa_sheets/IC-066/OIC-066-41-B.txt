- Psychiatric: schizophrenia (distinction between delusions and obsessions), EDC (anxious ruminations suggestive of obsessions), GAD, simple phobia, hypochondria (Item 72), abnormal movements / tics, obsessive-compulsive personality disorder (no obsessions or compulsions, preoccupation with order, perfectionism and control).

- Non-psychiatric: Tourette's syndrome and Huntington's chorea.
