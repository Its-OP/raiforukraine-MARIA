Therapeutic education is essential: reassuring the patient, naming the disorder, providing information about its functional impact and the risk of chronicity, the existence of effective drugs with a delayed onset of action, the use of high doses, CBT exercises outside the sessions, meeting the family and friends to explain the disorder and the treatment.
