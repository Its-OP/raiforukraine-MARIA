Adjustment disorder (AD) is characterised by symptoms in response to life events to which the subject is unable to adapt. No symptoms sufficient to establish a diagnosis of mood disorder, anxiety disorder or stress disorder. The subject's reaction is excessive compared to the usual reaction to similar stressors.
