The mood disorder is manifested by irritability, particularly if Attention Deficit and Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD): definition and symptoms 2C-067-DE-A01|Attention Deficit and Hyperactivity Disorder, or tantrums, particularly if Psychomotor Development in Infants and Children|Autistic Spectrum Disorder.

Episodic irritability in an EDC should be distinguished from chronic irritability, which is suggestive of severe emotional dysregulation in the child.

Look for disruption to performance or academic achievement.

Look for a change in weight trajectory.

The earlier the first episode, the higher the risk of recurrence and suicide.
