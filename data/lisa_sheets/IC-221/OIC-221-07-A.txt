Atheroma affects

- large and medium-calibre arteries,

- especially in areas where the arterial walls are subject to haemodynamic stress (bifurcation, ostium, etc.),

- mainly the coronary arteries, the carotid arteries, the terminal abdominal aorta, the iliac arteries and the arteries of the lower limbs, in particular the superficial femoral artery.

The main organs affected are the heart, brain, kidneys, muscles and digestive system.
