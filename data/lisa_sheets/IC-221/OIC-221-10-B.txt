'''Health and disease education consists of:'''

- the patient's understanding of atheromatous disease and cardiovascular risk factors,

- an explanation of the warning signs of the disease and what to do in the event of symptoms,

- the definition of therapeutic objectives which will be re-evaluated regularly,

- raising patients' awareness of the importance of adhering to monitoring and treatment, and of monitoring the efficacy and tolerance of medicines,

- repeated motivational talks to help people change their lifestyle habits (smoking cessation, diet and physical activity) and ensure that these are maintained.
