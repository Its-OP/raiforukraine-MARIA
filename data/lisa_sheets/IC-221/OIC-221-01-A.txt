Variable association of changes in the intima of large- and medium-calibre arteries.

Focal accumulation of lipids, complex carbohydrates, blood, blood products, fibrinous tissue and calcareous deposits in the intima, accompanied by changes in the media.

Atherosclerosis: inflammatory response to an attack on the arterial endothelium.
