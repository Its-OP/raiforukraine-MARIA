'''Risk factors determined at the 1<sup>er</sup> consultation that result in specialist management'''

- General risk factors (in particular individual and social factors; occupational risk; family history);

- medical risk factors (including obesity, diabetes, chronic hypertension, coagulation disorders and other chronic illnesses)

- '''personal history, gynaecological or otherwise'' (in particular surgical history; uterine pathologies);

- '''a personal history of previous pregnancy or childbirth'';

- '''exposure to toxic substances''' (alcohol, tobacco, drugs, potentially teratogenic medicines); '''[[Prevention of alcohol-related risks SD-313|Prevention of alcohol-related risks,]]''' ['''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''''

- '''risks [[Prevention of foetal risks SD-312|infectious]]''' (in particular toxoplasmosis, rubella, genital herpes, syphilis, whooping cough, CMV , HIV, hepatitis);
