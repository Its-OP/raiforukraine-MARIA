In a normal pregnancy, a pregnant woman can be monitored by her GP, a midwife or a gynaecologist, as she chooses. Follow-up at the end of pregnancy (from the 8th<sup> month</sup> onwards) is generally carried out by a midwife or gynaecologist at the maternity hospital chosen for the birth.
