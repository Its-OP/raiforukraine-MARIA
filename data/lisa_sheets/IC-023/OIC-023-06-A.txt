The declaration of pregnancy must be made during the first 14 weeks of pregnancy (16 SA) to the Health Insurance fund and the Family Allowances fund.

Entitles you to maternity-related social benefits. The declaration is most often made after the ultrasound dating of the first trimester.

Completed by the healthcare professional treating the woman, either online or on a paper form

Clinical and complementary examinations must be carried out beforehand (CF.)
