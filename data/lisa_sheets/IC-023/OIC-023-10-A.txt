==Follow-up includes
seven compulsory consultations, reimbursed by the health insurance. These consultations provide an opportunity to check that there are no complications, and to help patients through their pregnancy by providing information, answering any questions they may have and suggesting any additional tests that need to be carried out. '''[[Follow-up consultation for a normal pregnancy (17, 2nd and 3rd trimester) SD-268|Consultation de suivi d'une grossesse normale (1er ,2e ,3e trimestre)]]''''

During follow-up consultations we look for :

Stress and insecurity factors (identify situations of psychosocial vulnerability, domestic violence)

Genital bleeding during pregnancy SD-111|bleeding;

Fever ;

Functional urinary signs;

Leucorrhoea SD-104|leucorrhoea]] with a pathological appearance

Regular and painful uterine contractions ''[[Uterine contraction in a pregnant woman SD-098|Uterine contractions in a pregnant woman]]''''

Active foetal movements (from 18 to 20 weeks' gestation)

Biological monitoring anomalies

==Physical examination explores==
Measurement of blood pressure;

Weight measurement ;

Measurement of uterine height

Gynaecological examination in the event of complaints to be investigated or if necessary to carry out cervical cancer screening)

Fetal heart sound detection with fetal Doppler from 12-14 weeks of amenorrhoea
