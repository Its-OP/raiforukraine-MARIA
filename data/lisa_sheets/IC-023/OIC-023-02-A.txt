To be considered if :

Delayed menstruation in women of childbearing age.

Lack of contraception

Sympathetic signs of pregnancy (breast tension, morning sickness, vomiting, pollakiuria, asthenia, dizziness) ''[[Nausea SD-012|nausea]], [[Vomiting SD-013|vomiting]], [[Asthenia SD-021|asthenia]]''', '''[[Urination abnormality SD-023|urination abnormality]], [[Vertigo and dizziness SD-064|vertigo and dizziness]]'''''

on examination, possible enlargement of the breasts or uterus depending on the term

Confirmed if :

Positive urine or blood test (greater than 5 IU/L) for human chorionic gonadotropin (Î²-hCG or hCG).
