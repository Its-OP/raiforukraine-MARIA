|Title=Know the main criteria for the different stages of pressure sores.
|Description=None
|Rubric=Positive diagnosis
|Contributors=Antoine Guillon
|Order=7}}

Stage 0 = Reactive hyperhaemia disappears with pressure

Stage 1 = Permanent erythema

Stage 2 = Partial skin lesion involving the epidermis, dermis or both

Stage 3 = Skin lesion involving all layers of the skin

Stage 4 = Extensive destruction with deep skin loss affecting all 3 skin layers and extending to the underlying bone, tendons and joints.
