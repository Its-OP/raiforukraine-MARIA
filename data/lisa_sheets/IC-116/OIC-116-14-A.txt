<br />

* Treat the specific dermatosis revealed by the pruritus.

== Symptomatic treatment


General advice: limit irritants such as antiseptics, avoid tight, rough clothing and prefer cotton, use superfatted cleansers, apply emollients, cut nails short to limit scratching lesions.
