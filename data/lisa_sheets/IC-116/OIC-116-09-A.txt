Main systemic causes of pruritus :


-Cholestasis

-Chronic renal failure

-Haemopathies (lymphomas, myeloproliferative disorders)

-Dysthyroidism

-Chronic infections (HIV, hepatitis B and C)

-Martial deficiency

-Medicines

-Psychogenic causes
