'''Polymorphic disease.'''

'''Diagnosis of LS = clinical + biological signs.'''

<br />

== '''Most frequent diseases''' ==
- skin

- Raynaud's phenomenon

- joint damage (''joint pain)'''

- seritis (pericarditis, pleurisy).


'''Fever''' ('''hyperthermia/fever''') possible.
<br />

== '''Renal damage''' ==
40% of cases.'''' Sometimes no clinical signs and only '''proteinuria'''.
<br />

== '''Rheumatological manifestations''' ==
'''80% of LS'''

Bilateral, symmetrical, non-deforming, non-destructive polyarthritis of small and medium-sized joints (metacarpophalangeal, proximal interphalangeal, carpal, knee, ankle) (''inflammatory joint pain'', ''joint stiffness'')
<br />

== Central or peripheral neurological manifestations ==
Heterogeneous (''sensory and/or motor neurological deficit)''. Frequent but non-specific headaches. Organic psychiatric disorders.
<br />

== '''Cardiac manifestations''' ==
Most often '''pericarditis''' ('''chest pain; dyspnoea;''' '''discovery of abnormalities on cardiac auscultation; performance and interpretation of an electrocardiogram (ECG)'''), possible endocarditis or myocarditis
<br />

== '''Other disorders''' ==
Respiratory manifestations: '''pleurisy''' ('''chest pain; dyspnoea;''' '''discovery of abnormalities on pulmonary auscultation''')

'''Adenopathies''' peripheral ('''single or multiple adenopathies''') sometimes '''splenomegaly'''.
