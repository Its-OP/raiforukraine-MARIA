A ''''' colonic diverticulum''''' is an acquired herniation of the colonic mucosa and submucosa through an area of weakness in the muscular wall of the colon.

''''' colonic diverticulosis''''' is an asymptomatic condition characterised by the presence of several diverticula.


'''Diverticular disease'' includes:'''

- diverticulitis and its complications :

o colonic diverticulitis ''='' inflammation/infection of a diverticulum

o complicated diverticulitis '':'' abscess, fistula (sigmoido-vesical, sigmoido-vaginal...), peritonitis (purulent or stercoral), pseudo-tumoral stenosis

- diverticular haemorrhage.
