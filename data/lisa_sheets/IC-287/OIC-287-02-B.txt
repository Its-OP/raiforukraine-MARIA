- Diverticulosis and diverticulitis affect both sexes equally.

- Very rare before the age of 30, its prevalence is estimated at 20-35% in the general population. It increases with age: 50% of people aged 50 and over; 60-80% of people aged 85 and over.

- Diverticula are most often found on the sigmoid, but can be seen throughout the colonic tract with the exception of the rectum.
