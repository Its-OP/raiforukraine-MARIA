Differential diagnoses of diverticulitis include all causes of pain in the left iliac fossa. Among the most frequent and/or most serious are a number of inflammatory conditions:

- colic: ectopic appendicitis, colon cancer (abscessed), ischaemic colitis, bacterial colitis, IBD ;

- extra-colonic: salpingitis, ectopic pregnancy, etc.
