Main microorganisms isolated during national prevalence surveys :

- Urinary tract infections: E. coli (47% of microorganisms isolated)

- Surgical site infections: S. aureus (23%) or other micro-organisms (variable depending on the site of the operation)

- Pneumopathy: S aureus (15%) and P. aeruginosa (14%)

- Bacteremia: ''S. aureus'' (16%), ''E. coli'' (14%), ''S. epidermidis'' (14%), variable according to portal of entry)
