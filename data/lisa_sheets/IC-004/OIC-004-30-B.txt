Prevalence in France (National Prevalence Survey 2017): 5%, i.e. 1 in 20 patients hospitalised on a given day.


The four most frequent HCAI sites are :

- Urinary 30%

- 15% surgical site

- Pneumopathy 15% of cases

- Bacteremia 10% of cases
