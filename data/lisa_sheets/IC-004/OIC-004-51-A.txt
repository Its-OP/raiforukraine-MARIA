Peer groups'' are :

- Groups of doctors with the same speciality

- '''Without hierarchy''' between them

- Meeting ''regularly'' and in a climate of ''trust''.

- To make an informed analysis of their ''daily practice''.

- Centred on the presentation of ''clinical cases''.

- Drawing on ''collective expertise''...

With the aim of ''improving practices''.
