- Respect for asepsis when inserting urethral catheters

- Use a closed sampling system

- Avoid urethral catheterisation whenever possible (prefer penile sleeve-type alternatives)

- Shorten the duration of the survey as much as possible by reassessing the value of an indwelling survey on a daily basis

- Ensuring that patients are properly hydrated

- There is no need for systematic catheter changes, antibiotic prophylaxis, bladder washings, intra-vesical injections of antiseptics or antibiotics, or any other manipulation.
