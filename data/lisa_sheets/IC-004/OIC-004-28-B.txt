The HAS's missions can be broken down into three areas:


'''Assessment:'''

- Medicines

- Medical devices

- Professional acts


'''Recommendations:'''

- Good practice

- Public health

- Vaccines

- Care pathways and relevance

- Patient safety tools and programmes


'''Measurement/evaluation and support for improvement:''''

- Certification of public and private health establishments

- Accreditation of doctors and medical teams (risk specialities)

- Certification of prescription assistance software

- Evaluation of social and medico-social establishments and services

- Care quality and safety indicators

- Informing users about the quality of care provided.
