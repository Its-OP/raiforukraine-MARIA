The '''Haute Autorité de Santé (HAS)''' :

1 ''Evaluates'' medicines, medical devices and professional procedures, and proposes whether or not they should be ''reimbursed'' by the health insurance scheme;

2. recommends good professional, vaccination and public health practice

3.     Measure and improve "quality" in health establishments, in community medicine and in social and medico-social structures, in particular through :

- Certification'' of health establishments

- L''''accréditation''' des médecins des spécialités à risque

- L''''évaluation des pratiques professionnelles'''

4.     '''Informs''' healthcare professionals and the general public, and ensures the quality of medical information disseminated.


It is organised around :

- A college of eight members

- Specialist committees:

o Recommendations, relevance, pathways and indicators

o Assessment of medical devices and health technologies

o Vaccinations

o Transparency

o Certification of healthcare establishments

o Impact of recommendations

o Economic and public health evaluation

o Social and medico-social

- Five functional divisions

- External experts


'''Risk-bearing event reporting system''': undesirable or risk-bearing events must be reported to the Agence Régionale de Santé (ARS), which processes them and then forwards them to the HAS after anonymisation. This :

- Provide national feedback

- Identifies recommendations for improving patient safety
