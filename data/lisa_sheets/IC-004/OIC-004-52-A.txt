Continuing professional development (CPD) for all healthcare professionals:

- Is enshrined in law (HPST 2009, Modernisation of the healthcare system 2016)

- Mandatory since 1 January 2013

- Is an active approach throughout professional life

- Takes account of public health priorities

- Is a continuing education scheme with a "three-year obligation".

- Based on a CPD offer validated by the Agence nationale du DPC <nowiki>https://www.agencedpc.fr/</nowiki>

- Comply with the multiannual CPD programme defined by the national professional college (CNP) for your speciality
