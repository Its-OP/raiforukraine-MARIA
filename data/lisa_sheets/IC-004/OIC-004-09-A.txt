'''Modalities'''

- Check the tolerance of the application site: healthy skin, damaged skin, mucous membranes, etc.

- Check for incompatibilities between products

- Avoid inactivation of the product by organic matter (blood, pus, etc.): a detersion phase may be necessary prior to application.

- Respect the action time (contact time required for the activity).

-Scrupulously respect the conditions of use of products (concentrations and instructions for use) to avoid the emergence of resistant strains.

'''Precautions for use'''

- Application site: healthy skin, mucous membranes, other tissues

- Contraindications or precautions for use: allergy, age (newborn, premature), pregnancy, etc.

- Its spectrum of action (favour broad-spectrum products)
