Meningeal haemorrhage or subarachnoid haemorrhage (SAH) = presence of blood in the [[Arachnoid and subarachnoid spaces|subarachnoid spaces]] :

           Non-traumatic or spontaneous SAH: in the absence of any cranial trauma

           -Traumatic or post-traumatic SAH: secondary to head trauma, and most often associated with other traumatic lesions.
