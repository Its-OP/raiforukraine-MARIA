'''Traumatic SAH:''' no specific emergency treatment, apart from that for other associated lesions

'''Spontaneous SAH:''' multidisciplinary collegial discussion :

*emergency (< 72 h): secure the aneurysm to prevent rebleeding
*endovascular treatment = arterioembolisation or neurosurgical treatment = clipping of the aneurysm
