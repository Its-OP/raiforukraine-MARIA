("''[[Discovery of a brain abnormality on medical imaging examination SD-226|discovery of a brain abnormality on medical imaging examination]]'''")

Positive diagnosis: spontaneous hyperdensity of the [[Arachnoid and subarachnoid spaces|subarachnoid spaces including the basal cisterns]]

Complications: cerebral oedema, intra-ventricular haemorrhage, intracranial haematoma, mass effect on the brain stem or ventricles, cerebral ischaemia due to vasospasm.
