Refer to ophthalmologist if

* personal or family history: risk factors = ophthalmological examination between 3 and 12 months, even in the absence of warning signs.
* abnormality or doubt on inspection
* abnormality or doubt on screening test
* warning signs
* inability to measure visual acuity (verbal age)

'''IN EMERGENCY if'''

o leukocoria,

o cloudy cornea (glaucoma)

o sudden onset of strabismus

o recent onset of nystagmus
