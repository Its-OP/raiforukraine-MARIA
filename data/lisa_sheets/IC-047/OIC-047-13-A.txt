|Title=Know the indications for referring a child to an ENT specialist.

|Rubric=Management
|Contributors=
|Order=13}}
Refer to ENT if

* a family history of deafness
* parental concern about hearing
* abnormality in a screening test
* warning signs:
** absence of reaction to noises, strong reactions to vibrations or touch, absence of reaction to being called or given a name, etc.
** Language disorders at these different stages: crying instead of melodic sounds, delay or absence of vocal emission/language, regression, articulations that are difficult to understand.
** comprehension difficulties, learning difficulties, concentration difficulties
** Behavioural and social interaction problems, indifference to others or agitation/aggression.
