Dental articulation = relationship between the maxillary and mandibular teeth

Joint disorder = conflict between the 2 dental arches

Manifested by: an open bite, lateral deviation of the mandible, prognathia, abutting incisors, overlapping, maxillary proaleviation

Orthodontic treatment before the age of 16
