More than 10 million anaesthetic procedures are carried out in France every year. More than 50% of these are performed on an outpatient basis, with a long-term target of 70%. The 5 most frequent procedures are :

- digestive endoscopies

- vaginal deliveries and caesarean sections

- operations on the crystalline lens

- surgery of the mouth and teeth

- carpal tunnel releases
