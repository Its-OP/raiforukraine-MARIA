Secondary osteoarthritis occurs in pre-existing destructive arthropathies. The main types are: microcrystalline arthritis, septic arthritis and destructive inflammatory rheumatism such as rheumatoid arthritis or spondyloarthritis.

Monogenic genetic arthroses are very rare and are part of dysmorphic syndromes.
