Osteoarthritis is an osteoarticular disease affecting all the tissues of the joint. The main sites of osteoarthritis are the knee, hip, hand and spine.

When osteoarthritis becomes symptomatic, it leads to mechanical [[Joint pain SD-067|joint pain]] and functional discomfort ([[Joint stiffness SD-056|joint stiffness]], [[onset of difficulty walking]], [[pain in one limb (upper or lower)]], [[joint deformity]]).
