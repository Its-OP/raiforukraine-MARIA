Whatever its location, the radiographic signs of osteoarthritis are :

- Localised joint pinching

- Subchondral osteocondensation

- Subchondral geodes

- Osteophytes

Not all of these radiographic signs are necessarily present.

In the knee, osteoarthritis can affect 1, 2 or all 3 compartments: medial femorotibial, lateral femorotibial and patellofemoral.
