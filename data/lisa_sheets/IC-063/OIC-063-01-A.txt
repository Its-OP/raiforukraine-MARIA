'''[[Delusional Ideas SD-124|Delusional Ideas]]:'''' Alterations in the content of thought leading to a loss of contact with reality, forming the subject of a generally unshakeable conviction, inaccessible to reasoning or challenge by the facts, not shared by the socio-cultural group.

'''[[Hallucinations SD-122|Hallucinations]]:''' Sensory perception without an object.
