*Neurological: epilepsy, brain tumours, stroke, inflammation, autoimmunity, etc.
*Endocrine: dysthyroidism, etc.
*Metabolic: Wilson's disease, hypoglycaemia, dyscalcaemia SD-200|dyscalcaemia.
*Infectious: neurosyphilis, AIDS...
*Drug-induced: L-DOPA, mefloquine...
*Intoxication by a psychoactive substance: psychostimulants (amphetamines, cocaine), hallucinogens (cannabis, anticholinergics, LSD, ketamine, etc.), etc.
