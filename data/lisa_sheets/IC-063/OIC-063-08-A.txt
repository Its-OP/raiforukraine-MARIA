Delusional ideas SD-124 are described by the following characteristics:

===''Theme''===
Single or multiple themes on which the delusional idea is based.

Themes: persecution, megalomania, mysticism, filiation, somatic, erotomania, influence, reference, loss of psychic intimacy, mission, messianic

===''Mechanism''===
Process of constructing the delusional idea. Mechanisms: interpretative, hallucinatory, intuitive and imaginative

===''Systematisation''===
Organisation and coherence of delusional ideas.

*In schizophrenia, delusions are unsystematised, vague, without logic or narrative thread.
*In persistent delusional disorder, delusions are systematised with an apparent logic to the delusion.

===''Adhesion''===
The patient's belief in their delusions, which is fluctuating and dynamic.

*Total adherence: unshakeable conviction, inaccessible to reasoning.
*Partial adherence: possible criticism of delusional ideas.

===''Emotional and behavioural repercussions''===

*anxiety
*risk of acting out in a self- or hetero-aggressive manner
*lifestyle changes
