'''Depending on microbial identification:'''

* Staphylococcus aureus: systematic echocardiography
* Candida: systematic echocardiography and fundus examination
* Enterobacteriaceae, enterococci: ECBU +/- abdominal imaging
* Anaerobes: abdominal imaging
* Bacteria associated with endocarditis (see item 152: S. aureus, oral streptococcus, Streptococcus gallolyticus): echocardiography
* Streptococcus pneumoniae: chest X-ray +/- lumbar puncture (if meningeal syndrome or normal chest imaging)
