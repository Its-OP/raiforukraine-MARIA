Incidence of bacteraemia in hospitalised patients = 1%.

Serious pathology :

*1/4 of patients present with severity criteria (see [[Sepsis and septic shock in children and adults|sepsis/septic shock]]).
*10-20% case fatality, depending on the series

Incidence of fungal infections: 1 to 1.5/10,000 hospital days

Secondary septic complications occur in 10-15% of cases: retinitis, endocarditis, hepato-splenic candidiasis, skin.
