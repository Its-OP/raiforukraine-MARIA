'''[[Elevated c-reactive protein (cr) SD-203|Inflammatory biomarkers]] (CRP, procalcitonin)'''''

- Non-specific, orientation value only

- Procalcitonin < 0.25 ng/ml makes bacteremia/fungemia unlikely

- No reason to measure them systematically
