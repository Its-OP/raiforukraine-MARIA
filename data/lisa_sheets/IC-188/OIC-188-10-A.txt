[[Acute respiratory distress SD-160|Acute respiratory distress]]

'''Signs of struggle'''

o Speaks in short sentences or interrupted by inspiration

o Prefers sitting to lying down

o Increase in RF > 25/min

o HR > 100/min

o SpO2 < 95% in AA

o Peak Expiratory Flow (PEF) ≤ 50% of the best value (or theoretical if unknown)

'''Signs of failure'''

o Speak in words

o Sitting bent over

o Agitated

o RF > 30/min

o Use of accessory respiratory muscles

o HR > 120/min

o SpO2 < 90% in air

o PEF ≤ 50% of the best value (or theoretical if unknown) or impossible to achieve

o auscultatory silence

o paradoxical breathing

o consciousness disorders, bradycardia, collapse
