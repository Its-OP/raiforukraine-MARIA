The aim of background asthma treatment is to achieve asthma control as described above (see asthma control).

In addition to monitoring efficacy, treatment monitoring includes:

o Assessing compliance with treatment

o Systematic assessment of the grip technique

o Looking for side effects (oral mycosis)
