o Obstructive ventilatory disorder: FEV1/CV<0.7

o Reversibility: increase in post-bronchodilator FEV1 of more than 200mL ''AND'' ≥12% of the pre-bronchodilator value. Complete reversibility is defined as FEV1/CV≥0.7 and FEV1 >80% post-bronchodilator.
