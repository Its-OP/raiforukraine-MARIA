The clinical diagnosis will be evoked:

*in the presence of '''urinary functional signs: variable pollakiuria, [[Burning micturition SD-096|burning micturition]], pelvic heaviness, cloudy urine, [[Haematuria SD-102|haematuria]] possible, ± [[Uterine contraction in a pregnant woman SD-098|uterine contractions]];'''
*in the absence of signs suggestive of pyelonephritis: apyrexia, absence of chills, absence of spontaneous lumbar pain, absence of pain on palpation of the lumbar fossae,
*a [[SD-182 urine dipstick analysis|urinary dipstick]] with leucocytes and nitrites +/- haematuria
