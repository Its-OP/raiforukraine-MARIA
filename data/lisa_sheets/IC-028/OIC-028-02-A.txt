The germs most frequently found are Gram-negative bacilli: Escherichia coli (75%), Proteus mirabilis, Klebsiella pneumoniae, Serratia and Enterobacter.

E. Coli is resistant to amoxicillin (with or without clavulanic acid) in 25% to 45% of cases.
