'''The main bacterial aetiologies of zoonoses are:'''

-Pasteurellosis (''Pasteurella multocida''), Lyme borreliosis (''Borrelia burgdorferi'' sensu lato), cat scratch disease (''Bartonella henselae''), Q fever (''Coxiella burnetii''), brucellosis (''Brucella melitensis, B. suis, B. abortus)''.

'''The main parasitic etiologies of zoonoses are:''''

-Toxoplasmosis (''Toxoplasma gondii''), leishmaniasis (''Leishmania'' spp.), hydatid echinococcosis or hydatid cyst (''Echinococcus granulosus''), alveolar echinococcosis (''Echinococcus multilocularis'').
