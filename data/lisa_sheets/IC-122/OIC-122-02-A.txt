The physical medicine and rehabilitation doctor diagnoses, monitors and treats pathologies that cause disability.

Role :

* Assess the patient according to the International Classification of Functioning (ICF) of disability (impairments, activity limitations, participation restrictions) ('''[[Disability situation SD-345|Disability situation]]''')
* Establish the indication for re-education, participate in the elaboration and coordination of complex re-education programmes and ensure their follow-up.
* Develops the patient's therapeutic project including the development of a re-education programme, vocational guidance and reintegration if necessary, long-term monitoring of the specific symptoms responsible for the persistent disability.
