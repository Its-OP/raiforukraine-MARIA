Maintenance rehabilitation :

* Indicated in certain chronic disabilities, for example in cases of high spasticity, or in chronic low back pain patients to maintain current abilities and the benefits of rehabilitation (''''[[Disability situation SD-345|Disability situation]], [[Assessment and management of chronic pain SD-260|Assessment and management of chronic pain]], [[Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit SD-121|Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit]])'''''

Description :

* Self-maintenance always preferable: simple programme, limited number of exercises
* If self-maintenance impossible, prescription of short series of rehabilitation sessions possible for :
** Recovery of a functional level after progressive decline ('''[[Progressive loss of autonomy SD-031|Progressive loss of autonomy]])''''
** Revision or adaptation of exercises from the self-maintenance programme
