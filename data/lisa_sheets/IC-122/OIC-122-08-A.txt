Indications for occupational therapy in common pathologies:

* Upper limb impairment (''''[[Limb trauma SD-173|Limb trauma]], [[Pain in a limb (upper or lower) SD-071|Pain in a limb (upper or lower)]], [[Joint pain SD-067|Joint pain]], [Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit SD-121|Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit]], [[Joint stiffness SD-056|Joint stiffness]], [[Joint deformity SD-070|Joint deformity]])''''
* Loss of autonomy secondary to prehension problems, limitations to movement or transfer, cognitive problems, etc.
* Installation and positioning of the patient (bed, armchair) ('''[[Management of a patient in prolonged decubitus SD-276|Management of a patient in prolonged decubitus]], [[Escarre SD-086|Escarre]])'''''
