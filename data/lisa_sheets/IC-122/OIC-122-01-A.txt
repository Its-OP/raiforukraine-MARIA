The doctor's role :

* Prescribe rehabilitation if indicated
* Define the objectives of rehabilitation treatment,
* Be aware of any contraindications to rehabilitation.

Rehabilitation programme :

* Defined by the rehabilitation assistants involved, the number of sessions, their frequency and the means of evaluating their impact.
* Adapted according to the patient's progress
