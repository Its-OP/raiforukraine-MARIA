Indications for speech therapy in common pathologies :

* Dysphonia
* Dysarthria
* Written or spoken language disorders ('''[[Language and/or phonation disorders SD-134|Language and/or phonation disorders]])''''
* Communication disorders ('''[[Attention disorders SD-129|Atention disorders]], [[Memory disorders/cognitive decline SD-131|Memory disorders/cognitive decline]])''''
* Difficulties integrating into school (''''[[Psychomotor development abnormality SD-115|Psychomotor development abnormality]])'''''
* Swallowing disorders ('''[[Swallowing disorders or false swallowing SD-062|Swallowing disorders or false swallowing]])''''
