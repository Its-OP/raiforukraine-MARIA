The decision to stop rehabilitation depends on the objectives initially set and is taken if :

* Objectives achieved
* Objectives not achieved within the initial timeframe. The causes may be :
** Unsuitable prescription: rehabilitation is not the solution
** Unsuitable rehabilitation techniques used
** Poor tolerance of rehabilitation
** Non-cooperative patient
