'''Somatic differential diagnoses:''''

* Endocrine diseases (insulin-dependent diabetes, hyperthyroidism, panhypopituitarism, Addison's disease)
* Diseases of the digestive tract (Crohn's disease, achalasia of the oesophagus)
* Certain brain tumours (brain stem tumour, craniopharyngioma).
* Certain haemopathies (leukaemia).

'''Psychiatric differential diagnoses:''''

* Other OCDs (food restriction or avoidance disorders).
* Characteristic depressive episodes.
* Obsessive-compulsive disorder.
* Schizophrenia.
