* '''Renutrition:'''
** Restoration of a healthy weight.
** Must be cautious and progressive in order to avoid an ''inappropriate renutrition syndrome'' (IRS)
*** Hepatic cytolysis,
*** hydrosodium retention and hyperosmolar state,
*** haemostasis disorders,
*** hypophosphatemia
*** hypokalaemia
*** hypomagnesaemia
*** dysglycaemia,
*** vitamin deficiencies,
*** heart failure with risk of cardiac rhythm disorder
* '''Rehabilitation:''''
** Normalisation of behaviour and food intake.
