'''PICA'''

* Definition:
** '''Ingestion''' of repeated '''non-nutritive substances''' for at least 1 month (plaster, lead, sand, pebbles, hair, paper, plastic...)
* Often occurs in young children with
** martial deficiency or zinc deficiency
** neglect
** an attachment disorder
** or a severe neurodevelopmental disorder.
