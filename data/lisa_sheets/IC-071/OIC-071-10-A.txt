'''Main principles and objectives of management'''

* '''Management:''''
** As early as possible, to prevent chronicity and complications.
** Multidisciplinary, coordinated, seamless and prolonged.
** Initially on an outpatient basis, except in emergencies.
* Objectives
** Target weight, nutritional status and psychological state.
** Must be personalised, concerted and contractualised, with the involvement of the family.
