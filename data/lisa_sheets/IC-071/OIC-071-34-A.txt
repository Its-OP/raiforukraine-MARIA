'''Merycism'''

* Definition:
** '''Repeated regurgitation of food''' for a period of at least 1 month.
** Food may be ''remastiqued'', ''re-swallowed'' or ''spat out''.
** Primarily affects children aged 3 months to 1 year, more rarely adults.
