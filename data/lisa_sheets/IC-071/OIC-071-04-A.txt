'''Diagnosis of Anorexia Nervosa (DSM-5 criteria)'''
* '''Restriction of energy intake in relation to needs''' leading to a significantly low weight.
* Intense fear of gaining weight and of becoming fat, despite being underweight.
* Altered perception of body weight or shape (dysmorphophobia), low self-esteem, or persistent lack of recognition of the seriousness of current thinness.

* There are two types of Mental Anorexia
** Restrictive type'': over the last 3 months, weight loss is essentially achieved by dieting, fasting and/or excessive physical exercise.
** ''Hyperphagic/Purgative Access Type'': in the last 3 months, recurrent hyperphagic episodes and/or induced vomiting or purgative behaviour.
