'''Eating disorders''''

* Lifetime prevalence: 3-5% in the adult population.
* Sex ratio: 1 man to 2 women
* Affects 30-50% of obese subjects

'''Anorexia nervosa'''

* Lifetime prevalence: 1.4% in women and 0.2% in men.
* Sex ratio: 1 man to 8 women.
* Anorexia nervosa begins in the majority of cases between the ages of 15 and 25, but there are forms with an early onset from the age of 6-7.
* Psychiatric illness with the ''highest mortality rate'' (around 5% mortality 10 years after the onset of the illness), with the highest associated suicide rate of all psychiatric illnesses.
