{| class="wikitable"
|'''Anamnestics'''
|
* Rapid weight loss ≥ 20 % in 3 months in adults or more than 2 kg/week in adolescents.
* Malaise
* Incoercible vomiting.
* Failed ambulatory renutrition.
* Extreme restriction'' (refusal to eat and/or drink).
|-
|Clinical
|
* Obsessive, intrusive and permanent ideations.
* Inability to control compensatory behaviours.
* Need for nutritional assistance (nasogastric tube).
* Significant ''Amyotrophy'' with axial hypotonia.
* Dehydration, hypothermia, arterial hypotension or bradycardia.
* ECG abnormalities.
|-
|Paraclinical tests
|
* Hypoglycaemia.
* '''ASAT or ALAT ˃ 10 N.'''
* '''Severe hydroelectrolytic'' or '''metabolic disorders.'''
* Renal insufficiency.'''
* '''Leukopenia or neutropenia.'''
|-
|'''Risk'''

'''suicidal'''
|
* Suicide attempt or specific suicide plan.
* Repeated self-harm.
|-
|Comorbidities
|May alone justify hospitalisation if "severe":

* Characteristic depressive episode;
* Substance abuse
* Severe anxiety
* Psychotic symptoms;
* Obsessive-compulsive disorder.
|-
|'''Motivation,''''

'''cooperation'''
|
* Failure of outpatient care.
* Patient uncooperative with outpatient care.
|-
| colspan="2" |'''Environmental and social criteria'''
|-
|Availability

'''de l'entourage'''
|
* Family problems or absence of family.
* Exhaustion of family and friends.
|-
|Stress

'''Environmental'''
|
* High environmental criticism.
* Severe social isolation.
|-
|Availability

'''Des soins'''
|
* No outpatient treatment possible (lack of facilities...).
