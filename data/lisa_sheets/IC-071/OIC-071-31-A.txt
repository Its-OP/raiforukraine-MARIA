'''Somatic Comorbidities :''''

* Digestive disorders: halitosis, gastro-oesophageal reflux, bloating and abdominal pain, alternating diarrhoea and constipation.
* Hepatic steatosis.
* Complications of obesity.
* Poor nutritional status, even malnutrition.
* Hypofertility, even infertility.

'''Psychiatric and addictive comorbidities: They are frequent:'''

* Bipolar disorder and major depressive episode,
* Anxiety disorders,
* Substance use disorders,
* Risk of suicide.
