'''Semiology of bulimia (DSM-5 criteria)'''

* Occurrence of recurrent episodes of uncontrolled hyperphagia''. That is to say:
** Food intake well above average and in a short space of time ;
** associated with a ''feeling of losing control'' over the quantities eaten or the ability to stop.
* Compensatory behaviours to avoid weight gain.
** Induced vomiting,
** Taking laxatives or diuretics,
** Fasting,
** Excessive exercise
* With an ''average frequency of at least once a week'' for at least 3 months.
* Self-esteem is excessively disturbed by body shape and weight.
* The disorder does not occur exclusively during a period of anorexia nervosa.
