'''Psychotherapeutic aspect of care'''

* Objectives
** Promote adherence to care and the therapeutic alliance.
** To correct cognitive distortions and dysfunctional attitudes.
** Strengthen self-esteem, self-confidence and assertiveness.
** Improving interpersonal relationships.
** Treat any psychiatric co-morbidities.
* Principles
** Adapted to the patient's general medical condition, abilities and wishes.
** Prolonged ''at least 1 year'' after significant clinical improvement.
* Types
** Alone or in combination
** Family therapy (++ in children and adolescents).
** Cognitive-behavioural therapy.
** Other (motivational interviewing, supportive psychotherapy, psychoanalytically inspired therapies, body-mediated approaches).
