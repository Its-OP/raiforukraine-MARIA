Associated diagnoses (= comorbidities)

'''Somatic comorbidities:''''

*Osteoporosis
*Myotrophy
*Edema
*Hydroelectrolytic disorders: hyponatremia, hypokalaemia, hypocalcaemia.
*Functional renal failure.
*Hypoglycaemia, with malaise and loss of consciousness.
*deficiency anaemia (iron, B9, B12, etc.).
*Thrombocytopenia, leukopenia and lymphopenia (with increased risk of infection).
*Cardiovascular problems: rhythm disorders, bradycardia, hypotension.
*Digestive disorders: oesophageal burning, delayed gastric emptying.
*ENT and dental disorders: enlarged salivary glands, dental erosions, etc.

'''Psychiatric and addictive comorbidities: <u>frequent</u>''''

*Characteristic depressive episode
*Anxiety disorders
*Borderline personality disorder
*Substance use disorders
