'''Somatic Comorbidities :''''

*Fluid and electrolyte disorders (hyponatremia, hypokalaemia, hypocalcaemia).
*Functional renal failure and oedema, especially associated with vomiting.
*<u>Hypertrophy of the salivary glands</u>, <u>dental erosions</u>, <u>peptic oesophagitis</u> and <u>Malory Weiss syndrome</u>.
*Weight gain (even exceptionally obesity).
*Poor nutritional status, even undernutrition.
*Osteoporosis, particularly if amenorrhoea.
*Pseudo-occlusive syndrome.

'''Psychiatric and addictive comorbidities: they are frequent:'''

*Substance use disorders,
*Characterised depressive episode and bipolar disorder,
*Borderline personality disorder,
*Anxiety disorders.
