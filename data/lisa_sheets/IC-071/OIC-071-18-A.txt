'''Somatic differential diagnoses:''''

* Certain brain tumours and forms of epilepsy.
* Kleine-Levin syndrome (associating periodic hypersomnia and hyperphagia).

'''Psychiatric differential diagnoses:''''

* Bulimic hyperphagia.
* Anorexia nervosa of the hyperphagic/purgative type.
* Characteristic depressive episode
* Borderline personality disorder.
