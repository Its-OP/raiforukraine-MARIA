ACG = most common vasculitis in adults

Hits 4 women for 1 man.


Pseudo-polyarthritis rheumatica (PPR) = 3 times more common than CAG.

Hits 4 women for 1 man.

PPR = present in 40 to 60% of CAG patients.


ACG = present in 20% of patients with PPR.
