- Onset before the age of 50.

- Involvement of the aorta and its main collaterals, the renal arteries, the arteries to the limbs and, more rarely, the cephalic arteries: '''limb claudication ([[Intermittent claudication of a limb SD-069|intermittent claudication of a limb]])''', '''vascular murmurs ([[Discovery of a vascular murmur SD-019|discovery of a vascular murmur]])''', abolition of a pulse, renal-vascular HTA...

- General signs: ''fever ([[Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|hyperthermia/fever]]), [[Asthenia SD-021|asthenia]], [[Weight loss SD-017|weight loss]], anorexia''.

- Rheumatological signs: '''[[Joint pain SD-067|joint pain]]''' and '''[[Myalgia SD-077|myalgia]]'''.

- Pain in the pathways of the large vessels, in particular carotidodynia.

- Inflammatory syndrome often modest or absent at the time of diagnosis.

- Vascular lesions (stenoses, aneurysms) common at the time of diagnosis.
