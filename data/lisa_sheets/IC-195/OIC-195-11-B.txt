PPR = diagnosis of elimination (no clinical, biological or imaging sign is specific for PPR)

'''Main differential diagnoses'''

Rheumatoid arthritis with a rheumatic onset (more common in the elderly).

Neoplasia (multiple myeloma, bone metastases, paraneoplastic pain syndrome).

Microcrystalline rheumatism with a rheumatic form (hydroxyapathy rheumatism, chondrocalcinosis).

Drug-induced muscle toxicity (e.g. statin).

Other vasculitides.

Myositis.

Endocarditis.

If no inflammatory syndrome: osteomalacia, hyperthyroidism.
