C'''orticothérapie ([[Prescrire des corticoïdes par voie générale ou locale SD-251|prescrire des corticoïdes par voie générale ou locale]])''' = constant and remarkable efficacy.

Symptoms disappear in 24 to 72 hours.

Biological inflammatory syndrome disappears in 2 to 4 weeks.

No answer = look for another cause.

Corticosteroid therapy is gradually reduced and weaned over 12-18 months.

Measures associated with corticosteroid therapy

Regular clinical and biological monitoring.

Good prognosis.


Possible relapses.
