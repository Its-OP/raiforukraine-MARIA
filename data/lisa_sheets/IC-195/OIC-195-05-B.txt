If typical presentation = virtually no differential diagnosis for CAG.

Frustrated forms of ACG = look for other causes of prolonged inflammatory syndrome ''([[Acute or chronic inflammatory syndrome SD-186|acute or chronic inflammatory syndrome]])'': infections (especially endocarditis++), neoplasia, thrombo-embolic pathology and other inflammatory pathologies).

If symptoms and clinical signs are not sensitive within 48 hours = look for other causes+++.

If NOIAA = main differential diagnosis: atheroma
