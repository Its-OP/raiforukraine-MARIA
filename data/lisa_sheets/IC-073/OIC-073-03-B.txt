==== Regulatory aspects ====

===== Who can be a psychotherapist? =====
- A regulated title (2010): National Register

- Automatic for :

o Psychiatrist

o Clinical psychologist

- Possible, but with theoretical training in psychopathology (> 400 hours)

and practical training (> 5 months) for :

o Non-psychiatrist

o Non-clinician psychologist

o Psychoanalyst

===== Reimbursement for psychotherapy =====
- Full reimbursement or free of charge

o Psychologists and psychiatrists in public psychiatric establishments (CMP, day hospitals)

- Full or partial reimbursement - depending on the reform of the care pathway (you need to consult your GP for optimum reimbursement)

o Private psychiatrists

o Private psychologists (from 1 January 2022)

- Non-reimbursement or partial reimbursement (mutuelles)

o Private psychologists and psychotherapists outside the care pathway
