==== Supportive psychotherapy ====
At the root of all psychotherapy, the most widely practised and least theorised form of psychotherapy.

Main objectives:

- Accompanying a patient through a stressful life situation (separation, professional difficulties, bereavement, etc.)

- Allowing painful emotions to be expressed and relieved

- Encouraging a change of perspective

- Restoring hope and boosting feelings of personal effectiveness

Practical details:

- Identifying and structuring patient requests

- Empathetic listening

- Reformulation

- Home/validation of emotions

- Support/encouragement/strengthening of personal resources
