The creatinine value is inversely related to the Glomerular Filtration Rate (GFR); it also depends on muscle production of creatinine.

As renal function deteriorates, creatinine levels increase.
Values considered "normal" are between 50 and 90 µmol/L in women and between 80 and 115 µmol/L in men.
