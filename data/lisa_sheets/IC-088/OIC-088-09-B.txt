In the event of dysphonia, imaging will be carried out in the following contexts:

- unilateral or bilateral laryngeal paralysis: examination of the thyroid gland and neck for adenopathy and injected CT scan from the base of the skull to the mediastinum

- laryngeal malignant lesion with infiltration: cervico-thoracic CT scan with injection of iodinated contrast product
