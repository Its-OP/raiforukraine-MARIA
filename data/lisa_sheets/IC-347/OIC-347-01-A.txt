== Definition of acute urinary retention ==
Complete and sudden inability to urinate despite an urgent or painful need to do so

== Know the main causes of acute urine retention ==
'''Mechanical obstruction:''''

* <u>In men</u> : [[2C-127|Benign prostatic hypertrophy 127 (BPH)]], [[2C-161|prostatitis 161]], [[2C-310|prostate cancer 310]]
* <u>In women</u> : [[2C-044|Severe genital prolapse 44]], [[2C-300|cervical cancer 300]]
* <u>In both</u>: [[2C-265|Lithiasis 265]]/bladder or urethral foreign body, fecal impaction, endovesical clotting, [[2C-314|bladder tumour 314]], [[2C-300|pelvic tumour 300]] and [[2C-301|301]], urethral stricture, [[2C-283|constipation 283]]

'''Detrusor or sphincter contraction abnormality:''''

* <u>Clapped bladder</u>
* [[2C-092|<u>Peripheral neuropathies</u> 92]] : Diabetic/alcoholic polyneuropathy, Guillain-Barr√© syndrome, Cauda equina syndrome, Postoperative (pelvic surgery)
