*Therapeutic emergency
*Emergency bladder drainage by bladder catheter or suprapubic catheter
*Assess endovesical volume on catheterisation
*Prevent vacuo haemorrhage (clamping every 500ml)
*Monitor and treat obstruction lifting syndrome: hourly monitoring of diuresis and intravenous fluid compensation.
*Outpatient treatment if there are no complications (haematuria, infection, obstruction syndrome, renal failure).
*In the event of complications, hospitalisation is required.
