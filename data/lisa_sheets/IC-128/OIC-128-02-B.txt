After the age of 50, 1 in 2 women and 1 in 5 men will suffer an osteoporotic fracture during their lifetime.

The incidence of fractures increases exponentially from the age of 50.

'''In France, 70,000 ESFs, around 35,000 wrist fractures and around 70,000 vertebral fractures occur every year''. ''' '''
