Clonal myeloid haemopathies affecting a medullary haematopoietic stem cell resulting in abnormalities in the medullary production of blood cells:

- both quantitative (anaemia, thrombocytopenia, neutropenia)

- and qualitative (morphological and functional abnormalities of blood cells)

Their overall incidence (around 4 cases per 100,000 inhabitants per year) increases with age, reaching 70 cases per 100,000 inhabitants per year between the ages of 70 and 80.

- The median age at diagnosis is between 65 and 70.

- 15-20% of anaemias in the elderly

- More common in men
