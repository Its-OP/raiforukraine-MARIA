===Bacterial infections===

======Tuberculose======
To be detected in new arrivals. Pulmonary tuberculosis by clinical examination and chest x-ray. Latent tuberculosis infection (LTBI) by gamma interferon test.

======Colonisation by multi-resistant bacteria (MRB) or highly resistant bacteria (HRB) ======
Screening by rectal swab on admission to hospital in France if hospitalised in the country of origin in the previous year.

===Viral infections===

======VIH-VHB-VHC======
Screening newcomers with serologies

===Parasitosis===
======Parasitoses intestinales======
First-time arrivals should be screened by PE. Diarrhoea, anaemia and hypereosinophilia are classic signs of these infections.

======Schistosomoses======
Screening of new arrivals by serology with, if positive, EPS and EPU (parasitological examination of faeces and urine) for eggs.
======Chagas disease======
Pregnant women born in Latin America. By serology.

===Ectoparasitosis===
Related to poverty: scabies

Hypertension-Diabetes-Sickle cell disease/haemoglobinopathies
