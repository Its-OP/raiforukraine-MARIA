In most cases, no causative etiological agent is found. Sometimes, three types of causal factors can be identified:

- Infectious agent: transforming virus (EBV, HTLV1, HHV8), or chronic infection (Helicobacter pylori, hepatitis C)

- Congenital or acquired immunosuppression (HIV+++)

- Autoimmune disease
