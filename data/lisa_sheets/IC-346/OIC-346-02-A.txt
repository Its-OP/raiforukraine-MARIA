- '''Simple febrile seizure if all of the following are present'''

o Age between 1 and 5 years

o Duration < 15 minutes

o A single seizure during the febrile episode

o Generalised crisis

o No post-critical deficit

o Children with no neurological history

o Normal neurological examination

'''- Complex febrile crisis if only one of the following elements is present'''

o Age < 1 year

o Duration > 15 minutes

o Seizures recurring during the febrile episode

o Focal seizure

o Presence of a post-critical deficit

o Children with a neurological history

o Abnormal neurological examination
