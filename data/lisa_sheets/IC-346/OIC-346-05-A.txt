o Ensuring a free airway

o Put in PLS

o Scoper

o If seizures have not subsided after 5 minutes: intra-rectal diazepam 0.5 mg/kg (max 10 mg)

o Positioning the approach

o If persistent 5 minutes after the first dose, repeat a dose of IV benzodiazepine (clonazepam) or intra-rectal diazepam 0.5 mg/kg if no access is available

o If persistent, see management of the malignant state (long-acting anticonvulsant phenytoin<ins> </ins>or phenobarbital)
