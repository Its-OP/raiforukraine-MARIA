     '''Signs during the seizure'''

o lasting more than 15 minutes or recurring seizure without return to normal consciousness - seizure lasting more than 5 minutes = threat of seizure requiring treatment

o Prolonged trouble with consciousness

o Respiratory problems (cyanosis, bradypnoea, congestion)

o Haemodynamic disorders (tachycardia, mottling, etc.)


'''- Signs outside the crisis'''

o Sepsis, purpura

o Persistence of disturbances of consciousness after 30 minutes = [[Coma in adults and children 2C-336-DE-A01|coma]]

o Pallor, multiple haematomas ([[Child abuse and children at risk: medical management 2C-057-PC-A02|abuse]])?
