<br />
{| class="wikitable"
|'''Examinations'''
|'''Objective'''
|-
|ECG
|Search for associated acute coronary syndrome
|-
|Blood ionogram
|Check for hyperkalaemia
|-
|Troponins
|If conduction disorder of ischaemic origin suspected
|-
|Cardiac ultrasound
|Search for underlying heart disease: reduced ejection fraction, tight aortic stenosis, argument for endocarditis, hypertrophy of ventricular walls (overload disease), etc.
|-
|Lyme serology
|If patient at risk of Lyme disease
|}
