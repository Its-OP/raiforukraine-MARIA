The risk factors for breast cancer are :

* age
* gynaeco-obstetrical history (factors of exposure to oestrogens):
** early menarche (< 12 years),
** late menopause (> 55 years),
** late age at 1st pregnancy (> 30 years) or nulliparity,
** absence of breast-feeding, ,
** exposure to hormonal treatments (oestroprogestogenic contraception and hormonal menopause treatment),
** personal history of breast cancer or mastopathy at risk (e.g. fibrocystic mastopathy with the presence of atypia - atypical hyperplasia, carcinoma in situ).

* a family history of breast or ovarian cancer, with possible knowledge of a genetic predisposition (germline mutation ''BRCA1, BRCA2'')
* High body mass index (BMI) or an increase in BMI during the menopause.
* Sedentary lifestyle (after the menopause)
* Alcohol and tobacco consumption
* Thoracic irradiation
* High socio-economic status
