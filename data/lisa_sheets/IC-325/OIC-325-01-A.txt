==Definition of an adverse drug reaction== (ADR)
An ''adverse drug reaction'' [ADR] is any suspected ''noxious'' and ''unintended'' response to a medicinal product that occurs under conditions of use consistent or inconsistent with the terms of the authorisation or registration of the medicinal product, including in cases of

* use without a marketing authorisation,
* overdose
* misuse,
* abuse,
* medication errors
* interaction,
* when taken during pregnancy or breast-feeding,
* occupational exposure.
