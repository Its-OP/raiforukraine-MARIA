== Characteristics of ADRs in elderly subjects ==

* Increased frequency and severity (co-morbidities, multiple medications, age-related pharmacokinetic changes)
* Varied symptomatology, atypical clinical presentations
* Main drugs involved: cardiovascular drugs, psychotropic drugs, anticoagulants, non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, etc.
