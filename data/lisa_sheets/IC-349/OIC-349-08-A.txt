* Fistulisation
* Spread of infection :
** Locally: phlegmon, cellulitis, arthritis, osteitis
** Distant: lymphangitis, septicaemia
* Recurrence


Even if these complications are rare:

* Uncontrolled infection: amputation
* Functional prognosis: tendon necrosis, sequelae of stiffness, nail dystrophy, etc.
