*'''Invasion phase''': asymptomatic, during which the germ responsible multiplies
*''Inflammatory or phlegmatic phase'': appearance of local inflammatory signs (pain, redness, heat, oedema), without waking up at night
*''Collected phase'': circumscribed area, pulsating, sleepless pain
*'''Complication phase''': fistulisation, spread of infection (arthritis, evolution of the panariasis towards phlegmon, purulent melting or tendon necrosis)
