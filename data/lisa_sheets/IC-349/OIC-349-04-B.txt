* The bites are ''animal or human''.
* Most often it is a polymicrobial infection.
* ''''' Pasteurella multocida''''' (gram-negative bacillus) is the most common germ in cat and dog bites. Infection is rapid, with inoculation occurring within 24 hours. This germ is sensitive to amoxicillin.
