This is an EMERGENCY with a risk to the functional prognosis of the hand requiring hospitalisation in a specialised facility for medical and surgical management.

* '''Systematic surgical management'''
** Excision of the portal of entry
** Incision proximal and distal to the sheath, more or less extensive throughout the sheath.
** Bacteriological sampling
** Abundant washing of the sheath with physiological saline.
** If present, excision of necrotic tissue
** Controlled wound healing
* Probabilistic antibiotic therapy subsequently adapted to the results of the swabs
* Physiotherapy: Mobilisation of the fingers
