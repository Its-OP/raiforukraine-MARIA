The main paraneoplastic syndromes are :

- Endocrinological: SIADH (inappropriate secretion of anti-diuretic hormone), cushingoid syndrome, hypercalcaemia, carcinoid syndrome, hypoglycaemia, Zollinger-Ellison syndrome, necrolytic erythema migrans, Weber Christian panniculitis, gynaecomastia;

- Neurological: subacute cerebellar ataxia, limbic encephalitis, subacute Denny-Brown sensory neuropathy, Lambert-Eaton myastheniform syndrome, myasthenia ;

- Haematological: haemolytic anaemia, polycythemia, thromboembolic venous disease, Trousseau syndrome, disseminated intravascular coagulation, thrombotic microangiopathy;

- Nephrology: extra-membranous glomerulonephritis;

- Immunological: dermatomyositis and polymyositis, Pierre-Marie's pneumonic hypertrophic osteoarthopathy, Stauffer's syndrome, paraneoplastic fever.
