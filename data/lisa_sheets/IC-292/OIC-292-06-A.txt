The main general clinical pictures suggestive of cancer are :

- Changes in general condition: asthenia, anorexia, weight loss, even malnutrition and sarcopenia;

- Fever and night sweats.

- Painful symptoms.

Other signs may be classified by system (vascular, neurological, digestive, respiratory, etc.) and correspond to tumour mass syndrome, paraneoplastic syndrome or cachectic and inflammatory syndrome.
