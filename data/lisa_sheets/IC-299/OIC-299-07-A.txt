'''Extension work-up:''' look for primary cancer (and other extra-encephalic systemic metastases)

- '''Clinical:'''

o Skin examination (melanoma)

o Pulmonary auscultation

o Breast palpation, adenopathy

- '''Imaging:'''

o ''Thoracic-abdominal-pelvic scan'', with injection of contrast medium

o +/- fluorodeoxyglucose PET scanner (<sup>18</sup>F)

o Mammography in women
