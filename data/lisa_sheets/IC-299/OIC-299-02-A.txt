- Primary brain tumour =''' intra-axial, developed within the brain itself (i.e. brain, trunk, cerebellum), e.g. glioblastoma

- Primary adnexal tumour = extra-axial, developed from the meninges and cranial nerves, e.g. meningioma and schwannoma/neuroma of the vestibular nerve.
