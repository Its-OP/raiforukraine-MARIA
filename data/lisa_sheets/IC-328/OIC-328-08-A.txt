'''Limb prosthesis''': a device that replaces a missing limb or limb segment

''NB: residual limb = remaining limb segment''

'''Components:'''
[[File:Prosthesis.png|frame|Femoral prosthesis]]

* Sleeve (slipped over the residual limb)
* Socket (accommodates residual limb + sleeve)
* Intermediate effectors
** Connecting pieces
*** Articular
*** Non-articular
* End effector
** foot, pestle...
** hand, hook...
* Aesthetic trim





''Pour aller plus loin, rang B'' '':'' '''Règles générales pour la rééducation des sujets amputés :'''

- Early fitting of orthopaedic devices as part of rehabilitation in a specialised centre

- Choice of equipment according to the patient's life plan

- Significant and early interactions with the patient's environment

- In the lower limb: fittings for functional purposes, rarely for aesthetic purposes

- For the upper limb: devices for functional and aesthetic purposes
