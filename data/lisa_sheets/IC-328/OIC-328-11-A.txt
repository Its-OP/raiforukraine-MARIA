'''([[Progressive loss of autonomy SDD-031|progressive loss of autonomy]])''''

'''Manual wheelchair:''''

- on prescription by any doctor

- purchase or rental, paid for by the health insurance scheme

- whether or not combined with the prescription of an anti-bedsore cushion


'''Electric wheelchair'''

- requires a trial to validate that the wheelchair is suitable for the disability and is not dangerous, by a PRM doctor + a physiotherapist or occupational therapist

- on simple prescription + trial certificate

- purchase only

- subject to prior agreement with the health insurance, additional financing possible by the MDPH '''([[Situation of disability SDD-345|situation of disability]])''''

- whether or not combined with the prescription of an anti-bedsore cushion
