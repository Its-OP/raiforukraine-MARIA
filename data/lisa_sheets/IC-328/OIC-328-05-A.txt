'''Orthosis''': a device that supplements a limb or segment of limb that is deficient or the spine.

It can be small or large equipment.


'''Objectives of orthotic treatment''' ''(different from orthopaedic treatment after trauma or surgery)'''

- downtime

- rest

- stabilisation

- correction

- substitution
