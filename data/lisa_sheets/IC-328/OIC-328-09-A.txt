Orthopaedic footwear :

*custom-made orthopaedic shoes = major orthopaedic appliances
**reimbursed from 60 to 100%, first pair prescribed by a specialist
**indicated if: neurological pathology, volumetric disorder, instability, inequality of length, limping... '''([[Sensory and/or motor neurological deficit SDD-121|sensory and/or motor neurological deficit]], [[Fall in the elderly SDD-027|fall in the elderly]]),''''
**carried out by a chiropodist or ortho-prosthetist
*''standard therapeutic footwear:'''
**CHUT (temporary use) or CHUP (permanent use)
**reimbursed at 60%, prescribed by any doctor
**indicated if: trophic disorders, oedema
*foot orthoses
**reimbursed at 60%,
**performed by a chiropodist, chiropodist or ortho-prosthetist.
