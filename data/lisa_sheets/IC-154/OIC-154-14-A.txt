|Title=Know the principles of treatment for acute bronchitis (no antibiotics, no non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), no cough suppressants).
|Description=None
|Rubric=Management
|Contributors=
|Order=14}}
Treatment of acute bronchitis is symptomatic only.

There is no indication for antibiotic therapy

Non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) and cough suppressants should be avoided.
