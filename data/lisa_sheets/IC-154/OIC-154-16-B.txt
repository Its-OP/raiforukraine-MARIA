'''Complication of acute community-acquired pneumonia (CAP):'''

*Loco-regional complications:
**pleural complications:
***uncomplicated para-pneumonic effusion
***complicated para-pneumonic effusion = purulent pleurisy = pleural empyema
**pulmonary complications
***abscess
***atelectasis
*General complications :
**decompensation of an underlying chronic condition'', including respiratory insufficiency
**septic complications": meningitis, abscess, septic shock, etc.

'''Complications of bronchiolitis in infants'''

- apnoea,

- atelectasis,

- bronchopulmonary superinfections,

- Acute bacterial otitis media.
