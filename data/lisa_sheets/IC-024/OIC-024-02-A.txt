'''Attention+++ :'''

- '''prevention of rhesus alloimmunisation'''-D

- Importance of ''information'' given to the patient - ''reassurance''.

- Psychological care offered

After discussion with the patient, it is possible to consider expulsion of the terminated pregnancy. Two methods are possible:

- medical treatment (prostaglandins) for small bags and minor haemorrhages

- surgical treatment'' in other cases.

- hospitalisation, pre-operative assessment and anaesthetic consultation

- under general or local anaesthetic

- dilatation of the cervix, aspiration with a cannula + anatomopathological examination of the aspiration product

- Checking uterine vacuity on ultrasound

- out the same day or the following day.
