Menace d'accouchement prématuré (MAP) = association de '''modifications cervicales''' et de '''contractions utérines''' (CU) régulières et douloureux '''avant 37 SA,''' soit entre '''22 SA et 36 SA + 6 jours.''' ''(Cf. [[Uterine contraction in a pregnant woman SD-098|09. Uterine contractions in a pregnant woman]] / [[Pelvic pain SD-099|99. Pelvic pain]])''''

Regarding changes to the cervix: assessed by TV and/or ultrasound of the cervix (more objective and reproducible vs TV. A shortened cervix is defined by a length < 25 mm.

If the birth occurs before 22 weeks' gestation, it is known as a "late miscarriage".
