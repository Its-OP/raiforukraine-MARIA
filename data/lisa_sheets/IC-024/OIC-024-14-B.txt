- enhanced monitoring: self-monitoring of blood pressure, consultation every 10 days or so, regular biological check-up, monthly ultrasound scan

- a "Pregnancy and hypertension" follow-up booklet to optimise the care pathway between the various healthcare professionals involved in follow-up

- at 9 months, the possibility of artificially inducing labour depending on obstetric conditions.
