== Extradural causes ==

* Degenerative: herniated disc +++ (most frequent cause); osteoarthritic stenosis of the lumbar spinal canal/narrow lumbar canal (more progressive).
* Tumour: vertebroepidural metastases of osteophilic cancers (lung, breast, prostate, kidney), primary bone tumours (chordoma), haematological malignancies (plasmacytoma, multiple myeloma).
* Infectious: spondylodiscitis with epiduritis
* Vascular: epidural haematoma (anticoagulant treatment)

== Intradural causes ==
neuroma, meningioma, terminal filum ependymoma, subdural haematoma
