==Extradural causes:==

*Tumour: vertebroepidural metastases of osteophilic cancers (lung, breast, prostate, kidney), primary bone tumours (chordoma), haematological malignancies (plasmacytoma, multiple myeloma of the bones, multiple myeloma of the bones).
*Degenerative: cervico-arthrosic myelopathy, herniated disc (especially thoracic)
*Infectious: spondylodiscitis with epiduritis ([Osteoarticular infections (OAI) of children and adults|Osteoarticular infections of children and adults]])
*Vascular: epidural haematoma (anticoagulant treatment)

==Extramedullary intradural causes:==

*Tumour: meningioma and neuroma
*Inflammatory: arachnoiditis (post-meningitis, neurosarcoidosis, post-dural surgery, etc.)

==Intramedullary intradural causes:==

*Tumour: ependymoma, astrocytoma, metastasis
*Hydraulic: syringomyelia
*Vascular: arteriovenous malformation (AVM), cavernoma, dural arteriovenous fistula, subdural haematoma, etc.
