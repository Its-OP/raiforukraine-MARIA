* Diagnostic and therapeutic urgency: any delay in treatment compromises the functional prognosis.
* The degree of urgency depends on the kinetics of the onset of symptoms: the more rapid the worsening, the more rapid the treatment.
* As a general rule :
** Acute course: malignant vertebroepidural tumour, infectious epiduritis, epidural haematoma.
** Subacute: intramedullary intradural tumour, dural arteriovenous fistula.
** Chronic: cervico-arthrosic myelopathy, syringomyelia, slow-growing benign extramedullary intradural tumour (meningioma, neuroma).
