*MRI of the lumbosacral spine, ''T2'' (Figure 01) and ''T1'' (Figure 02) sequences.
*''Sagittal section'' (Figure 01): herniation of the L5-S1 intervertebral disc excluded.
*''Axial section'' (Figure 02): compression of the dural sac and the left S1 spinal root (arrowhead), which is laminated and compressed dorsally.
