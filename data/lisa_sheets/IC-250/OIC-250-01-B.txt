Undernutrition affects people of all ages.

It can accompany :

all pathologies, acute or chronic, organic or psychiatric.

situations of socio-economic insecurity


Prevalence in hospitals :

10% of children

30% of adults

50% of elderly subjects (> 70 years old)


Prevalence outside hospital

           4% of French people aged 70 to 79 living at home

10% after age 80


The overall prevalence of undernutrition in France concerns almost 3 million individuals.
