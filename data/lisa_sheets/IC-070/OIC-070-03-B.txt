Known as "psychological and behavioural symptoms of dementia" (BPSD)

Present in over 80% of patients with Alzheimer's disease or other dementias. Early onset.

           Negative symptomatology: withdrawal and apathy 50% prevalence

Frequent psychotic symptoms: delusions, hallucinations
