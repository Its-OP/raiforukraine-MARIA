'''Cannabis''':

- From the Cannabis Sativa plant.

- 3 forms: herb (marijuana, weed); resin (hashish, shit); oil (rarer)

- Smoked (joints, water pipe or bong), vaporised (tobacco-free), vaped or ingested (space cake)

'''Cocaine''':

- Intranasal (sniff) or intravenous (injection, slam, shoot) for cocaine hydrochloride and inhaled (smoke) for base (very rarely injected after solubilisation).

'''Amphetamines''' :

.       Oral (gulp), intranasal (snort), intravenous, inhaled

'''Opiates/opioids''':

- Inhalation (smoking), intranasal (sniffing), intravenous (shoot, fix), ingested (tablets, syrup)

'''New synthetic products''' :

- Consumed for their psychoactive effects, which mimic those of illicit psychoactive substances such as stimulants (cocaine, amphetamines) and cannabis.

.      Oral (drop), intranasal, intravenous (slam), inhaled, intrarectal (plug) use
