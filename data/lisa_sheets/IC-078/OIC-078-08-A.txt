'''Cannabis''':

- Craving, tolerance, withdrawal syndrome

.      Cognitive disorders

- Amotivation syndrome: emotional indifference, disinvestment in social relationships, reduced performance at work or school

'''Cocaine''':

.      Craving, tolerance, withdrawal syndrome

.      Cognitive disorders

'''Amphetamines''' :

- Psychiatric manifestations: asthenia, weight loss, sleep disorders, anxiety, dysphoria, transient psychotic symptoms with hallucinations, delusions and behavioural disorders.

- Somatic manifestations: damage to certain organs (kidneys, stomach, intestine); pulmonary oedema, heart rhythm disorders; dry mouth

- Tolerance phenomenon, addictive risk +++.

'''Opiates/opioids''' :

.     Craving, tolerance, withdrawal syndrome

.      Cognitive disorders

'''New synthetic products''':

- Addictive potential with craving and tolerance

- Withdrawal syndrome (e.g. MDPV and mephedrone): sad mood, anxiety, sleep disorders, anhedonia, anergy, asthenia, concentration problems, palpitations and headaches.

- Craving, anhedonia and anergy for several weeks.
