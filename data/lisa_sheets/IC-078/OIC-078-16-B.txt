'''Cannabis/Cocaine/ Amphetamines/New synthetic products:'''

.    Symptomatic, with prescription of histaminergic anxiolytics such as hydroxyzine (as first-line treatment) in cases of major anxiety or agitation, and antipsychotics as second-line treatment or in cases of psychotic symptoms.

- Motivational interviewing +++

'''Opiates/opioids:'''

.    Symptomatic treatment: non-opioid analgesics, antispasmodics, antiemetics, antidiarrhoeals, non-benzodiazepine anxiolytics such as sedative antipsychotics (cyamemazine) or antihistamines (hydroxyzine).

.     Initiation of opioid substitution treatment: the majority and preferred choice.

- Motivational interviewing +++
