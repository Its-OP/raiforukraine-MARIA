Harmful use :

. consumption of a psychoactive substance that is harmful to health

. complications which may be physical or psychological, but without the criteria of dependence (the patient does not know the links between harm and consumption) ;

<nowiki>*</nowiki> Dependency:

. defines substance use disorder

. consists of a set of behavioural, cognitive and physiological phenomena in which the use of a specific psychoactive substance or category of substances leads to a progressive loss of interest in other activities.
