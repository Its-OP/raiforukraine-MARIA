'''Cannabis:'''

- Problem use or dependence concerns 3% of 18-64 year olds and 7% of 17 year olds.

'''Cocaine:'''

- 400,000 people affected by cocaine use in France

.       5.6% of adults have tried cocaine and 1.6% are current users 2.8% of 17-year-olds have tried cocaine

'''Amphetamines:'''

.       5% of adults have tried MDMA/ecstasy

. 0.3% of the population aged 18-64 have tried amphetamines

'''Opiates/opioids:'''

- 1.3% of adults have tried heroin 0.2% are current users 0.7% of 17-year-olds have tried it

.      45,000 people treated for opioid dependence in addiction centres over the course of the year

- 180,000 people receiving opioid substitution treatment in towns and specialist centres

'''New synthetic products:''''

.      286 new synthetic products registered in France since 2008, including 16 in 2018

.       1.3% of 18-64 year olds have experimented with synthetic cannabinoids
