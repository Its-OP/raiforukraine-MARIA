'''Cannabis:'''

# Confusional syndrome

- Installation in a few hours or days

- Fluctuating trends over the day

- Symptoms: disturbance of consciousness, attention, memory, disorientation, disturbance of language or disturbance of perception.

2. Psychiatric complications

- Induced psychotic disorder: onset during or shortly after acute cannabis intoxication (up to one month after intoxication); clinical symptomatology of brief psychotic disorder; treatment involving cessation of intoxication and antipsychotics; potentially serious disorder with the same evolutionary risks as any brief psychotic disorder.

- Panic attacks

- Characteristic depressive episode
