'''Main causes of sensorineural hearing loss (in adults)'''

#Unilateral deafness
#*sudden unilateral deafness
#*Acoustic neuroma (= vestibular schwannoma)
#*post-traumatic deafness (translabyrinthine rock fracture, labyrinthine concussion)
#*labyrinthitis (postoperative, complication of acute or chronic otitis media)
#*Menière's disease and other pressure disorders of the inner ear (may be bilateral)
#Bilateral deafness
#*Presbycusis
#*genetic deafness (progressive)
#*acute or chronic sound trauma
#*toxicity (aminoglycosides, antimitotics, quinine, retinoids)
