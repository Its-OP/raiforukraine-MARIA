'''Adults :'''

*earwax plug
*chronic non-cholesteatomatous ear (ossicular damage, tympanosclerosis, large tympanic perforation)
*cholesteatoma
*otosclerosis
*ossicular trauma, rock fracture (extralabyrinthic)
*external or middle ear tumours
*seromucous ear (rarer than in children, think of cavum cancer)

'''Children:'''

*seromucous ear
*earwax plug
*chronic non-cholesteatomatous ear
*cholesteatoma
*ossicular trauma, rock fracture (extralabyrinthic)
*ear malformations or aplasia
