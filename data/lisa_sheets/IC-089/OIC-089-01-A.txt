'''Pure tone audiometry''' = subjective measurement of the '''detection threshold of pure tones between 125 and 8000 Hz''', presented in silence, in bone and air conduction for each ear.

Determines the type of hearing loss (sensorineural, conductive or mixed) and the level of hearing impairment<br />.
