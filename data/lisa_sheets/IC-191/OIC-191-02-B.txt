<small>Invasive fungal infections occur preferentially in patients with one or more risk factors:</small>

<small>- '''Neutropenia (PNN <0.5G/L) > 7 days''' ''(Aspergillosis, Candidaemia)''</small>

<small>- '''Central vascular catheter (parenteral nutrition)''' ''(Candidemia)'''</small>

<small>- '''Prior colonization''' ''(Candidémie)''</small>

<small>- '''Prior antibiotic therapy''' ''(Candidemia)''</small>

<small>- '''Major abdominal surgery''' ''(Candidemia)''</small>

<small>- '''Renal insufficiency''' ''(Candidemia)''</small>

<small>- '''Severity of disease''' ''(Candidemia)''</small>

<small>- '''Length of stay in intensive care'''''(Candidémie)'''</small>

<small>- '''Toxicomanie IV''' ''(Candidémie)''</small>

<small>- '''Corticotherapy''' ''(Candidaemia, pneumocystis...)''</small>

<small>- '''HIV infection with CD4<200/mm<sup>3</sup>''': ''candidemia, pneumocystis CD4<100/mm<sup>3</sup>: cryptococcosis...''</small>

<small>- '''Allograft patient''' ''(Candidaemia, pneumocystis, aspergillosis...)''</small>

'''<small>-</small>''' <small>''Organ transplant'''</small> (''<small>pneumocystis, aspergillosis, candidemia, ...)</small>'''
