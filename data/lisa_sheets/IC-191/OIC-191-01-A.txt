<big>Definition neutropenia: PNN < 1500/mm3 but the infectious risk is major if PNN < 500/mm3</big>.

<big>The definition of '''febrile neutropenia''' is therefore:</big>.

<big>- '''Fever''': T°≥38°C on 2 occasions or for more than 1 hour or any T°≥38.3°C)</big>

<big>- In a patient with '''Neutrophil Polynuclei (PNN) <0.5G/L'''</big>

<big>- '''It's a therapeutic emergency'''</big>
