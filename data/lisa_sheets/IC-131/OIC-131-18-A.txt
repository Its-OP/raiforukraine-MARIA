# Begin by eradicating the acute aetiological trigger and/or improving the treatment of the chronic pathology, where possible.
# Educate the patient to break down the time spent in orthostasis
# Use of class 2 venous restraint (even if the level of evidence is low)
# Proper hydration
# Review the prescription with all treatments

Secondly, discuss the introduction of midodrine (by a vasoconstriction mechanism) or fludrocortisone (by haemodynamic inflation, but off-label), respecting the contraindications and explaining the main side-effects.
