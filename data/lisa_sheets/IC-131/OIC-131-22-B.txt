'''Drafting a standard physiotherapy prescription for an elderly post-fall patient'''

           - Motor physiotherapy twice a week (often at home initially)

           - WALKING rehabilitation (possibly with technical aids, walker, cane)

           - Work on proximal MUSCULATION of the lower limbs and spine

           - Working on BALANCE

           - TRANSFER work (bed-chair-toilet)

           - Lifting from the ground in the event of a fall

           - URGENT TREATMENT (to avoid waiting for CPAM prior agreement)
