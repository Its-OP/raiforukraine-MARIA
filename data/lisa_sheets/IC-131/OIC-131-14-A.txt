'''Traumatic'''

- contusions, haematomas, wounds, dislocations,

- bone fractures,

- intracranial haemorrhage.

'''Linked to the extended ground station:''''

- rhabdomyolysis,

- renal failure, dehydration

- hyperkalaemia

- inhalation pneumonitis, hypothermia

- bedsores

- thromboembolic venous disease,

'''Psychological:'''

- post-fall syndrome or psychomotor maladjustment syndrome

- confusion

. functional decline
