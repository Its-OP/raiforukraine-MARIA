The incidence of urinary lithiasis varies from country to country, depending on geographical, climatic, ethnic, dietary and genetic factors. The prevalence of urolithiasis varies from 1% to 20% worldwide. In France, the prevalence is estimated at almost 10%.

The sex ratio is 2 men to 1 woman.
