The symptoms associated with a urinary calculus, other than renal colic, are :

*none: asymptomatic stone discovered by chance
*[[Haematuria|haematuria 260]], rather of effort. macroscopic or microscopic
*[[Urinary infections of children and adults|urinary infections 161]] (pyelonephritis)
*[[Chronic renal insufficiency in adults and children|renal insufficiency 264]] (chronic obstruction and coralliform stones)
