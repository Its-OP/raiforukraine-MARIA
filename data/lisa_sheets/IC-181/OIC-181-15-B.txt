Vigilance of tattooing products, or tatouvigilance, is one of the health vigilances handled by the French National Agency for the Safety of Medicines and Health Products (ANSM).

This is the continuous process of collecting, identifying, evaluating and investigating potential adverse events linked to the use of tattoo products.
