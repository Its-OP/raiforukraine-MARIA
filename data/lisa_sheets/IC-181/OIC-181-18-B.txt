Toxicovigilance is one of the monitoring activities carried out by the French National Agency for Food, Environmental and Occupational Health and Safety (ANSES).

It involves monitoring the toxic effects on humans of exposure to a mixture or a natural or synthetic substance available on the market or present in the environment.

Toxicovigilance includes the monitoring activities carried out by poison control centres.
