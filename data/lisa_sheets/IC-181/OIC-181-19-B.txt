Nutrivigilance is one of the vigilance activities carried out by the Agence Nationale de Sécurité Sanitaire de l'alimentation de l'environnement et du travail (ANSES).

It covers the monitoring of adverse reactions in humans linked to the consumption of foodstuffs, including food supplements.
