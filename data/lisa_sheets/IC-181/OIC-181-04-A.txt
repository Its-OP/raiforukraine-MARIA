Human health products include medicines, biological products, medical devices, cosmetics and tattoo products.

All medicinal products are covered, whether before or after marketing authorisation. This includes raw materials, blood-derived medicinal products, narcotics, psychotropic drugs, vaccines, homeopathic products, herbal products and magistral and hospital preparations.

Biological products include organs, tissues and cells used for therapeutic purposes, cell and gene therapy products, labile blood products and ancillary therapeutic products (e.g. graft preservation media).

Medical devices include all devices used for therapeutic or diagnostic purposes (including in vitro diagnostics) and used on technical platforms.
