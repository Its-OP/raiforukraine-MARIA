Reactovigilance is one of the health vigilance measures managed by the French National Agency for the Safety of Medicines and Health Products (ANSM).

This is the continuous process of collecting, identifying, evaluating and investigating potential adverse events linked to the use of in vitro diagnostic devices (biological analyses and laboratory reagents).
