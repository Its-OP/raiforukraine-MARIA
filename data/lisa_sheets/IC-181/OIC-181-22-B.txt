The French National Agency for Food, Environmental and Occupational Health Safety (ANSES) is responsible for monitoring, assessment, research and reference in various areas of human, animal and plant health. In particular, it manages declarations of toxicovigilance and nutrivigilance, and is also responsible for veterinary pharmacovigilance and phytopharmacovigilance.
