|'''Imidazoles''''

'''(molecule: metronidazole)''''
|Infections caused by susceptible anaerobic bacteria, prevention of post-operative infections caused by anaerobic bacteria, amoebiasis, urogenital trichomoniasis, non-specific vaginitis, giardiosis, treatment of uncomplicated Clostridoides difficile colitis.
|Hypersensitivity to the active substance, to other 5-nitroimidazoles or to one of the excipients.
|Anaerobes (except ''Actinomyces'' and ''Cutibacterium),'' antiparasitic activity (''Entamoeba histolytica, Giardia intestinalis, Trichomonas vaginalis'')
|Antabuse effect with alcohol, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, glossitis, stomatitis, metallic taste, headache, neuropathy.


Interactions: amiodarone, barbiturates, busulfan, carbamazepine, cimetidine, coumarin derivatives, cyclosporine, lithium, phenytoin
