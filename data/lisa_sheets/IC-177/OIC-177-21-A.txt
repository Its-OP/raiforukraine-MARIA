Some antibiotics are particularly prone to bacterial resistance

- combination of amoxicillin and clavulanic acid

- cephalosporins: greater concern for products administered orally than by injection; greater concern for third- and fourth-generation cephalosporins, and for the "other cephalosporins" category; concern for ceftriaxone

- fluoroquinolones

- temocillin
