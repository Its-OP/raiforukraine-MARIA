Urinary tract infections: Escherichia coli

Skin infections: Staphylococcus aureus and Streptococcus pyogenes

ENT infections: Group A Streptococci or "Streptococcus pyogenes" (angina), "Streptococcus pneumoniae", "Haemophilus influenzae" (acute otitis media), anaerobes (in particular "Fusobacterium" in severe mastoiditis).

Meningitis: neonatal (''E. coli'' K1, ''Streptococcus agalactiae'', and much more rarely ''Listeria monocytogenes''), infant and child (''Neisseria meningitidis'', ''S. pneumoniae'')
