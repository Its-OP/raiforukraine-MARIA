A- Inhibitors of bacterial wall synthesis

* beta-lactams: penicillins, carbapenems, monobactams, cephalosporins
* fosfomycin
* glycopeptides
* lipopeptides
* polymyxins

B- Protein synthesis inhibitors

* aminoglycosides
* macrolides and related agents
* phenicols
* cyclines
* oxazolidinones
* fusidic acid

C- Inhibitors of nucleic acid synthesis

* quinolones
* mupirocin
* rifamycin

D- Folic acid synthesis inhibitors

* sulphonamides

E- Other mechanisms

* nitrofurans
* nitro-imidazoles
* antituberculosis drugs
