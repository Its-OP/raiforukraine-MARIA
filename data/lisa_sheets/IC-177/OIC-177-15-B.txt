'''(molecules: vancomycin, teicoplanin)''''
|Serious MRSA infections, serious meti-S staphylococcal infections in patients allergic to penicillin M, serious streptococcal, enterococcal and pneumococcal infections in patients allergic to 1st-line beta-lactam antibiotics, febrile episodes in neutropenic patients, treatment of C. difficile infections.
|Hypersensitivity to vancomycin, breast-feeding.
|Gram-positive bacteria: streptococci, pneumococci, enterococci, MSSA, MRSA, other staphylococci, listeria, C. difficile.
|Venous intolerance (phlebitis, endoveinitis), erythroderma if infused too rapidly (red man syndrome), nephrotoxicity, ototoxicity.


Interactions: oral anticoagulants
