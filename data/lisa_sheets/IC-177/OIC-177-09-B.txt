|'''Aminosides'''

'''(molecules: gentamicin, amikacin)''''
|Antibiotics to be used in combination

Serious infections with aerobic gram-negative bacilli, serious infections with P. aeruginosa, streptococcal/enterococcal endocarditis, meningitis with Listeria monocytogenes.
|Hypersensitivity to the active substance or to one of the excipients, grade B and C cirrhosis, myasthenia gravis.
|Meti-S staphylococci, L. monocytogenes, aerobic Gram-negative bacteria


Low-level resistance in streptococci and enterococci: efficacy of dual therapy
|Nephrotoxicity, cochleovestibular toxicity (irreversible)


Interactions: nephrotoxic, ototoxic drugs, ataluren, polymyxin B
