The first thing to do is to assess the impact of vomiting on hydration levels and manage any dehydration.

Treatment is primarily aetiological (see [[Understanding the diagnostic approach to vomiting in children 2C-274-DP-A01|Understanding the diagnostic approach to vomiting in children 2C-274-DP-A01]]).
