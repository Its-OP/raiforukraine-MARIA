== Blood smear ==
=> Activated mononuclear cells, characterised by a ''large size and hyperbasophilic cytoplasm = polymorphic cells, sometimes'' described as ''atypical'' = activated T lymphocytes.

=> Absence of blast cells

=> Other haematopoietic lineages are normal.
