== '''Allergic drug reaction ([[Suspicion of an adverse drug reaction or treatment SD-348|suspicion of an adverse drug reaction or treatment]])''' ==


- Drug Rash with hyperEosinophilia and Systemic Symptoms (DRESS) syndrome

'''-'''' Look for medication taken in the previous 6 weeks.

- Clinical signs :

- Evocative skin rash: ''[[Erythema SD-085|erythema]]''''

- Polyadenopathies '''([[Single or multiple adenopathies SD-016|single or multiple adenopathies]])''' ;

- General signs: '''[[Hyperthermia/fever SD-044|hyperthermia/fever]]''' ;

- Associated biological signs :

- '''[[Hypereosinophilia SD-219|Hypereosinophilia]]''';

- Hepatic cytolysis ([[Elevation of transaminases without associated cholestasis SD-206|elevation of transaminases without associated cholestasis]]);''''

- Impaired renal function.


- Drugs frequently incriminated: '''antibiotics'' (sulphonamides and beta-lactams), '''anti-convulsants'' and '''allopurinol.'''
<br />

== '''Autoimmune diseases''' ==


- Possible in systemic lupus, rheumatoid arthritis

- Look for other causes (in particular viral or drug-related causes).
