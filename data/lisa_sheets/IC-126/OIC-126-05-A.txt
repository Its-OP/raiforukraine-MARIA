== Description : ==

*Priapism is the existence of a pathological erection for more than 4 hours in the absence of any sexual stimulation.
*Its main causes are: iatrogenic (intracavernous injections for erectile dysfunction), haematological (sickle cell anaemia, leukaemia), toxic (antipsychotics, cocaine), traumatic (pelvic trauma), pelvic tumours (prostate, bladder).
