Malignant skin tumours include tumours of epithelial origin such as basal cell or squamous cell carcinomas, which develop from keratinocytes, and melanoma, which is a malignant melanocytic tumour.

Melanoma is a malignant tumour that develops at the expense of melanocytes (cells that produce melanin).
