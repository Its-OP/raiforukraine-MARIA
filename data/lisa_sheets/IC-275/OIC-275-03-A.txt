'''Associated signs suggestive of [[Hepatomegaly and abdominal mass|hepatopathy]]''''

- '''[[Hepatomegaly SD-006|hepatomegaly]]''''

- signs of portal hypertension (collateral venous circulation, ascites)

- signs of hepatocellular insufficiency (stellate angiomas, palmar erythrosis, ''[[Jaundice SD-047|icterus]]'', ''[[foetor hepaticus]]'', signs of hypogonadism, signs of encephalopathy: asterixis, ''[[Mental confusion/disorientation SD-119|mental confusion/disorientation]]''').
