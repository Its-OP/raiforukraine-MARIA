HSV : HSV2 is a sexually transmitted infection (STI), 15 to 20% prevalence in the adult population, 90% in sex workers and people infected with HIV.

HSV1 infection occurs in childhood, most often between the ages of 1 and 4 (80% of children and 90% of adults are seropositive).

VZV: 90% of infections occur between the ages of 1 and 14; severe in adults, pregnant women and immunocompromised patients, clinical reactivation in 20% of the population.
