Main treatments : Aciclovir ointment, Aciclovir IV, Valaciclovir PO (metabolic precursor of aciclovir) or Famciclovir PO.

Risk of acute renal failure due to precipitation of crystals in the renal tubules, neuropsychological disorders (headaches, dizziness, agitation, confusion or even encephalopathy).

If pregnant, aciclovir is preferable.
