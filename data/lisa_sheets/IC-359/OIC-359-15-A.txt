Causes:

- Infectious ++: viral or bacterial pneumopathy, bronchiolitis, whooping cough, laryngitis, etc.

- Malformative: tracheal stenosis, tracheal compression

- Acute heart failure

- Upper airway obstruction

- Spontaneous or secondary pneumothorax (underlying respiratory pathology, trauma)

- Specific to newborns: inhalation of meconium fluid, transient respiratory distress, hyaline membrane disease, neonatal infection, etc.
