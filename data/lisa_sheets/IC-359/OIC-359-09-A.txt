o Arterial blood gases

o Chest X-ray

o ECG

o Other (etiological orientation and impact): blood ionogram, creatinine, urea, liver function tests, CBC, haemostasis test

- For paediatric patients

o Venous or capillary blood gas

o Chest X-ray

- COPD decompensation

- OAP

o Blood ionogram, troponin, CPK

o Echocardiography

- EP

o Thoracic angioscan

o Venous Doppler of the lower limbs

o Echocardiography

o D-dimer, Troponin, BNP (or NT-proBNP)

- Pneumonia

o Microbiological samples (depending on severity: ECBC; blood cultures; legionella and pneumococcal antigenuria; multiplex nasopharyngeal PCR; legionella, mycoplasma and chlamydia serologies)

- Asthma

o Microbiological samples

o Search for therapeutic non-compliance

o Search for exposure: tobacco, allergens

o Search for drug use

o Search for GERD

- Bronchiolitis :

o No systematic additional examination

o Chest X-ray +/- blood gas in the most severe forms
