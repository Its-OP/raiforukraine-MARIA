Amenorrhoea is the absence of menstruation.

There are two types:

*primary amenorrhoea" which is defined as an absence of menstruation after the age of 15 despite normal development of secondary sexual characteristics.
*Secondary amenorrhoea" corresponds to a cessation of cycles lasting more than three months in a previously menstruating woman. The term "primary-secondary amenorrhoea" is also used in the case of a single menstrual episode followed by amenorrhoea.

According to the WHO, a normal menstrual cycle lasts 25 to 35 days. Menstrual cycle disorders SD-094|troubles du cycle menstruel]]'' are very often due to a lack of ovulation.

Spaniomenorrhoea" refers to cycles lasting more than 45 days.
