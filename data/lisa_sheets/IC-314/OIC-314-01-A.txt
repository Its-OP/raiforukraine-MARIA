*Urothelium is the lining epithelium of the entire urinary tract, so urothelial carcinoma can affect, synchronously or metachronously:
**calyces
**pyelons
**ureters
**bladder
**urethra

*A bladder tumour is a urothelial carcinoma in over 95% of cases.
