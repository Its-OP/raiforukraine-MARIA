*Industrial carcinogens 182]]: the discovery of a bladder tumour should systematically suggest [[occupational exposure 184]].
**Polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (''table 16 bis'' of the general social security scheme and '''35 bis'' of the agricultural scheme)
**tars, oils and coal tar pitch
**coal combustion soot
**metallurgy

*Aromatic amines and N-nitroso-dibutylamine (Table 15 ter of the General Social Security Scheme):
**colouring agents (dyeing)
**rubber industry (car tyres)
**plastics industry (manufacture of plastics)
**pharmaceutical industry (laboratories)
