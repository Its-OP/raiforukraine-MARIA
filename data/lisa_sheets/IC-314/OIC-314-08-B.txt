'''TVNIM :'''

Uro-CT is indicated in cases of macroscopic haematuria, multiple tumours, trigone tumours, high-risk tumours (T1, high grade, CIS) or high-grade urinary cytology. Uro-CT can be used to rule out a synchronous lesion of the upper urinary tract.

'''TVIM :'''

Thoraco-abdomino-pelvic CT scan with injection of contrast medium (with late stage: uro-CT scan) is recommended as part of the extension assessment. Recommendations AFU Oncology Committee 2022-2024
