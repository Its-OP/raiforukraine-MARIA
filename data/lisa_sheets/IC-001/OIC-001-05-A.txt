|Title=Knowing the notion of representation of the disease
|Description=Know that the patient's representations may differ from those of the doctor.
|Rubric=Definition
|Contributors=
|Order=5}}
The meeting between the carer and the patient concerning an illness is a meeting of representations, i.e. the image that each has of this illness. The representations of the doctor and the patient may differ as a result of their personal positive or negative experiences. The representations of the disease have biomedical or psychosocial consequences, the impact or the vision of the patient's entourage, etc.
