Vaccine protection is based both on humoral immunity through the production of antibodies designed to destroy the antigen and on cellular immunity through the activation of T lymphocytes (cytotoxic CD8 which destroy infected cells and helper CD4 which stimulate the production of antibodies by B lymphocytes).
