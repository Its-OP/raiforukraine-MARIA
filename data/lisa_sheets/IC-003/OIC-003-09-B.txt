The '''therapeutic approach''' is part of the broader medical approach.

- '''Naming the clinical situation'' (e.g. results of a consultation in general practice)

- '''Decide''', in a '''negotiated''' way with the patient, according to :

o The clinical situation described.

o Patient preferences and characteristics.

o Characteristics of the doctor.

o Economic factors.
