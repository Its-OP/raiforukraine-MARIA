- Within the framework of the ethics of care, the doctor is careful to ''work in collegiality''. Considering the limits of his knowledge, he will seek the advice of other recognised health professionals in fields with which he is less familiar.

- Faced with a complex situation involving ethical issues, he or she will be able to create a "climate of interdisciplinary deliberation" that can include doctors, carers, family and friends, and of course the patient.
