- '''Loi Clayes- Leonetti''''

- Any person over the age of 18 may appoint a "trusted support person", who may be a parent, relative or the doctor treating them, and who will be consulted in the event that they are unable to express their wishes and receive the necessary information to do so.

- Testifies to ''the expression of the person's wishes''. His or her testimony prevails over any other testimony.

- The trusted support person will be able to ensure that the advance directives are respected.
