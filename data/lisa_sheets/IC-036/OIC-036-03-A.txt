Progestin-only contraception involves the administration of small doses of progestins.

=== Available routes of administration ===

==== Oral route ====
§ Levonorgestrel: to be taken every day

§ Desogestrel: take every day

§ Drospirenone: to be taken 24 days placebo 4 days

==== Subcutaneous use ====
§ Etonogestrel delivered continuously for 3 years.

==== Intrauterine route ====
§ Levonorgestrel delivered into the uterine cavity; three doses; duration of action 5 years or 3 years for the smallest size
