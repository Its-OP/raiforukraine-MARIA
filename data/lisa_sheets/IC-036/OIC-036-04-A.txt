=== Administration of high doses of Levonorgestrel (1.5 mg) ===

* single dose
* taken orally
* as soon as possible (within 72 hours of potentially fertilising intercourse)

=== Administration of Ulipristal acetate (30 mg) ===

* single dose
* taken orally.
* Its efficacy appears to be greater than 72 hours after fertilisation and extends up to 120 hours.

=== IUD insertion ===

* application possible up to 5 days after unprotected sex
