== Clinical follow-up ==
'''Consultation after 3 months then annually'''

- Checking:

* compliance
* tolerance
* the absence of any new personal or family event

Clinical breast examination

'''- Taking blood pressure''''

- Checking weight stability.

== Biological monitoring ==
- fasting measurement of blood sugar, total cholesterol and plasma triglycerides

- 3 months then ''every 5 years'' in the absence of new facts
