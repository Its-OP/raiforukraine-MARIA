=== '''Barrier methods''' ===
- Male condom (various sizes)

- Female condom,

- Diaphragm,

- Cervical cap (one size and/or different sizes to suit anatomy)

=== '''Natural methods''' ===
These methods consist of '''spotting ovulation''' to '''avoid having unprotected intercourse in the days around ovulation'''.

- Ogino (calendar method),

- Temperatures (temperature curve),

- Billings (observation of cervical mucus),

- Symptothermia (combination of methods)...
