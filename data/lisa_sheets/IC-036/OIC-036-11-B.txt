Complementary examinations recommended when initiating COP are :

=== - Metabolic panel (total cholesterol, triglycerides, fasting glycaemia) ===
o At 3 months if no family history of dyslipidemia or arterial accident

o Before the start if there is a family history of dyslipidaemia or arterial accidents

=== - Thrombophilia test ===
o if there is a family history of venous thromboembolism in a 1st degree relative before the age of 50,

=== - Cervico-vaginal smear ===
(every 3 years) after the age of 25 (as recommended)
