=== '''Hormonal contraception''' ===
Ideally started on the 1<sup>rst</sup> day of menstruation, a delay until the 5th day is acceptable.

After this period (quick start method), contraceptive efficacy is not immediate and intercourse must be protected for at least 7 days.

=== '''IUD contraception''' ===
Ideally inserted during the menstrual period

=== '''Emergency contraception''' ===
As soon as possible after high-risk intercourse (see dedicated section)
