== '''Contra-indications''' '''des COP''' ==
There are several contraindications to taking COP:

- '''carcinological''' (personal history of malignant tumours of the breast or uterus (endometrium)),

- metabolic disorders (dyslipidaemia, poorly controlled or complicated diabetes),

- vascular'' (''biological thrombophilia'', personal or family history of venous thromboembolic events, arterial cardio-vascular diseases [MI, ischaemic stroke, obliterative arteriopathy of the lower limbs], ''arterial hypertension'', migraine with aura, heavy smoking, age > 35-40 associated with other vascular risk factors)

- hepatic and biliary (history of lithiasis)

- hormonal (systemic hormone-dependent diseases, lupus for example).

== '''Contraindications''' '''of microprogestational contraception'''' ==
- Progressive liver disease,

- Breast cancer,

- Active venous thromboembolic events

- Unexplained bleeding

- Progesterone-dependent diseases (meningioma....)

== '''Contraindications''' '''of an IUD''' ==
- Pregnancy in progress and desired

- Ongoing purulent cervicitis or upper genital infection

- Puerperal infection in progress

- Immediately after a septic abortion

- Proven pelvic tuberculosis

- Unexplained vaginal bleeding

- Malignant gestational trophoblastic disease

-Any congenital (malformations) or acquired (including uterine myomas) anomaly of the uterine cavity resulting in deformation of the endometrial cavity incompatible with insertion of an IUD.

- Long-term immunosuppressive or corticosteroid treatment (copper-bearing IUD)

- Diseases at risk of endocarditis

- Wilson's disease (copper IUD)
