== Non-permanent contraception ==

==== Hormonal ====
o Combination of estrogen and progestin

o Progestin only

==== Non-hormonal ====
o Intrauterine device (IUD)

o Different barrier methods (male or female condom, diaphragm, cervical cap)

o All the other so-called "natural" methods (Ogino, temperatures, billings, symptothermy....) are not effective enough in normally fertile women.

== Permanent contraception
o Female (tubal ligation)

o Male (vasectomy)
