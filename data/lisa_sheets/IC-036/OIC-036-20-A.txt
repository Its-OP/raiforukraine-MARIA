In women: laparoscopic method using a tubal clip or ring, and/or tubal ligation section.

In men: bilateral vasectomy: ligation and surgical section of the vas deferens. Vasectomy is considered to be sterilisation and is considered irreversible.

Before the vasectomy, the patient must be informed that it is possible to cryopreserve his sperm at the CECOS.

After a vasectomy, the man's sperm may be fertile for 3 months. The patient must be informed of this. A spermogram at 3 months can confirm the absence of sperm in his semen.
