====1. Cardiovascular complications====

*functional impairment:
**sinus bradycardia, reduced contractile force
**heart failure and ventricular rhythm disorders (rarely);
*infiltration: pericardial effusion;
*coronary artery disease: hypothyroidism favours coronary atheroma; symptoms of coronary artery disease may become apparent when replacement therapy is instituted. [Acute coronary syndromes

====2. Neuromuscular and neuropsychological complications====

*depression, confusional syndrome or dementia, which are more common in the elderly
*proximal myopathy and sleep apnoea.

====3. RARE myxedematous coma ''':'''====

====<small>- rare (in cases of profound primary hypothyroidism: TSH > 50 mIU/l)</small>====

====<small>- a calm hypotonic and hypothermic coma</small>====

====<small>- prognosis is severe. [[Non-traumatic coma in adults and children]]</small>====
