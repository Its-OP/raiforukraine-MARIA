Thyroid hormone deficiency defines hypothyroidism.

Most hypothyroidism is caused by damage to the thyroid gland (peripheral hypothyroidism).

Central involvement due to hypothalamic-pituitary dysfunction (thyrotropic insufficiency) is much rarer.
