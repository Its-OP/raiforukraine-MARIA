|Title=Knowing the risk factors of hypothyroidism
|Description=None
|Rubric=Epidemiology
|Contributors=Eva Feigerlova,Igor Tauveron
|Order=8}}


*women (particularly after the age of 50)
*dietary iodine deficiency
*pregnancy
*personal or family history of thyroid disease or autoimmune disorders
*medications such as lithium, amiodarone and antithyroid drugs used to treat hyperthyroidism
*treatments with a thyroid tropism (radioactive iodine, thyroid or cervical surgery), cervical or cerebral radiotherapy, etc.
