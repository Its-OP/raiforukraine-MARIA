<u>Frank clinical signs of hypothyroidism are currently rare because TSH measurement, which is often carried out, enables screening at stages that are often asymptomatic or only mildly symptomatic.</u>

Clinical symptoms depend on the age and depth of the hypothyroidism.

*'''Signs of hypometabolism''' with asthenia [[Asthenia SD-021]], bradycardia, constipation [[Constipation SD-001]]<nowiki/>somnolence, hypothermia with chilliness, modest weight gain.

*'''More rarely:''' cutaneous and phanere involvement (paleness or carotidoderma, depilation), mucocutaneous myxedema (hands, face and ENT), muscular involvement (myalgia cramps) and endocrine repercussions (cycle disorders, libido disorders).
