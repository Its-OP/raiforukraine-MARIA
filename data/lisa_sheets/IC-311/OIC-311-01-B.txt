==Prevalence==

*Kidney cancer is the second most common urological cancer (after prostate and slightly ahead of bladder) and the 6th most common cancer of all sites combined.

*Incidence: 15,300 new cases annually in France, and rising
*Median age at diagnosis: 68 years
*In 2018: 67% men and 33% women (sex ratio: 2H/1F)
*Mortality: 5,600 deaths/year/France, declining in Western Europe including France and Scandinavia
