* A cardiovascular risk factor is a clinical or biological factor associated with an increased risk of developing cardiovascular disease, with a causal relationship between the factor and the disease. For a cardiovascular risk marker, no causal relationship between the marker and the disease has been demonstrated.

*'''Know the 4 major, independent, modifiable risk factors:''''
**HYPERTENSION
**Smoking,
**Diabetes,
**Hypercholesterolaemia
*Know the 3 major, independent, non-modifiable risk factors:'''
**Age,
**Male gender,
**Heredity: sudden death or myocardial infarction (MI) in a 1<sup>st</sup> degree male relative before the age of 55, or in a 1<sup>st</sup> degree female relative before the age of 65.
