First and foremost: preventing accidents in the home

Principles of treatment in the event of intoxication :

* Thorough questioning and clinical examination
* Call the poison control centre
* Immediate management of the life-threatening emergency according to the ABC algorithm
* Symptomatic management of the various malfunctions
* Specific management according to the toxic agent involved (CO, paracetamol, opioids, etc)
