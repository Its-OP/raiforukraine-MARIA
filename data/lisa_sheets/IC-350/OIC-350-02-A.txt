The main causes of acute fatty red leg are :

- in terms of prevalence: ''erysipelas'' with an incidence of 2/1000 per year in adults in the USA, ''lipodermatosclerosis'' due to venous insufficiency and eczema.

- in terms of urgency and severity: necrotising fasciitis.
