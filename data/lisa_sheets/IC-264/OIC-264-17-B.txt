In order to control proteinuria and reduce the decline in renal function, a protein restriction of less than 0.8 g/kg/day is recommended from stage 3A onwards. In order to avoid [[Denutrition in adults and children|denutrition]], this adaptation must be accompanied by a specialised assessment of dietary intake, particularly in the elderly.
