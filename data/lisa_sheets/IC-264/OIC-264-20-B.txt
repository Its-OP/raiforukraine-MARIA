Metabolic acidosis occurs during CKD due to a failure to eliminate the acid load.

It is usually moderate, except in the case of certain tubulopathies, with :

* Decrease in bicarbonates,
* slight increase in the anion gap,
* pH maintained until the advanced stage of CKD.
