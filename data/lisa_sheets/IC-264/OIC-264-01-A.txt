Chronic kidney disease (CKD) is defined by the existence of "chronic kidney disease for more than 3 months":

*chronic renal failure (CRF) defined as an irreversible reduction in glomerular filtration rate (GFR) to less than 60 ml/min/1.73 m2,
*and/or a morphological or histological renal abnormality with clinical repercussions,
*and/or a plasma or urine abnormality secondary to renal damage.
