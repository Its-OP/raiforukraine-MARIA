Clinical proteinuria is defined by one of these criteria:

* an albuminuria/creatininuria ratio > 300 mg/g or 30 mg/mmol
* a protein/creatinine ratio > 500 mg/g or 50 mg/mmol
* 24-hour proteinuria > 0.5 g.
