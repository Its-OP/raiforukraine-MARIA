Apart from the etiological treatment of CKD, interventions to slow the progression of chronic kidney disease include :

* strict control of blood pressure;
* reducing proteinuria
* prevention of acute renal failure and nephrotoxicity (iodine, drugs);
* moderate protein restriction adapted to the patient;
* control of diabetes, if present, and smoking cessation.
