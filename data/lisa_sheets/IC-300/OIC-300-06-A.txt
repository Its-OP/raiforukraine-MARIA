=='''During systematic screening'''==

==In the presence of clinical signs ==

===On routine examination: ===
red, irregular cervix, bleeding on contact

===Symptomatic patient:===
- '''[[Abnormal genital bleeding (excluding known pregnancy) SD-112|bleeding]]'''' caused by sexual intercourse: scanty, painless, even capricious or intermittent, they should raise the suspicion of invasive cancer until proven otherwise.

- Spontaneous ''[[Abnormal genital bleeding (excluding known pregnancy) SD-112|metrorrhagia]]''': in the absence of sexual intercourse, the symptomatology will be delayed and the increase in the lesion will eventually lead to spontaneous metrorrhagia.

- '''[[Leucorrhoea SD-104|leucorrhoea]]''': usually pinkish, it is a classic sign, but rarely the cause of the diagnosis.
