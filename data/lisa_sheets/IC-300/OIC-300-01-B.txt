'''In France,''' cervical cancer is in 2018 the '''12<sup>th</sup> most common cancer in women''' with :

- 2,920 new cases

- 1,117 deaths.

'''Worldwide''', cervical cancer is in 2018 the '''4<sup>th</sup> most common cancer in women''', with :

- 569,847 new cases, or 6.6% of all newly diagnosed cancers.

- In terms of mortality, it was responsible for 7.5% of cancer deaths worldwide, or 311,365 women a year.
