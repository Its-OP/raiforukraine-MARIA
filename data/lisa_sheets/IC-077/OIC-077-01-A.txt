Benzodiazepines and related drugs are agonists of GABA-A receptors. This action is at the origin of their acute effects, and in mirror image of withdrawal symptoms in the event of tolerance induced by prescription or prolonged use.

The therapeutic properties of benzodiazepines are :

           * anxiolytics,

           * sedative

           * hypnotic,

           * muscle relaxants,

           * anticonvulsants.
