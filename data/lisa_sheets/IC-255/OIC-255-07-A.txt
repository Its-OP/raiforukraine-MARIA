For the mother:

- Progression to type 2 diabetes, the risk of which increases with time and weight gain (risk increased by a factor of 7; 50% of glycaemic abnormalities and 10% of type 2 diabetes at 10 years).

- Appearance of metabolic syndrome

- Doubled risk of cardiovascular disease

- Risk of recurrence of gestational diabetes

For the child:

- Risk of being overweight during childhood and adolescence

- Increased risk of type 2 diabetes in adulthood
