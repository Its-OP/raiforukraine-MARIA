The diagnosis of a reaction following a hymenoptera sting is based on :

- history

o identification of the insect by the subject, whether or not there is still a stinger in place

o type of reaction and chronology of symptoms.

- skin tests: intradermal tests (IDR) (''no prick'')

- biological tests: venom-specific IgE assay
