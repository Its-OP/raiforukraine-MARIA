In addition to the ''eviction of the allergen'' and systemic treatment with 2nd generation anti-H1 medication, the treatment of allergic conjunctivitis includes a ''local treatment'':

- frequent eye washing and application of cold wet compresses.

- H1 antihistamines in eye drops.

- mast cell degranulation inhibitors in eye drops (sodium cromoglicate).
