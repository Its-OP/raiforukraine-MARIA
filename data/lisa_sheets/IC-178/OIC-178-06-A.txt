'''Use or threat of use of biological agents as a weapon'' with a view to inducing disease or death in humans, animals and/or plants.

'''Biological agent:''' natural infectious agent (bacterium, virus, parasite, fungus), toxin produced by an infectious agent, or genetically modified infectious agent.

The unusual presentation of an infection or the sudden appearance of clustered cases of severe infections may suggest a bioterrorist origin.
