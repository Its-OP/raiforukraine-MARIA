===''Factors associated with low back pain and neck pain''===

* Repeated trauma or microtrauma, prolonged postures in leisure or work '''[[Occupational risk prevention SDD-315|(occupational risk prevention,]] ''[[Identify the consequences of a pathology/situation on maintaining a job SDD-316|identify the consequences of a pathology/situation on maintaining a job)]]''''

* obesity (low back pain only)
* spinal static disorders ''[[Spinal deformity SDD-065|(spinal deformity)]]'''''
* smoking
* family history
