== '''Mortality''' ==

* 20% at 1 month and 40% at 1 year.
* Higher in the acute phase in intraparenchymal haemorrhage.

== '''Morbidity after stroke''' ==

* One third of survivors are dependent.
* One third of survivors retain after-effects but are independent.
* One third of survivors return to their previous state.

== '''Possible Complications''' ==

* Recurrence: 30% at 5 years.
* Cognitive problems (or vascular dementia).
* Post-stroke mood disorders (depression, anxiety).
* Spasticity
* Bladder and bowel problems.
* Neuropathic pain and complex regional syndrome.
* Vascular epilepsy

== '''Follow-up''' ==
The aims of post-stroke monitoring are as follows:

* Assess neurological sequelae and complications.
* Confirm the aetiological diagnosis.
* Ensure that secondary prevention treatment is prescribed, tolerated and effective.
* Assess the impact of vascular risk factors on the various target organs.
