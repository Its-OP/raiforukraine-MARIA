'''Clinical signs -'''' Inflammatory joint pain ([[joint pain SD-067]]; [[chronic pain SD-035]]; [[joint stiffness SD-056]]; [[joint deformity SD-070]]) '''with''' '''joint swelling''''. Preferential involvement of the wrists, metacarpophalangeal joints, interphalangeal joints of the fingers and metatarsophalangeal joints '''(Image 1).'''' Topography bilateral and globally symmetrical. Evolution > 6 weeks.
