'''Clinical signs -''' Search for extra-rheumatological manifestations that may point to other diseases accompanied by peripheral arthritis

'''Biological signs -''' Look for abnormalities in the haemogram, urine dipstick, transaminase assay or look for antinuclear antibodies (if ACPAs are negative), which may point to other diseases accompanied by peripheral arthritis.

'''Imaging signs -''' Search for radiographic abnormalities that may point to other diseases accompanied by peripheral arthritis
