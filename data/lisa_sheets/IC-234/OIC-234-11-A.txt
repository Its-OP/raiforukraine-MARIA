None of these additional tests should delay patient management.


- Blood tests: blood ionogram (Na+,K+), urea, creatinine, CBC, liver function tests

- Troponin, especially in cases of chest pain and ECG changes

- BNP or NT Pro BNP

- ECG

- Chest X-ray

- Arterial blood gas

- Cardiac ultrasound
