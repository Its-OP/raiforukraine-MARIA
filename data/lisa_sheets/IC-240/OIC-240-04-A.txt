'''Characteristics of hypoglycaemic coma:'''

* variable depth, up to very deep comas
* sudden onset
* often agitated
* profuse sweating
* signs of pyramidal irritation
* hypothermia
* rapid and complete disappearance of symptoms after re-sugaring with glucose
