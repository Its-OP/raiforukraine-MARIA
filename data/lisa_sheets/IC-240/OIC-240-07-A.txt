'''RESUCRAGE ==>will depend on the severity of the hypoglycaemia'''

*Moderate hypoglycaemia'': the patient is conscious ==> the patient can take 15g of sugar (3 lumps) per os.
*''Severe hypoglycaemia'': need for a third person
**Moderately severe: the patient is still conscious and can swallow ==> can be resugared per os by a third party with 15g of sugar (3 lumps).
**Very severe: the patient is no longer conscious, coma, convulsion ==>
***- IM or SC injection of glucagon, then oral resugaring
***- if glucagon not available: serum G30% IV 10 mL/20 kg weight, then relay with G10%.
