Each healthcare profession carries out an increasingly codified and regulated activity, with authorisation to practise based on obtaining a state-supervised qualifying diploma. Obtaining this diploma confers rights as well as responsibilities. Healthcare professionals are working together in increasingly organised ways, in hospitals and nursing homes in particular.
