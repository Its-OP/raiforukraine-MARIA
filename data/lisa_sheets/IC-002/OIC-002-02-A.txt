Medical practice is a technical and relational activity that uses specific knowledge and skills to meet the health needs of individuals and the population.

Ethics" refers to the reflection of someone who takes action and asks himself the question: "What is the best way to act in this situation? Ethics in medicine cannot be dissociated from technique.
