The following are listed as healthcare professionals in the French Public Health Code (in italics, the professions that have a professional order):

* Medical professions: doctors, dental surgeons and midwives;
* the pharmacy and medical physics professions: pharmacists and pharmacy assistants, medical physicists, etc;
* regulated medical auxiliaries: nurses, masseurs, physiotherapists, chiropodists, speech therapists, orthoptists, occupational therapists, psychomotor therapists, hearing aid acousticians, opticians, radiology technicians, medical laboratory technicians, prosthetists and orthotists, dieticians;
* care assistants, ambulance drivers and childcare assistants.

Psychologists, social workers, stretcher-bearers, mortuary attendants and hospital orderlies are also essential health professionals, even if they are not listed among the health professionals.
