If PA is isolated: psycho-education to recognise PA if it recurs, prevention of recurrence, dietary hygiene rules (less psychostimulants, regular sleep).

If PA is repeated, specific treatment for panic disorder (Item 66c).

If PA associated with a psychiatric disorder: treatment of the psychiatric disorder.
