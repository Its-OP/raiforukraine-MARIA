PA has a sudden onset, intense symptoms quickly, of limited duration (20-30 min), with gradual reduction until relief or post-crisis fatigue. PA is accompanied by variable symptoms:

- Physical : respiratory ([[Dyspnoea SD-162|dyspnoea]], choking, respiratory blockage, hyperventilation), cardiovascular ([[Tachycardia SD-166|tachycardia]], palpitations, chest weight), neurovegetative (sweats, tremor, pallor, dizziness, vertigo), digestive (pain, nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea), genitourinary (pollakiuria) or neurological ([[Tremor SD-128|tremor]], pseudo-paralysis). 

- Psychic: thoughts of impending disaster (fear of dying or going mad), intense fear without an object, impression of losing control, depersonalisation (strangeness of oneself, detachment from oneself/one's body, spectator of the scene), and derealisation (impression of unreal strangeness).

- Behavioural: variable behaviour, [[Agitation SD-114|agitation]], inhibition, stupor, [[Suicide and suicide attempts, suicidal ideation 2C-353-PE-A01|risk of self-harm]].
