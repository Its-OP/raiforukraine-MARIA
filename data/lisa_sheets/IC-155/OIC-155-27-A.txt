'''Impetigo:''' vesiculo-pustular skin lesions secondarily crusted due to a superficial non-follicular infection of the skin with ''Staphylococcus aureus'' and/or ''Streptococcus pyogenes''.

The term "impetiginisation" refers to the infection of a pre-existing dermatosis with Staphylococcus aureus and/or Streptococcus pyogenes.

Paediatric disease (peak prevalence between the ages of 0 and 10), predominantly in summer.
