'''Local signs'''

- Severe pain

- Collection

- crepitation, cyanotic livedoid areas, skin necrosis, hypoesthesia or anaesthesia

- trismus

'''General signs'''

Signs of sepsis: hypotension, tachycardia, confusion, polypnoea.
