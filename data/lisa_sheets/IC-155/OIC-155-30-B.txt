'''Localized or not very extensive impetigo:'''

Local antibiotic therapy with mupirocin: 2 to 3 times a day for 5 days.

'''Extensive or severe forms of impetigo:'''

Oral antibiotic therapy for 7 days, without waiting for microbiological results (children: amoxicillin/clavulanic acid or cefadroxil or, if allergic to penicillins, josamycin).

adult: pristinamycin or cefalexin)

'''Associated measures'''

Daily or twice-daily cleansing, with soap and water followed by careful rinsing. No application of local antiseptics, no local antibiotic therapy;

Eviction from the community: if lesions cannot be covered with a dressing: 3 days' eviction after the start of treatment.
