'''=> What Palliative Care (PC) is'''

*For whom?

- For patients and their families, whatever their age

*'''When?'''

- Serious, life-threatening illness (not just cancer patients)

- Should be started as early as possible (not just for terminally ill patients)

*'''By whom and where?'''

- By any healthcare professional,

- Team management,

- Wherever the patient is (hospital, home, etc.)

*'''What do we do and why?'''

- Active, ongoing care,

- For comprehensive care (physical, psychological, social, spiritual...) combining both prevention and relief of suffering,

- To improve the quality of life of patients and their families/carers.

- <u>Caution</u>: providing PS does not mean stopping investigations and treatments. PS can be combined with specific treatments for the underlying disease, which can prolong life.
