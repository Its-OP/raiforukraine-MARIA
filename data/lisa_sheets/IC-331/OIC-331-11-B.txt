Adrenaline is used in boluses of 1 milligram every 3 to 5 minutes during cardiopulmonary resuscitation.

Its introduction is recommended immediately in the event of an initial non-shockable rhythm.

Its use is recommended on a delayed basis in the event of an initial shockable rhythm, after the 3rd defibrillator analysis.

The use of amiodarone is recommended in the event of an initial shockable rhythm, after the 3rd defibrillator analysis.
