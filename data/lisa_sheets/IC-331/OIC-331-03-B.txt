- 40,000 sudden deaths per year in France

- 80% of coronary origin

- 50% of mortality from coronary heart disease

- Male > Female - Average age 60

- Survival in France 5% - in Europe 8%

- Paediatrics (A)

o Incidence 15 times lower than in adults

o Poor prognosis <5% at 1 year

o Etiology respiratory insufficiency +++ or circulatory insufficiency with non-shockable rhythm
